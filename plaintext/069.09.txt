69.9.1: men ða leofestan we gehyrdon oft sæcgan be þam æþelan tocyme ures drihtnes hu he on ðas weoruld þingian ongan 
69.9.2: þæt heahfæderas sædon and cyddon and witegan witegedan and heredan and sealmscopas sungan and sædon þæt se wolde cuman of þam cynestole and of þam rice hider on þas woruld and him eall þas cynericu on his anes æht eall geahnian 
69.9.3: eall þæt wæs gelæsted syððan heofenas tohlidan and seo heamiht on ðisne wang gestah 
69.9.4: and se halga gast wunade in þam æþelan innoðe and on þam betstan wæstme and on þam gecorenan hordfate 
69.9.5: and on ðam halgan breostum he eardode nigen monað 
69.9.6: ða ealra fæmnena cwen cende þone soðan scyppend and ealles folces frefriend and ealles middaneardes hælend and ealra gasta nerigend and ealra sawla helpend 
69.9.7: ða se goldbloma on ðas weoruld becom and menniscne lichaman onfeng æt sancta marian þære unwemman fæmnan 
69.9.8: ðurh ða byrþran we wæron gehælede and þurh þæt gebyrðor we wurdon alysede 
69.9.9: and þurh þa gesamnunge we wæron gefriðode feonda gafoles þurh þone tocyme we wæron geweorðade and gewelegade and gearade 
69.9.10: and syððan he drihten crist her on weorulde wunade mid mannum and hym fela wundra cydde and beforan worhte and hym liðelice hælo sealde and mildheortnesse tæhte 
69.9.11: heo wæron stænenre heortan and flintenre þæt heo þæt ongitan ne mihton þæt heo þær gehyrdon ne þæt na cweðan ne mihtan þæt hy þær gesawon 
69.9.12: ac þa se ælmihtiga god afyrsode hym þone unrihtan wrigels of heora heortum and heo onlihte mid leohtum andgite 
69.9.13: þæt heo þæt ongitan and oncnawan mihtan hwa him to hælo and to helpe and to fyrhnere on þas woruld astah 
69.9.14: and syððan he hym mildheortnesse earan ontynde and to geleafan gecyrde and his mildse onwreah and his mægensibbe gecydde 
69.9.15: ær þam we wæron steopcild gewordene forðan we wæron astypede þæs heofenlican rices 
69.9.16: and we wæron adilegode of þam frumsceapenan frumgewrite þe we to heofenum awritene wæron 
69.9.17: syndan we nu eft þider amearcode þurh þone soðan scyppend and þurh þone lifigendan god and þurh þone acennedan sunu urne drihten to þam gefean neorxnawanges 
69.9.18: ne gelette us þæs siðes se fecna feond ne us ne forwyrne þæs wilweges ne us þa gata ne betyne þe us opene ongean standað 
69.9.19: ne us þære byrig ne ofteo þurh his leasbregdas ne us þæs rices ne forwyrne þe we to gesceapene sindon ne us ne gedwelle þæs rihtan geleafan þe we to gelærede syndan 
69.9.20: and utan we þa drihtenlican wæra simble gehealdan and þa siblican lufan godes and manna 
69.9.21: ne syn we to gifre ne to frecene to firenlustgeorne ne to æfestige ne to inwitfulle ne to tælende ne to twigspræce ne morðor to begangenne ne aðas to swerianne ne niðas to fremmanne ne leasunga to sæcganne ne þeofenda to begangenne ne werignessa we ne fylian 
69.9.22: ne heafodlice leahtras ne lufian ne scyncræftas onhyrgen ne galdorsangas ne unriht lyblac onginnen ne to yðbelige ne syn ne to langsum yrre hæbben ne in oferhydo we ne scylon gewitan 
69.9.23: ac wið þas uncysta ealle we us scylan warnian ða þe gode laðoste syndon 
69.9.24: þæt we þurh þæt þone awergedan gast gefleman and gehynan þurh þa hean mihte ures drihtnes 
69.9.25: forðam we scylon beon ymbhydige and gemyndige sawle rædes and ure heortan rihtan and swutelian gode to willan and geþencan we þone dom þe we to gelaðode syndan and þone deman þe to ðam dome cymð 
69.9.26: he demð rihtne dom and emne dom 
69.9.27: ne bið þær hada andfengnes mid gode ne cyninga seara þrymmum gefrætewad butan þam anum þe ofer ealle rixað 
69.9.28: ne beoð þær forð borene sigele ne beagas ne heora heafodgold to ðam sigedeman 
69.9.29: ac on þam gemote standeð anra manna gehwylces sawl 
69.9.30: and bið forð lædende eall þa weorc þe heo ær oððe sið æfre gefremedan godes oððe yfeles 
69.9.31: gif þonne beoð maran þa misdæda and þæs godes to lyt þonne wynsumað se wiðerwearda feond and se awyrgeda gast on gesihðe þæs hean deman and þæs reðan cyninges 
69.9.32: and he bealdlice clypað to þam hean deman and þus cweð 
69.9.33: dem la dema dem rihtne dom and emne dom 
69.9.34: be þam þe þine bebodu forhogedan and þine æwe abræcon and besmitan mid synnum and bysmeredan mid gyltum 

69.9.35: gearelice witan þas heregas þreo þe mid þe wæron 
69.9.36: an is se heofonlica ðreat se ðe mid færeð and þe þenað oðer is þæt eorðlice mægn þe þu samnast and to dome cumen is þridde is þæt helcunde wered þe hider com to ðam þæt heo woldan þine domas gehyran and hu ðu þam forworhtum scrifan woldest 
69.9.37: eall þis mægn wat þe her to gemote com þæt þu eart eall anes þrymmes ful and mid soðfæstnesse and mid rihtwisnesse geseced 
69.9.38: dem la ðrihten dem rihtne domas and emne domas forlæt me mines wyrðe wesan þæs ðe ic me sylf begiten hæbbe 
69.9.39: þæt wæron mine þa ðe to ðe noldan 
69.9.40: rihtlic is þæt ic ða mid witum witnige þa þe þine hersumnesse forhogedon 
69.9.41: heo scirpton heo mid minum reafum na mid þam gewædum þe þu hete 
69.9.42: hy wæron ungemetfæste on eallum tidum heora lifes and oferhydo to fulle and mines willan to georne 
69.9.43: ðonne heo gehyrdon þine bec rædan and þin godspel sæcgan and heora lif rihtan and hym ecne weg tæcan and cyðan heo simble heora earan fordyttan and hit gehyran noldan 
69.9.44: ac þonne ic mine hearpan genam and mine strengas styrian ongan heo þæt lustlice gehyrdon and fram þe acerdan and to me urnan 
69.9.45: and ic heo mine leahtras lærde and heo me hyrdon georne 
69.9.46: ic heo to þeofendum and to geflitum stihte and to inwitfullum geþohtum þæt ic wolde þæt hy þe afremdedon 
69.9.47: hwæt woldan hy on minum hordcleofan and þine circean forgeaton 
69.9.48: and æt me leornedan sceandword and lease bregdas 
69.9.49: and þine soðfæstan lare forletan and þisne soðfæstan dom ne gemundon ac minre neawiste wilnedon and þine forhogedon 
69.9.50: dem la drihten ealra gesceafta reccend and styrend rihtne dom 
69.9.51: hwæt þu þe sylfne geeadmeddest for heora ealra lufan and for ure læððe 
69.9.52: þin feorh for heora on deað settest and þu þe sylf on rode astige þær ðu þin þæt halige blod on eorðan agite for heo and mid þinum æþelan swate gebohtest and mid þinum þam deorwurðan weorðe alysdest and gefreoðodest 
69.9.53: hy þæt lean eal forgeaton 
69.9.54: þa hy to me cyrdon næfre ic him are gefremede ne ænige helpe ne geuðe 
69.9.55: ac forlæt me hy on wita lædan and on susle cwylmian and þa mishernessa gewrecan þe hy wið þe forworhtan 
69.9.56: hwæt we nu gehyrdon hu bealdlice se deofol sprecð to þam hælende and þa syndæda stæleð on þa gastas 
69.9.57: hine þonne ofer eaxle besihð se soðfæsta dema and se rihtwisesta to þam forwyrhtum and to þam scyldigum and þus cweð worda grimmast 
69.9.58: non noui uos discedite a me maledicti qui operamini iniquitatem 
69.9.59: nelle ic eow habban on minre geferrædenne 
69.9.60: ac ge fram me gewitað wuldre bedælede freondum afyrede feondum betæhte in þam hatan wylme hellefyres 
69.9.61: þær ge awyrgedan scylon wite adreogan in þam hatestan hellebrogan and þær on witum a wunian butan ende 
69.9.62: ðonne bið drihtnes word reðe gehyred þam synfullum stefna strangas 
69.9.63: habbe we þonne men þa leofestan mycle nedþearfe þæt we ne syn mid þam scyldigum ac mid þam soðfæstan and mid þam gecorenum cristes herum 
69.9.64: we scylon mildheortnesse geearnian þæt we eft mildne deman gemetan and arfæstne 
69.9.65: wæron we þonne oft gemyngode to ures drihtnes hersumnesse þæt we sceoldan his willan wyrcan and his beboda healdan and rummode beon rihtra gestreona and þearfendum mannum arfulle and wudewena helpend and steopcilda arigend and earmra retend and wependra frefriend 
69.9.66: gif we þas weorc onginnað and gelæstað and gehealdað þonne beo we godes dyrlingas on heofonum 
69.9.67: nis urum hælende nan þing behyddes ne bedigledes þæs ðe men wyrcað on þysse worulde forðam þe his eage ofer ealle locað 
69.9.68: swa sanctus iacobus cristes discipul sæde hu se hælend spræc to sumum weligum men þe he hine hæfde mid wlencum and mid wiste gegodadne 
69.9.69: and he wæs swangor and swær and him wæs lað þearfendum mannum mete to syllenne 
69.9.70: and he wæs uncumliðe and him wæs uneðe þæt he for gode awiht dælde oððe þam sealde þe hit him eall forgeaf 
69.9.71: and se hælend hine þare leana eft gemyngode þa he cwæð 
69.9.72: ne gemundest þu na salamones cwide þe he cwæð 
69.9.73: ahyd þa ælmessan under þæs þearfan sceate se clypað to me and ic hine simble gehyre and mine mildse ofer þone sende 
69.9.74: efne hit bið gelic þæt man mid wætere þone weallendan welm ofgeote þæt he leng ne mot rixian swa man mid ælmessan synna ealle alyseð gif hy habbað geandet hyra scriftan heora misdæda and on bote befangen 
69.9.75: ðonne noldest þu weliga mine bebodu healdan 
69.9.76: ac sceolde se min þearfa aswæman æt þinre handa 
69.9.77: noldest þu geþencan hwa hit þe sealde 
69.9.78: þonne he clypode earmre stæmne þu wiðsoce þæt þu hine geherdest 
69.9.79: ac ic his geomerunge gehyrde and geseah hwæt þu him dydest mine þearfan þe mildheortnesse bædon 
69.9.80: and þu hy forhogedest and geunrettest and þinne andwlitan fram hym awendest and ne gemundest þu na hwæt se witega cwæð 
69.9.81: se þe his andwlitan fram þam þearfan wendeð þonne he hludast clypað god hine gehyreð þonne se man nele þone oðerne swa don 
69.9.82: se hælend cwæð to þam wlancan 
69.9.83: forhwi wære þu swa fæsthafol minra goda þe ic þe sealde 
69.9.84: to hwon receleasadest þu ðære gife þe ic ðe geaf 
69.9.85: ic þe nu afyrre fram minre selene þe ic þe forgeaf 
69.9.86: þonne bist þu wædla on woruldlife 
69.9.87: forhwon noldest þu geþencan þæt ic wille forgeldan æghwylcum men ane gode dæde þe for minum naman man gedeð 
69.9.88: mid hundteontigum ic hit him forgylde swa hit is on minum godspelle gecweden and gesæd 
69.9.89: swa oft swa ge doð anum of minum þam læstum ge hit simle me syllað 
69.9.90: and ic eow wið þam gesylle ecne dream on heofenum 
69.9.91: þu man to hwan eart þu me swa ungeþancfull minra gifena 
69.9.92: hwæt ic ðe gesceop and geliffæste and æghwæt þæs ðe þu hafast ic ðe sealde 
69.9.93: min is eall þæt þu hæfst and þin nis nanwiht ic hit eall afyrre fram þe 
69.9.94: þu leofa butan me gif þu mæge 
69.9.95: þe ic hit sealde to þan þæt þu hit sceoldest þearfum dælan 
69.9.96: ic swerige þurh me sylfne þæt ic eom se ilca god þe þone weligan and þone heanan geworhte mid minum handum þæt ic wolde þæt þu mine þearfan feddest þonne hy wæron þe biddende minra goda 
69.9.97: and þu him symble tiðe forwyrndest 
69.9.98: forhwon noldest þu hit geþencan gif þu him mildheortnesse on gecyddest þæt þu ne sceoldest þæs nan þing forleosan þe þu him dydest ne me on ðære sylene abelgan mines agenes 
69.9.99: to hwon anum agnodest þu þe þæt ic inc bam sealde to hwon feddest þu þe ænne of þæm þe ic inc bam gesceop to welan and to wiste and to feorhnere 
69.9.100: to hwon heolde þu hit þe anum and þinum bearnum þæt mihte manegum genihtsumian on 
69.9.101: unyðe þe wæs þæt þu eall ne mihtest gefæstnian ne mid inseglum beclysan 
69.9.102: wenst þu þæt hit þin sy þæt seo eorðe forðbringeð heo þe groweð and bloweð and sæd lædeð and andlifan bringeð 
69.9.103: ic nu afyrre minne fultum fram þe 
69.9.104: hafa þu æt þinum gewinne þæt þu mæge and æt þinum geswince 
69.9.105: ic ðe ætbrede mine hrenas þæt heo þinre eorðan ne hrinað 
69.9.106: ic afyrre fram þe mine mildheortnesse and þonne bið sona gecyþed þin yrmðe and ætywed 
69.9.107: gif þu wene þæt hit þin bocland sy and on agene æht geseald hit þonne wæron mine wæter þa þe on heofenum wæron þonne ic mine gife eorðwarum dælde 
69.9.108: gif þu mihte hæbbe dæl renas ofer þine eorðan 
69.9.109: gif þu strang sy syle wæstmas þinre eorðan 
69.9.110: ic ahyrde mine sunnan and heo gebyrhteð 
69.9.111: þonne forbærneð heo ealle þine æceras 
69.9.112: and þonne bist þu dælleas mines renes and þe þonne bið þin eorðe idel and unnyt goda gehwylces 
69.9.113: mine þearfan lifiað be me 
69.9.114: gif þu mæge wuna butan me 
69.9.115: mine þearfan me ealne weg habbað and ic hy næfre ne forlæte 
69.9.116: mine þearfan hy me lufiað and hy hycgað ymb heora drihten 
69.9.117: and hine gelome nemnað and lufiað and him ege to witan and habbað swa men sculon to heora hlaforde 
69.9.118: ðonne þu weliga ne þu þinne drihten ne lufadest ne þu are æt him hæfst ne þu earming ne most lifian aht lange 
69.9.119: hwæt wendest þu wlanca gif þu me sealdest owiht þines þæt þe þonne wære þin woruldgestreon eall gelytlad 
69.9.120: hwæt þu to lyt hogedest ymb þone ende þines lifes 
69.9.121: to dol þu wære and to gedyrstig þa þu wendest þæt þinra feohgestreona ende ne gewurde 
69.9.122: ic wat hwæðere þæt þin lif geendað þonne þu his ne wenest 
69.9.123: ðu weliga to hwon treowodest þu on þinne welan and on oferflownessa þinra goda and na on me þe hit þe eall forgeaf þæt þu on wære 

69.9.124: he ða drihten crist cwæð to þam weligan men 
69.9.125: eala þu dysiga and þu gedweleda to hwan getreowdest þu on þine speda and on þine æhta 
69.9.126: þin sawl bið afyrred fram þe in þisse ilcan nihte and be minre hæse of þinum lichaman gelæded 
69.9.127: ac hwa fehð þonne to þan ðe þu lange samnodest 
69.9.128: oððe hwam gearwadest þu þin bold and þine getimbru nu þine erfeweardas lifian ne motan forþon þu me noldest þanc witan minra goda 
69.9.129: sceoldan þa word beon ealle cuðlice gelæste and gefyllede þe se hælend sylf cwæð 
69.9.130: hine þa þone weligan man þære ilcan nihte deað on becom and on his bearn ealle 
69.9.131: fengan þa to his gestreonum fremde and laðe 
69.9.132: quid nos possumus fratres de hoc cognoscere et ignoscere 

69.9.133: hwæt we nu magon men þa leofestan be þysum ongitan and oncnawan þæt se ælmihtiga god nele þæt him man his gifena þanc nyte 
69.9.134: ne þurfan we na þæs wenan þæt he us nolde þæra leana eft gemanian þe he us to gode forgeaf her on worulde 
69.9.135: efne swa he us mærlicor gifeð swa we him mærlicor þancian scylon swa þrymlicre ar swa mare eadmodnes 
69.9.136: ðam þe drihten micel syleð mycles he hine eft manað 
69.9.137: þam þe he micel toforlæteð mycel he eft to þam seceð 
69.9.138: æghwylc heah ar her on worulde bið mid frecnessum geseald 
69.9.139: efne swa þa woruldgeþincða beoð maran swa ða frecnessa beoð swiðran 
69.9.140: swa we magon be þam þa bysne oncnawan and ongitan 
69.9.141: þæt treow þonne þe wexeð on þam wudubearwe þæt hit hlifað up ofer eall þa oðre treowu and brædeð hit þonne semninga storm bestandeð and se stranga wind þonne bið hit swiðlicor geweged and geswencged þonne se oðer wudu 
69.9.142: swa bið eac gelice be þam heaclifum and torrum þonne hi hlifiað feor up ofer þa oðre eorðan 
69.9.143: hy þonne semninga feallan onginneð and full þearlice hreosan to eorðan 
69.9.144: swylce eac be ðam hean muntum and dunum þa ðe heah standað ofer ealne middaneard 
69.9.145: þa hwæðere wite habbað þæs ealdordomes þæt heo beoð genehhe mid heofenfyre geþreade and geþræste and mid ligum geslægene 
69.9.146: swa ða hean mihta her on worulde hreosað and feallað and to lore weorðað 
69.9.147: þisse worulde welan weorðað to sorge and þas eorðlican wunder weorðað to nahte 
69.9.148: ðeah we þissa worulde wlenca tilian swiðe and in wuldre scinan swiðe 
69.9.149: þeah we us gescirpen mid þy readestan godwebbe and gefrætwian mid þy beorhtestan golde and mid þam deorwurðum gimmum uton ymbehon hwæðere sceal on eorðan ende gebidan 
69.9.150: ðeah þe ða mihtegestan and þa ricestan hatan him reste gewyrcan of marmanstane and mid goldfrætwum and mid gimcynnum eal astæned and mid seolfrenum ruwum and beddum eall oferwrigen and deorwyrðum wyrtgemengnessum eal gestyred and mid goldleafum gestrewed ymbutan hwæðere se bitera deað þæt todæleð eal 
69.9.151: þonne bið se glencg agoten and se þrym tobroden and þa gimmas toglidene and þæt gold tosceacen and þa lichaman tohrorene and to duste gewordene 
69.9.152: forðam nis naht þyses middaneardes wlite ac he is tweogendlic þysse worulde wela 
69.9.153: he is hwilendlic and feallendlic and gebrosnadlic and tobrocenlic he is wyrslic and yfellic and forwordenlic swa þa rican syndan her on worulde 
69.9.154: hwær syndon nu þa rican caseras and þa cyningas þe io wæron 
69.9.155: oððe ealdormen þe beboda setton 
69.9.156: hwær is demera domstow 
69.9.157: hwær is heora ofermedla butan mid moldan beðeaht and in witum gewrecen 
69.9.158: wa is worulde scriftum butan heo mid rihte domas reccan 
69.9.159: ne bið þam leornerum nahte þe sel þonne þam lærendum butan heo mid rihte domas secan 
69.9.160: hwær com middaneardes gestreon 
69.9.161: hwær com worulde wela hwær com foldan fægernes 
69.9.162: hwær coman þa þe geornlicost æhta tiledan and oðrum eft yrfe læfdon 
69.9.163: swylc is seo oferlufu eorþan gestreona 
69.9.164: efne bið gelice rena scurum þonne hy of heofenum swiðost hreosað and eft hraðe eall toglideð bið fæger weder and beorht sunne 
69.9.165: swa tealte beoð eorðan dreamas and swa wace syndan æhta mid mannum 
69.9.166: and swa todæleð lic and sawle 
69.9.167: þonne bið ful uncuð hu se dema ymbe þæt don gewile 
69.9.168: forðan nis us nanwiht betere ne selre þonne we lufian urne drihten mid eallum mægne and mode and of eallum ingehydum 
69.9.169: swa hit awriten is 
69.9.170: se þe mine lufan on him hæfð and his bene to me sendeð ic hine symble gehyre and mine milddse ofer þone læte 
69.9.171: and þa þe to me gecyrrað fram heora gyltum and hy geandettað on minum naman heora scriftan mid fæstenum and mid teara gyftum and mid gebedum ðonne ic hym toforlæte mine mildse and hym forgifnesse sylle and min rice alyfe and heofonlicne weg tæce 
69.9.172: þær bið eal god and seo hea blis and seo micele med 
69.9.173: for þissum eorðlicum wisum ic sylle þa heofonlican for þissum lænan life þæt unlæne for þissum hwilwendlicum þa ecan for þyssum ungecorenum þæt gecorene and for þyssum earman rice ic sylle þæt eadige 
69.9.174: gesælige beoð þa ðe þam fyliað 
69.9.175: ne helpeð þam men ænig wiht þeah þe he ealne þysne middaneard on his agene æht gestryne gif deofol nimð þa sawle 
69.9.176: ne him na þe bet ne bið þeah þe he her on life libbe þusend wintra gif he æfter his deaðe bið gelæded in helle and þær in witum wunað syððan awa 

69.9.177: utan we us þonne nu wendan to þam beteran and cyrran to þam selran þonne motan we geseon soðne drihten and his gefean to fæder rice 
69.9.178: þær is frætwednesse þæs æþelestan æþelinges 
69.9.179: and þær is cyninges þrym gesene and þær is arwyrðnes witena 
69.9.180: ðær is gesene þegnes ioguðe and þær is ar and fægernes werum and wifum and geþoftscipe engla and heahengla and geferræden apostola and heahfædera and witegena 
69.9.181: and þære eadigan ceastre weras gefeoð and wynsumiað on lisse and on blisse and on rice and on ecum gefean 
69.9.182: þær is sang and wynsang and godes lof and þæs hehstan cyninges herenes and seo beorhtnes þæra haligra 
69.9.183: and þa soðfæstan scinað swa sunne and þa men rixiað swa englas on heofenum 
69.9.184: we wæron þider gehatene and gelaðede to ðam halgan ham and to ðam cynelican friðstole 
69.9.185: þær drihten crist wunað and rixað mid eallum halgum sawlum aa butan ende in ealra worulda woruld amen
