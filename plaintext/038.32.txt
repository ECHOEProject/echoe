in die sancto pasce 

38.32.1: men ða leofestan her segð on ðisum bocum hwæthwega be þysum halgan easterdæge þe we nu todæg wurðiað and mærsiað swa gecweden is on þisum 
38.32.2: unarimedum wundrum under his anwealde 
38.32.3: and sealde him ealle eorðan mid nytenum and sæ mid fixum and mid hire gecyndum 
38.32.4: and sealde him sunnan and monan on his þeowdome 
38.32.5: and þæt he mihte libban buton geswince and buton deaðe wunian 
38.32.6: and anra gehwilc on tælmete mihte wunian and libban swilce he þrittig wintre wære 
38.32.7: and symble habban leoht buton þeostrum and dæg buton nihte 
38.32.8: and efne swa þa tida on hærfæst mid wæstmum and mid blostmum beoð gefyllede swa him wæron ealle god gesealde 
38.32.9: oð ða æt nextan he forheold godes bebodu swa him drihten ne bebead þurh þæs deofles lare 
38.32.10: ac þurh unwisdom he gesyngode beforan gode 
38.32.11: and þa æfter þam nænige hreowsunge he ne dyde þæs gyltes 
38.32.12: swa hit is awriten on halgum bocum 
38.32.13: se ðe synne deð and þa ne beteð se bið þære synne þeow oððæt he hi eft gebeteð gode to willan 
38.32.14: swa þonne men ða leofestan æfter þam þeowdome drihten god wæs ariende and helpende menniscum cynne 
38.32.15: and swa swa on ðam sixtan dæge adam gesceapen wæs 
38.32.16: and he on þam sixtan dæge he gesyngode æfter þan 
38.32.17: and on þam sixtan dæge cain acwealde abel his broðor 
38.32.18: and on þam sixtan dæge sodoma burh and gomorra godes englas bærndon for godes yrre mid fyres lige 
38.32.19: and on þam sixtan dæge iacobes suna hi endleofone becipton heora twelftan broðor ioseph ægiptum on þeowdom wið þrittigum scillingum 
38.32.20: and on þam sixtan dæge se unlæda iudas becypte urne hælend 
38.32.21: and on þam sixtan dæge petrus se apostol wiðsoc urum drihtne þriwa and eftsona he gelyfde 
38.32.22: and on ðam sixtan dæge ure drihten wæs on rode ahangen 
38.32.23: and on þam sixtan dæge þeostru wæron gewordene ofer eorðan fram ðære sixtan tide oð ða nigoðan tide 
38.32.24: and stanas wurdon tobrocene and eorðbyrgenna wurdon opene on gewitnysse ures drihtnes þæs þe nu todæg of deaðe aras on þisne drihtenlican easterdæg 
38.32.25: þæt is þonne wurðunge dæg and gebletsod dæg swa we ær beforan bodedon and sædon be þam mænigfealdum tacnum þe on ðam dæge getacnode wæron 
38.32.26: ærest æt fruman he geworhte heofonas and eorðan sunnan and monan 
38.32.27: and ealle gesceafta and þa seofon heofonas mid nigon engla endebyrdnyssum 
38.32.28: þone seofoþan heofon he geworhte him sylfum on to sittenne 
38.32.29: þæt is þonne þære halgan þrynnysse heofon 
38.32.30: on þam sit se ælmihtiga god se ðe þone man of eorðan slime geworhte 
38.32.31: and eorðan gesette mid hire wæstmum and wriðas mid treowum 
38.32.32: and ealle þa wæstmbærenda gesceafta palmtreow and ealle blowende wyrta 
38.32.33: on þam drihtenlican dæge ealle þa wurc drihten gefylde 
38.32.34: and hine þa gereste on þone seofoðan dæg 
38.32.35: and he hine þa gehalgode eallum mannum to restedæge 
38.32.36: and on þone drihtenlican dæge ure drihten wæs gefullod on iordanes streame 
38.32.37: and on ðone drihtenlican dæge he genam þone fruman þæs fæstenes þe he gefæste feowertig daga and nihta ær eastron 
38.32.38: and on ðone drihtenlican dæg iohannes se godspellere geseah fægere gesihþe on þam iglande þe hatte pathmos 
38.32.39: and þurh þæt he gesette þa halgan boc apocalipsis 
38.32.40: and eac he geseah þa feower godspelleras ures drihtnes on his ymbhwyrfte 
38.32.41: and he geseah þa seofon candelstafas and þæt hiw mannes bearnes 
38.32.42: þæt is þone god sylfa 
38.32.43: his loccas wæron swa hwite swa swiðe hwit wull 
38.32.44: and his ansyn wæs swa fæger swa sunne 
38.32.45: and his eagan wæron swilce se reada lig þæs fyres 
38.32.46: and his stemn wæs swilce manegra wætera geþunn 
38.32.47: and on þone drihtenlican dæg he geseah micel gesihþe on heofonum 
38.32.48: and þa fæmnan sancta marian seo wæs beorhtre þonne sunne 
38.32.49: and on hire heafde heo hæfde þone coronan þæra twelf steorrena 
38.32.50: and on ðone drihtenlican dæg iohannes geseah metan þa burh þa heofonlican hierusalem mid gyldenre rode 
38.32.51: and þa wæron on ðære byrig twelf gatu of ðam xii gimstanum geworhte þurh synderlice þing 
38.32.52: and seo strætlanu is on ðære byrig of clænum golde geworht 
38.32.53: seo leng and seo widnys and seo hehþo syndon gelice twelf mila ælces þinges 
38.32.54: and on þone drihtenlican dæg hine ætywde se hælend þam halgan apostole sancte petre be þære sæ þe tiberiad is haten 
38.32.55: and on þone drihtenlican dæg þære eastrena ure drihten aras of deaðe 
38.32.56: and þurh þa rode his þrowunge þæs ecan lifes ærest he gecydde 
38.32.57: se ðe nu todæg us generede of adames gylte 
38.32.58: and nu todæg crist eode on helware and þa isenan forescyttelsas he þær tobræc and towearp 
38.32.59: and leuiathan þæra deofla ealdorman he geband 
38.32.60: and iudam þe hine belæwde on þam dimman þrosme þære sweartan dypan he besencte 
38.32.61: and he þa genam adam and euan and mid him up alædde mid heora tudre and teame 

38.32.62: nu todæg men ða leofestan wæs gefylled se cwyde þæs halgan witegan isaias 
38.32.63: þam mannum on þam lande eardigendum under þæs deaðes sceade micel leoht nu todæg him wæs up cumen 
38.32.64: and þæt folc þe þa on helle on þystrum sæt micel leoht hi þa gesawon 
38.32.65: be þyses dæges leohtes micelnysse nu todæg wæron wundrigende þa hiwscipas on helle 
38.32.66: forðan hi næfre ær ne gesawon swa fæger leoht swa hi þa gesawon 
38.32.67: and hi þa swa cwædon 
38.32.68: hwæt la is þes wuldres cyning buton hit si se ælmihtiga god 
38.32.69: and eac hi swa cwædon geseoð ge þæt we geseoð urne ealdorman leuiathan 
38.32.70: se ðe ær wæs wealdend ofer us he is nu gebunden mid isenum racenteagum and his þegnas mid him 
38.32.71: swa þonne men ða leofestan helware wæron be drihtnes tocyme se ðe þone ærestan man generede 
38.32.72: swa cwæð se halga apostol sanctus paulus 
38.32.73: se æresta man wæs of eorðan eorðlice gesceapen þæt þonne wæs adam 
38.32.74: se æftera man wæs of heofonum heofonlic þæt is crist sylfa se ðe of heofonum astah on eorðan 
38.32.75: and hine geeadmedde menniscum hiwe to onfonne 
38.32.76: and þurh his þrowunge eall mancyn alysde 
38.32.77: and swa manna gehwilcum gedafenat to arisanne of his gyltum 
38.32.78: eac swilce we sceolon andettan ure synna beforan urum scriftum 
38.32.79: and þa læcedomas urum sawlum we sceolon geornlice secan ægðer ge on worde ge on weorce 
38.32.80: þæt we magon orsorglice gan to ures drihtnes weofde 
38.32.81: and þær cristes lichaman and his þæt gebletsode blod niman and þicgan 
38.32.82: swa he sylfa cwæð 
38.32.83: se man se ðe þigeð minne lichaman and drincð min þæt halige blod se wunað on me and ic beo on him 
38.32.84: he halgode hlaf ær his þrowunge and todælde his discipulum and þus cwæð 
38.32.85: etað þisne hlaf hit is min lichama and doð þis on minum gemynde 
38.32.86: eft he bletsode win on anum calice and cwæð 
38.32.87: drincað ealle of þisum þis is min blod þæt bið for manegum agoten on synna forgyfennysse 
38.32.88: eft syððan þa apostolas dydon swa swa crist het þæt hi halgodon hlaf to husle and win on his gemynde 
38.32.89: eac swilce heora æftergengan ealle bisceopas and sacerdas be cristes hæse halgiað hlaf and win to husle on his naman mid þære apostolican bletsunge 
38.32.90: soðlice se hlaf and þæt win þe beoð þurh heora mæssan gehalgode 
38.32.91: oðer þing hi ætywað menniscum andgytum wiðutan and oðre þing hi clipiað wiðinnan geleaffullum modum 
38.32.92: wiðutan hi beoð gesewene hlaf and win ægðer ge on hiwe ge on swæcce 
38.32.93: ac hi beoð soðlice æfter þære halgunge cristes lichama and his blod þurh gastlice gerynu 
38.32.94: hæðen cild þe man fullað ne bryt hyt na his hiw wiðutan ac hit bið swaðeah wiðinnan awend and aþwogen on þam fante fram eallum synnum 
38.32.95: swa is eac þæt halige fantwæter oðrum wæterum on hiwe gelic 
38.32.96: ac þurh þæs sacerdes bletsunge genealæcð þæs halgan gastes miht and hit syððan aþwyhþ þa sawle fram eallum synnum þurh gastlice mihte 
38.32.97: we rædað on ðære bec þe is gehaten uita patrum þæt twegen munecas bædon æt gode sume swutelunge be þam halgan husle 
38.32.98: and þa æfter þære bene hi gestodon him mæssan þa gesawon hi licgan on ðam weofde an cild and godes engel stod mid anum sexe anbidigende oððæt se preost þæt husel tobræc 
38.32.99: þa toliþode se engel þæt cild on þam disce and his blod into ðam calice geat 
38.32.100: and þa hi to þam husle eodon þa wearð hit awend sona to hlafe and to wine 
38.32.101: and hi hit þigdon gode þancigende þære geswutelunge 
38.32.102: eac we rædað þæt sanctus gregorius abæd æt criste þæt he ætywde anum tweonigendan wife micle geswutelunge be þam halgan husle 
38.32.103: heo eode to husle tweonigendum mode 
38.32.104: þa abæd se halga gregorius æt gode þærrihte þæt him bam wearð ætywed seo snæd þæs husles þe heo þicgan sceolde swilce þær læge on þam disce anes fingres lið eal blodig 
38.32.105: and þæs wifes twynung wearð sona gerihtlæced 
38.32.106: forðan þonne men ða leofestan micel neadþearf us is þæt we us geornlice clænsien and gemedemien ær þam tocyme þæs halgan husles þurh hreowsunge and þurh ælmyssan and þurh fæsten 
38.32.107: and ne agymeleasien we ða teoðan sceattas to syllanne for godes lufan 
38.32.108: swa se apostol cwæð 
38.32.109: efne swa þæt wæter fyr adwæsct swa deð eac seo ælmysse þa synne 
38.32.110: þy us þonne gedafenað men þa leofestan þæt we ondrædon þa andweardnysse godes and his engla 
38.32.111: and hu he andswarigende byð þam arleasum gastum and þus cwæþende 
38.32.112: gað fram me ge awyrgde on þæt ece fyr on hellewite and on þone ecan ungefean 
38.32.113: þa syndon deoflum gegearwode and eallum heora geferum 
38.32.114: þær is toða gristbitung and þa singalan þystru 
38.32.115: and he þonne cwyð ure drihten to ðam arleasum 
38.32.116: ic wæs hungrig and ne sealdon ge me mete 
38.32.117: and ic wæs þurstig and ne sealdon ge me drincan 
38.32.118: and ic wæs nacod þa ne wruge ge me mid eowrum hræglum 
38.32.119: þonne cweðað þa unrihtwisan men to him 
38.32.120: hwænne gesawon we þe drihten god swa þearfiendne 
38.32.121: þonne andswarað him ure drihten and ðus cwyð 
38.32.122: þonne ge hit ne dydon minum þam earmestum þearfum þe ic eow to sende þonne ne dydon ge hit me 
38.32.123: soð ic eow secge ne can ic eow 

38.32.124: men ða leofæstan we us sculun ondrædan þysne andweardan cwyde þe drihten cwyð þonne he demende byð lifigendum and forðgewitenum 
38.32.125: and efne swa he nu todæg of deaðe aras swa he becymð on ða ilcan easterniht swa hit on þam godspelle awriten is 
38.32.126: drihten cymð swa se þeof cymð þonne he stelan wile 
38.32.127: swa ungecyþed he byð æghwilcum men ge se dæg ge seo niht swa him byð þæs þeofes cyme 
38.32.128: be þysum dæge þonne wæs micel word gecweden 
38.32.129: wella þu micela dæg and þu egeslice niht on þam beoð astyrede eall heofona mægen 
38.32.130: and be þæs dæges þrymme and his ege is awriten and gecweden þæt se an dæg byð swa lang geþuht swa þusend wintra 
38.32.131: men ða leofestan on þam dæge drihten demð þrym hiwscipum 
38.32.132: þæt is heofonware and eorðware and helware 
38.32.133: and on ðam dæge ealle deade arisað 
38.32.134: and æfter þam hi beoð standende and andettenne heora dæda beforan þam hehstan and þam æðelestan cristes heahsetle 
38.32.135: þær manna gehwilc his dæda forð sceal beran swa gode swa yfele swa hwæðer swa he ær dyde 
38.32.136: and þonne æfter þan ure drihten god gelaðað and gecigð ealle his gedefan and gecorenan on þæt ece lif and on þa ecan gefean and þus him to clypað 
38.32.137: uenite benedicti patris mei percipite regnum quod uobis paratum est ab origine mundi 
38.32.138: cumað ge gebletsode and onfoð mines fæder rice þæt eow fram þyses middaneardes fruman gegearwod wæs 
38.32.139: and þonne þa halgan mid englum and mid heahfæderum and mid þam halgan apostolum and mid eallum halgum wuniað on ðære ecan and on ðære langsuman sibbe and blisse þæs singalan dreames 
38.32.140: he þonne drihten wile þæt ealle men hale and gesunde sin 
38.32.141: and he nele þæt ænig forweorðe 
38.32.142: ac ealle he wile gelædan and gelaþian on þæs ecan lifes gefean þæra þe her willað rihtlice and clænlice æfter boca bebodum libban 
38.32.143: þær he nu mid þam clænum and þam rihtwisum leofað and rixað mid fæder and mid suna on annysse haliges gastes a on ecnysse buton ælcum ende amen
