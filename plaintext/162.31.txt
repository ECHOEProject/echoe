162.31.562: ða gewat zosimus to his mynstre god wuldrigende and bletsigende and mid lofum herigende 
162.31.563: sona swa he to þam mynstre becom þa rehte he heom eallum of frymðe þa wisan 
162.31.564: and naht ne bediglode ealra þæra þinga þe he geseah oððe gehyrde þæt hi ealle godes mærða wurðodon and mærsodon and þære eadigan forðfore dæg 
162.31.565: iohannes soðlice ongeat sume þa mynsterwisan to gerihtanne swa swa seo halige ær foresæde 
162.31.566: ac he þa sona gode fultumigendum gerihte 
162.31.567: zosimus on þam mynstre wæs drohtnigende an hund wintra and þa to drihtne hleorde 
162.31.568: wuldor sy urum drihtne hælendum criste þe leofað and rixað a on worulda woruld amen
