215.4.1: hit sagð þæt ða geforewritu cyðað be þara haligra martira lyfe þæt heora behatu wæron trumme to þam sygefestan criste 
215.4.2: and heora gewinn and campdom hyg welwyllendlice geendodon 
215.4.3: and timbrunge þæs fullan geleafan hyg on him sylfum fæste geheoldon 
215.4.4: and þone deaðes wæg myd ealle forhogedon 
215.4.5: and þæt æce rice myd fulfræmednisse him sylfum geearnodon 
215.4.6: amang þam wæs ðe eadiga and se halga quintinus gewilnigende þæt he þam heofonlican criste hys gewinnu and hys haligan drohtnunge
