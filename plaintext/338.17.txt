338.17.1: leofa man ðe is mycel þearf þæt ðu þas drihtenlican tide georne geþence 
338.17.2: þonne þu arisan wylle bide þinne drihten eadmodlice forgifennesse ealra þara synna þe þu wið godes willan geworht hæbbe 
338.17.3: þara þe þu gemunan mæge and eac þara þe ðu gemunan ne mæge 
338.17.4: and lufa þinne drihten mid inneweardum mode and mid inneweardum mægene 
338.17.5: and bide hine georne þæt ðu mote on þisse halgan tide hwæthwyge gewyrcan þæs þe his milda willa sy and ðinre sawle þearf sy 
338.17.6: oððe on setle oððe on stealle 
338.17.7: oððe on gange oððe on æriste 
338.17.8: oððe on gesihðe oððe on gehyrnysse oððe on suman wege 
338.17.9: þæt god ælmihtig þin mod geteo and getihte to rihte and to ðinre sawle ðearfe 

338.17.10: and ðenc þæt god ælmihtig on þisse halgan tide swyðe mycel adreah and aræfnode and geðrowode for mancynnes lufan and hælo þæt he wolde us of ðam ecan witan alysan 
338.17.11: and he sylf ða gesette þas feowertig daga and nihta togædere 
338.17.12: and he sylf hi gesette butan æghwylcan eorðlican bigleofan 
338.17.13: and he wæs geset ofer þæs temples gehwing 
338.17.14: and he wæs gecostnod þurh þone ealdan feond mancynnes gewinnan 
338.17.15: and he þær ðone deofol oferswiðde 
338.17.16: and geðreatode þæt ungetreowe folc 
338.17.17: and ða unlædan iudeas hine ascunian ongunnon and his lare hyrwan 
338.17.18: and him heora þegna þreat to gelæddon 
338.17.19: and hine gebundon and hine to heora demum and to heora ealdormannum gelæddon 
338.17.20: and hi hine syððan to deaðe forræddon 
338.17.21: and hine on rode ahengon and gefæstnodon 
338.17.22: and hi þær his hand blod ut aleton 
338.17.23: and hine mid readbasuwan hrægle gegeredon 
338.17.24: and him ðyrnenne cynehelm worhtan 
338.17.25: and hi him þærtoeacan fela bysmæra gebudon and gespræcon 
338.17.26: and heo hine mid bradre handa under þæt wange slogan 
338.17.27: and heo him fullice on þæt neb ræhton 
338.17.28: and him huxword spræcon and on bysmor cwædon þæt he iudea cyning wære 
338.17.29: soð hi þæs sædon þeah hi his na wendon 
338.17.30: he is and a wæs and a bið ealra cyninga cyning 
338.17.31: and he mihte eaðe him gebeorgan æt ðam deaþe gyf he swa wolde 
338.17.32: ac he wolde þæt we clæne þurh hine wurdon 
338.17.33: and he wolde þa and gyt wile þæt we onfon rihtne geleafan 
338.17.34: forðon þe he us of deofles hæftnydnysse alysde 

338.17.35: and þonne is ðe leofa man mycel nydþearf þæt ðu on ælcne timan wið deofles lare wær sy 
338.17.36: and þæt þu þas drihtenlican tide georne geþence mid þinre cyricsocne and mid þinum gebedum 
338.17.37: and mid þinum fæstene and mid þinre ælmessylene 
338.17.38: and mid þinum paternostrae oft and gelome 
338.17.39: and beorh ðe georne wið ealle unnytlice þing and wið ealle idelnessa 
338.17.40: and geearna ðe to gode ælmihtigum þæt ðu to him cuman mote and mid him wununge habban and mid eallum his halgum on ealra worulda woruld a butan ende amen
