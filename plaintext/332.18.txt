332.18.1: forlæte ælc cristenman stala and leasunge morðdæda and mane aþas wyccecræftas and wiglunga and þa myclan druncennesse þe manslihtas of cumað and þa fracodan forligra 
332.18.2: and utan habban þa soðan lufa to gode and to mannum 
332.18.3: and ic bidde eow for godes mihtiges lufe and for his ege ðæt ge eower lif rihtlice libban and eowerne cristendom swiðe well healden 
332.18.4: and beoð earmra manna retent and miltiend 
332.18.5: and beo ælc laford milde his earman mannum and þa men ægþer ge freoh ge geþeowe 
332.18.6: beon hi hyra laforde swa holde swa hi willon þæt god ælmihtig beo him æt þam gemænon dome on domesdæg 
332.18.7: geþenceð hu yfle gelamp iudas scariot þe swicode ymbe crist 
332.18.8: and we biddeð ælcne cristene man þurh god sylfne and þuruh þa myclan lare þe he us on his bocum gewissað þæt næfre hine nan man to nanum deofulgilde ne gebidde 
332.18.9: þæt is to nanum deadum stane ne to nanum dumbum treowe 
332.18.10: ne nanum wille ne to nanum þillicum scandlicum biggencge 
332.18.11: and oferhogian þone soðan hælend þe ealle þing mæg gehelen 
332.18.12: ic wolde æt summum gescadwisum men þe þis gehyrað geaxian hwæt se deade stan mihte þam me gehelpan þe him neod byð and ne mæg forþon hine sylfne of þare stowe awylton 
332.18.13: þeahhwæðre se ðe swa earmlice beswicen byð þurh deoful and geornful he mæg þonne deoful þyder bespanan him sylfum æfre ecelice to genyþrunge and to forwyrde þa wifmen þe berað hyra cild to wegelætum and teoð þurh þa eorþan 
332.18.14: ne begytað hi næfre godes miltsa butan hi geswicon and hit æfre beton 
332.18.15: forþon witodlice hi ofriað hi sylfe deoflon and hyra bearn þe þis doð 

332.18.16: uton forðig beþencon us sylfe and biddan georne godes mildheortnesse 
332.18.17: and uton biddan þa mæran cwene sancta marian þæt heo beo us milde and arful þingere to þam heofonlican cyninge þæt we his mildse habban 
332.18.18: drihten us to ðam gefultumie þe leofað and ricsað a in anre godcundnysse fæder and þe halige gast buton ælcum ende amen
