xuii kalendas augusti passio in anglice de sancta margareta christo uirgo 

177C.a.1: post christi passionem et resurrectionem et ascensionem eius ad patrem 
177C.a.2: æfter æriste ures drihtenes hælendes cristes and his wuldorfæstan upastigenesse on heofonas to þam ælmihtigan gode on his naman andetnysse maniga martyros wæron þrowiende 
177C.a.3: and his apostoli geliffæste 
177C.a.4: nu gebroðra mine ge gehyrdon be þære eadigan margaretan þrowunge hu heo oferswiðe ealra deofla mægen 
177C.a.5: gelyfað on hi and on god ælmihtine 
177C.a.6: and doþ gemynd þare halgan fæmnan sancta margaretan and sancta marian 
177C.a.7: and on heora ðanc ælmessan syllað 
177C.a.8: þæt hi eow onfon on gesihþe hælendes cristes 
177C.a.9: him is lof and wuldor and weorþmynt 
177C.a.10: and a he lyfaþ mid englum and mid heahfæderum 
177C.a.11: and mid witegum and mid apostolum 
177C.a.12: and mid haligum fæmnum in ealra worulda woruld a buton ende amen
