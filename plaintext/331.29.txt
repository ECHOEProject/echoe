331.29.1: gyf hit geweorðe þæt on þeodscype becume healic ungelimp for manna gewyrhtan here oððon hunger bryne oððon blodgyte unwæstm oððon unweder orfcwealm oþðon mancwealm þurh færlice uncoða þonne sece man þa bote aa to gode sylfum 
331.29.2: ealra þinga ærest ga man to scrifte and gebuge man to rihte and unriht forlæte huru heafodmen and gehadode men 
331.29.3: and ðonne do man syððan swa swa dyde dauid and eac mænig oðer geomerigendum mode clypige man to criste 
331.29.4: and ealswa niniuete gyf man þæt geræde gold and glencga alecge man þa hwile and ælce wigwæpna and idele renca and æghwylce woruldsaca læte man stille and nime man hæran oððon wyllen to lice 
331.29.5: and fæste swyðe georne and ælmessan dæle 
331.29.6: god is swyðe milde 
331.29.7: niniuete wæron forsyngode swyðe 
331.29.8: ac hy dydan swa heom þearf wæs gewendan to rihte fram eallum unrihte and fengan on fæsten 
331.29.9: and ðingedan georne geomerigendum mode and þæt mæg to bysne 
331.29.10: utan don eac swa swa oft swa þæs neod sy 
331.29.11: beode man sona þreora daga fæsten and hwilum læs hwilum ma be þam þe seo neod sy 
331.29.12: and sece gehwa his scrift swyðe georne and unscodum fotum georne godes cyrican dæges and nihtes 
331.29.13: and ga man mid reliquium ut and mid letanian 
331.29.14: and sceote man ælmessan be ðam þe man þonne to þearfe geræde 
331.29.15: swa æt heafde peninc swa æt heorðe peninc swa æt sulhgange peninc swa æt faldgange pening swa gesyfledne hlaf gyf man þæt geræde æt hreocendum heorðe swa elles hwæt ðinga be ðam þe witan þonne to ðearfe gerædan 
331.29.16: hwilum weaxgescot hwilum flexgescot hwilum mealtgescot hwilum melagescot hwilum ælmesbæð and hwilum þearfena fotþweal and elles ælmesgedal and hwilum be teoþunge hwilum be mannes efenwihte hwilum be freotmen hwilum be healffreon and hwilum an hwilum oðer witan scylan rædan folce to þearfe 

331.29.17: and sacerda gehwylc do swa hit þearf is on his mæssesangum sona swyðe georne clypige to criste 
331.29.18: to æghwylcre neode man hæfð on cyricbocum mæssan gesette 
331.29.19: and tilige man georne mid þam and mid halgum gebedum aa þonne þearf sy 
331.29.20: and þæt is hwene betere þonne man to wiccan and to wigleran tilunge sece æt ænigre neode 
331.29.21: and ealle godes þeowas don swa hit neod is mid sealmsangum þingian georne for ealle þas þeode 
331.29.22: and æt ælcum tidsange aþenedum limum sumne sealm singan preces and collecta and geornlice biddan are and miltse ealre þysse þeode 
331.29.23: and godes þearfan myngie man gelome þæt hy eac to criste clypian swyðe georne and anrædlice gebiddan for ða þe heom on godes est ælmessan syllan 
331.29.24: and for ealle þas þeode þingian hy georne 
331.29.25: and ælces mannes þeowetlingas ealle þa ðry dagas þe þæt fæsten aboden sy weorces beon gefreode wið cyricsocne and wið þam þe hy þæt fæsten þe lustlicor gefæsten and wyrcan heom sylfum þæt þæt hy willan and clypian to criste eac swyðe georne 
331.29.26: and gyf hi þonne þæt fæsten rihtlice ne gefæstan þolian þære hyde oððon hydgyldas 
331.29.27: and gyf frigman swa fræc sy þæt he þæt fæsten abrece oðþon his ælmessan rihtlice ne gelæste swa swa hit gelagod sy gebete þæt swa scire witan sylfe geceosan oððon þus gyf hit beon mæge bonda mid þryttiga penigan þegen mid xxx scillingan gyf man swa geræde 
331.29.28: and swa hwar swa þæt feoh up arise dæle man on godes est æghwylcne penig 
331.29.29: and manna gehwylc dæle on godes est georne godes þearfum þæne sylfan mete þe he wolde brucan gyf him þæt fæsten swa geboden nære 
331.29.30: and huru is mæst þearf þæt man fram unrihte gebuge to rihte and yfeles geswice and godes lage healde and godcundre lare gyme swyðe georne and rihtlice gelæste eall þæt man behate on godes est to donne 
331.29.31: god us gehealde
