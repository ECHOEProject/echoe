de sancto iohannes 

48.67.1: sanctus iohannes geseah ofer garsegc swilce hit land wære 
48.67.2: þa genam hine se engel and gebrohte hine to paradisum 
48.67.3: þæt is neorxnawange 
48.67.4: paradisum nis naðor ne on heofonum ne on eorþan 
48.67.5: feowærtig fædma heah wæs noes flod ofer ða hehstan dune þe on worulde is 
48.67.6: seo dun is on bocleden gehaten armenia 
48.67.7: nu is paradisum feowærtig fædma hehgra þonne noes flod wæs 
48.67.8: paradisus hangað betwynon heofonan and eorðan wundorlice swa hit se ealwældend gesceop 
48.67.9: paradisus is eall efenlang and efenbrad 
48.67.10: nis ðær naðor ne dæl ne dune 
48.67.11: ne þær ne bið snaw ne forst ne hagol ne ren 
48.67.12: ac þær is fons uite 
48.67.13: þæt is lifæs wylle 
48.67.14: ðonne kalendas ianuarii on tun gæð þonne flowæð seo welle swa fægere and swa smoltlice swa hunig and na deopre þonne mon mæg wætan his finger forwyrdne 
48.67.15: ofer þæt land eall hio floweð ælce monað ana siðe þonne se monaþ in cymeð ianuarii 
48.67.16: and þær is se fægere wuduholt þe is on bocum gehaten radiansaltus 
48.67.17: þær is ælc treow swa riht swa bolt and swa heah þætte næfre on eorðan nan man geseon meahte 
48.67.18: ne eac hwilces cynnes hio syndon 
48.67.19: ne fealð þær næfre leaf of 
48.67.20: ac a he bið singrene wlitig and wynsum welena unrim 
48.67.21: paradisus is uprihte on eastewearde ðysse worulde 
48.67.22: nis þær ne mete ne hungor 
48.67.23: ne þær niht næfre ne cymeð ac a simble dæg 
48.67.24: sunne þær scineð seofan siðe beorhtlicor ðone her deð 
48.67.25: ðær wuniaþ on godes ænglas unrim mid þam halgum saulum oþ domæsdæg 
48.67.26: ðær wunað on an fæger fugol fenix haten 
48.67.27: he is mycel and mære swa se ælmihtiga hine sceop 
48.67.28: he is hlaford ofer eall fugelcynn 
48.67.29: ælcere wucan æne siþe se fægre fugol hine baþað in þam lifæs wylle 
48.67.30: and þonne fleogeð se fugel and asett hine on þæt heagoste treow þe is on paradisum ongean þa hatan sunnan 
48.67.31: þonne scinæð he swa sunnan leoman 
48.67.32: and glitenað swa swylce he sio eall gylden 
48.67.33: his fiðera syndon ænglas feðerum gelice 
48.67.34: his breost and his bile beorht syndon fægere and fage æghwilces cynnes 
48.67.35: hwæt his eagan eþelice sendon swa clæne swa cristallo and swa scire swa sunnan leoma 
48.67.36: his fet syndon blodreade begen twegen and se bile hwit 
48.67.37: hwæt se fægera fugol fleogeþ of his earde se þe is fægerlice fenix haten 
48.67.38: witodlice wunað he on egypta land fiftene wucan fæste ætgedere 
48.67.39: ðonne cumaþ to him swylce he cyning wære fægniaþ and folgiaþ eall fugolcynn 
48.67.40: hwæt þæt fugolcynn eall fægere fenix gretaþ 
48.67.41: writigað and singaþ onbutan him 
48.67.42: ælc on his wisan ænne heriaþ 
48.67.43: ðonne færð þæt folc feorran 
48.67.44: swiþe wafiaþ and wundriaþ wellcumiað fenix 
48.67.45: hal wes þu fenix fugela fægerest feorran hider cumen 
48.67.46: ðu glitenast swa read gold ealra fugela cyning fenix gehaten 
48.67.47: ðonne wercað hio of weaxe writiað fenix 
48.67.48: metað fenix and hine mærlice þær wordum heriað fugela fægerest fenix haten 
48.67.49: ðonne fagniaþ þær fugelas ealle fægere and fage 
48.67.50: fela ætgedere feallað to fotum fenix gretað 
48.67.51: his stæfn is swa beorht swa byme 
48.67.52: and his sweora swilce smæte gold 
48.67.53: and his forebreost fægre gehiwod swylce marmorstan mærost cynnes 
48.67.54: and him an ræd hiow rudaþ on þam ricge 
48.67.55: goldfylle gelic glitonaþ fenix 
48.67.56: hwæt þes fugol ferde fægere to his lande ymbe fiftene wucan 
48.67.57: fugelas manige eall embuton efne ferdon ufene and neoþone and on ælce healfe 
48.67.58: ða hio becomon þær heora cyning sceolde fenix se fægere fugol gehaten faraþ forþ into paradisum 
48.67.59: þæt is under heofonan hama hegost 
48.67.60: ða wunede þær on paradisum fenix se fægere fugel eall halig 
48.67.61: and þæt fugelcynn eall ferdon heom hamweard ælc to his earde aredon fenix 
48.67.62: nu sagað her sanctus iohannes soðum wordum wislice and wærlice swa se wertacen þæt vi þusend her on worolde crist forestihte 
48.67.63: ðone æfre embe an þusend geara farað fenix se fægra fugelwlitig and wundorlic 
48.67.64: þynceð him þæt he forealdod sy 
48.67.65: and gaderað þonne ofer eall paradysum togædere ealle þa deorwurðan bogas and macaþ mycelne heap togædere 
48.67.66: and þurh godes mihte se hate sunne scineþ and þurh þara sunnan hatnesse and hire lioman se heap wyrðeþ onæled þe he se halga fugol fenix gewroht hafað 
48.67.67: and he feallað þonne onmiddan þæt fyr and wyrðaþ forbærned eall to duste 
48.67.68: ðonne on þone þriddan dæg ariseð se fægere fugol fenix of deaðe and bið edgung 
48.67.69: and farað to þam lifes wylle and baþað hine þærinne 
48.67.70: and him wexan onginnað þa feþera swa fægere swa hio æfre fægereste wæron 
48.67.71: and þus a emb þusend wintra he hine forbærneþ and eft forfæger ediung up ariseþ 
48.67.72: and nafaþ he nenne gemacan 
48.67.73: ne nan man ne wat hweþer hit is þe carlfugol þe cwenfugol buton crist sylf 
48.67.74: he is þas halgan fugeles fenix gehaten 
48.67.75: wlitig and wynsum swa hine eallwealdend gesceop 
48.67.76: and þus dreogan sceal drihtnes willan 
48.67.77: nu is us andgyt forgyfen mancenne 
48.67.78: fugelas heriaþ crist 
48.67.79: nu gedafenað us þæt we herian urne drihten mid ælmæssan and mid halgum gebedum and mid eallum þingum þæt witon þæt gode leof is 
48.67.80: and gescildan us wiþ ða eahta heahsynna þæt we þa ne fremman 
48.67.81: þæt is morþor and stala mane aþes and unrihtgitsunge and unrihthæmedu and gifernesse leasunga and attorcræftas dyrneligera and twispæce and ofermodignæss 
48.67.82: beorgan we us wiþ ealle þas heahsynna 
48.67.83: and lufian urne drihten mid eallum mægene and mid eallum mode 
48.67.84: se god þe leofað and rixaþ se ðe on heofonum is heah and halig ealra cyninga cyning crist us generie þæt we on wynne wunian moton 
48.67.85: se þe leofað and rixað soðlice mid fæder and sunu and mid þam halgan gaste a buton ænde amen
