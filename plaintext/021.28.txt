feria tertia in letania maiore 

21.28.1: se eadiga apostol iacobus us manode on þam pistole þe us man her beforan rædde hu we ure sawla hæle begytan magon gif we geornlice þam bebodum gehyrsumiað þe he us ealle gemænelice to manað 
21.28.2: he cwæð se healica apostol 
21.28.3: andettað eowere synna eow betweonan and gebidde eower ælc for oðerne to þi þæt ge gehealdene beon 
21.28.4: ne cwæð se apostol forði þas word þe god ælmihtig ure andettednysse beþurfe forðam þe him synd ealle þa þing on andwyrdnesse þe we geþencað oþþe sprecað oþþe doð 
21.28.5: ac forðam þe we ne magon elles gehealdene beon buton we ure synna ðe we dæghwamlice on befeallað mid geomerunge andettan and hi mid geswicennysse and goddra weorca bigenge geornlice adwæscan 
21.28.6: se þe hine sylfne wregð and his gyltas her on life begeomerað næfð se deofol on domesdæg þær eallum mannum gedemed beon sceall ænig edwit ongean to weorpenne 
21.28.7: ac þam ðe his synna her nele oþþe forhogað to andettene he sceal beforan heofenlican mægenðrymme and eorðlican ealra hyra edwitt grimlice gehyran and ece biternessa onfon mid deofle and his gesiðum 
21.28.8: nis us nan þing selre on þysum life þonne we behreowsian ure synna and hi mid ælmessan alysan her on life þæt we ne þurfon eft þa ecan wita ðrowian and þolian 
21.28.9: nis nan mann on eorðan swa mære ne swa mihtig þæt he ne scule deaþes abyrgean 
21.28.10: and mid ure sawle anre we sculon riht agyldan on domesdæg 
21.28.11: wa bið hire þonne earmre gif heo ana stent ealra godra dæda bedæled on domesdæge beforan gode and beforan eallum his halgum 
21.28.12: ðær beoð þonne on us sylfum gesyne and opene ealle þa gyltas þe we æfre gefremedon oþþe dydon on þisum life ongean godes willan 
21.28.13: buton hi beon her on worulde ær gebette us eallum to ðearfe 
21.28.14: forðan þe þær ne mæg nan mann oþran gehelpan ne se fæder þam sunu ne se sunu þam fæder ne nan mann þær ne mæg oþrum gehelpan 
21.28.15: ac anra gehwylcum menn þær bið gedemed æfter his agenum gewyrhtum 
21.28.16: ac uton andettan ure synna þa hwile þe we magon and moton 
21.28.17: þonne gebeorge we us sylfum wið þæt ece wite and geearniað us heofena rice 
21.28.18: god us ealle gestrangie and getrymme to ure agenre þearfe and us gehealde swa his willa sy 
21.28.19: god is ure ealra fæder and he þæt gecydde þa ða he asende his agen bearn hider crist of heofenum and her wearð mann geboren þurh þæt clæne mæden sancta marian þe næfre nahte weres gemanan 
21.28.20: and he for eall mancynn deaþ þrowode and us ealle þa ðurh his deað alysde of ðam ecan deaþe and us ealle gestrynde to þam ecan life 
21.28.21: ðurh clæne mæden crist wearð geboren and þurh clæne fulluht we syndon ealle cristene gewordene þæt we sceolon eac on ealre clænnysse healdon gif we ænige miltse begytan sculon æt gode 
21.28.22: crist ðrowode deaþ for us synleas 
21.28.23: þonne moton we be gewyrhtum fela for urum synnum þrowian and ðolian and æt gode geearnian þæt he us þurh his micclan mildheortnysse huru wið ecne deað ahredde and us on þam toweardan life reste geunne 
21.28.24: uton us habban symle gemyndige þone timan þe us toweard is 
21.28.25: þonne se earma lichama and seo werige saul hi totwæmað and todælaþ 
21.28.26: þonne us forlætað ealle ure woruldfrynd 
21.28.27: ne magon hi us þonne ænigum gode 
21.28.28: ac bið æt gode anum gelang eall hwæt we gefaran sculon 
21.28.29: geþence gehwa him sylf hu sceort and hu earmlic þis læne lif is 
21.28.30: ac ne þence we na fram dæge to dæge þæt we to gode ne gecyrran 
21.28.31: forðan þe we us nyton witodlice lif oð æfen 
21.28.32: ne we nyton þonne we to ure reste gað hwæðer we moton eft dæges abidan 
21.28.33: hit is sorhlic and earmlic eallum cristenum mannum to gehyrenne eall þæt man us foresegð embe cristes þrowunge and ymbe his hidercyme and ymbe hellewites brogan 
21.28.34: þæt we syndon swa heardheorte þæt we ne magon ongytan þa godcundan lare þe us man lærð and laþað to urum drihtene fram deofles anwealde 
21.28.35: us is eallum swiðe micel þearf þæt we understandan þæt hit to þam dome nu geornlice genealæcð þe drihten sylf tocymð 
21.28.36: and wile þonne æt us witan hu we him geleanod habban eall þæt he for us ðrowode and ðolode on þyssum life 

21.28.37: we habbað oncnawen fela þæra foretacna þe crist sylf foresæde þæt her cuman sceolde on þysne middaneard and eac he sæde for micclum egesan þe gyt toweard is 
21.28.38: he sæde þæt æfter þisum fæce gyt geweorðan sceall swa egeslic tima swa næfre ær ne wearð syððan þeos woruld gewearð 
21.28.39: uton don forði swa us micel þearf is beon geornfulle ure agenre ðearfe geswican ura synna and swærra gylta and forbugan ælc unriht and gebugan georne to rihte 
21.28.40: þonne geearnige we us ece blisse on heofenan rice mid drihtene sylfum 
21.28.41: ne sceal næfre nanum cristenum menn to langsum þincan þæt he his agene þearfe gehyre seggan 
21.28.42: soðlice us eallum is gemæne neod and healic þearf þæt we georne cyricsocna began 
21.28.43: and us þær synna forgyfennysse and eces lifes gesælþa and eac þyses lifes frema biddan 
21.28.44: ne mæg ure nan him nane mare ðearfe began þonne he þyder ga þær he mage his drihten him gegladian and forgyfennysse and ealra halgena geþingrædene begytan 
21.28.45: we sceolan swyðe inlice on cyrcan to gode clypian þæt he us arie and wið deofol bewerige and to ecere myrhþe gebringe 
21.28.46: hit is swiðe unþæslic and pleolic þæt we on godes huse idele spellunga and hlacerunga began 
21.28.47: forði þe hit cymð us to mycelan hearme buton we ær geswican ure plega 
21.28.48: and ure hleahter byð eall mid wope and wanunge geendod þonne we heonon gewitan sculon 
21.28.49: to uran forðsiðe cumað godes englas and ure sawla fram ure lichaman totwæmað 
21.28.50: and hi to drihtenes þrymsetle gelædaþ þæt we ðær underfon swa we ær geearnodon 
21.28.51: þonne beoð ure sawla gemyndige ure ærran misdæda 
21.28.52: and swiðe forhtigað and bifiað for þan micclan ege and gryre þe us þonne getencge byð unþances 
21.28.53: and earhlice of lichaman gewendað and underfoð æt gode ecne dom swa swa hi ær geearnodon 
21.28.54: forði sceal ælc mann hine sylfne georne to gode geþeodan on ælce wisan 
21.28.55: ge on gebedum ge on fæstenum ge on ælmesdædum 
21.28.56: þy gehwa wat þæt god ælmihtig ne forlæt nænne þæra þe to him ðencð on hihte and on mode and on worde and on weorce 
21.28.57: and þam ðe his lare filiað and his bebodum he forgyfð þæt ylce rice þe he him sylf on wunað 
21.28.58: and him þæs nan ende ne becymð 
21.28.59: and we eac geornlice dæghwamlice biddan æt gode þæt he us gescylde and bewerige wið ðone ungesewenlican mancynnes feond 
21.28.60: forðam þe ure strengð ne mæg him wiðstandan butan godes fultume 
21.28.61: and uton forði geornlice æfre cunnian hu we magon his leasunga and his swicdomas and his lare mid halgum gebedum ofercuman 
21.28.62: and huru þæt ælc cristenmann georne him wiðstande 
21.28.63: forðam þe him is æfre swa leofre swa he ma manna forpære mid his lotwrencum to forwyrde 
21.28.64: and he us ne mæg nan oþer rice forgyfan buton þæt ylce þæt he him sylf on wunað 
21.28.65: þæt synd þa ecan tintregan on hellewite þe næfre ne ateoriað ne ne ablinnað on ecnysse 
21.28.66: ac uton æfre ge on gelimpe ge on ungelimpe geornlice to gode clypian 
21.28.67: and his helpes biddan and geþencan hu hit is gerædd on bocum 
21.28.68: se ðe æfre þurhwunað on anrædum geleafan se byð gehealden 
21.28.69: nu byð forðig micel ræd þam ðe his sylfes recð þæt he him gebicge þæt ece lif þe we ymbe sprecað 
21.28.70: nu þængst ðu and cwyst hu mæg ic þæt ece lif gebiggan 
21.28.71: ic þe segge syle þe sylfne gode swa þæt þu lufige þa þing þe god lufað 
21.28.72: and þa þing ascunige þe god ascunað 
21.28.73: and kepe gehwa þæt he his lif on unnyt ne aspende 
21.28.74: ac leornige godes beboda æt wisum lareowum 
21.28.75: and þa healde swa he selost mage 
21.28.76: god bebyt þæt þu lufige þynne nyhstan swa swa þe sylfne 
21.28.77: eft he cwyð gyf ge forgyfað þam mannum þe wið eow agyltað þonne forgyfð eow eower fæder eowre synna 
21.28.78: gyf ge þonne nellað forgyfan nele eac eower fæder eow forgyfan eowre gyltas 
21.28.79: ge gehyrað nu mine gebroðra þæt hit stent þurh godes gyfe on urum agenum dihte hu us byð æt gode gedemed 
21.28.80: he cwæð gyf ge forgyfað eow byð forgyfen 
21.28.81: ne bepæce nan mann hine sylfne 
21.28.82: witodlice gyf hwa furðon ænne mann hatað on þysum middanearde swa hwæt swa he to gode gedeþ eall he hit forlyst 
21.28.83: forðan þe se apostol paulus ne byð geligenod þe cwæð 
21.28.84: þeah ðe ic aspende ealle mine æhta on þearfena bigleofan and þeah þe ic minne agene lichaman to cwale gesylle swa þæt ic forbyrne on martyrdome gyf ic nabbe þa soðan lufe ne fremað hit me nan þing 
21.28.85: be þam ylcan cwæð se godspellere iohannes 
21.28.86: se ðe his broðor ne lufað he wunað on deaþe 
21.28.87: eft he cwæð 
21.28.88: ælc þæra ðe his broðer hatað is manslaga 
21.28.89: ealle we synd gebroðra þe on god gelyfað 
21.28.90: and we ealle cweðaþ pater noster qui es in celis 
21.28.91: ðæt is þu ure fæder þe eart on heofenum 
21.28.92: ne gedyrstlæce nan mann be mægðhade buton soðre lufe 
21.28.93: ne truwige nan mann be ælmesdædum oþþe on gebedum butan þære foresædan lufe 
21.28.94: forðan þe swa lange swa he hylt þone sweartan nyð on his heortan ne mæg he mid nanum þinge þone mildheortan god gegladian 
21.28.95: ac gyf he wille þæt him god milde sy þonne hlyste he godes rædes na of minum muðe ac of cristes sylfes 
21.28.96: he cwæð gyf ðu offrast þine lac to godes weofode and þu ðær gemyndig byst þæt þin broðer hæfð sum þing ongean ðe 
21.28.97: forlæt þærrihte þa lac ætforan þam weofode and gang ærest to þinum breðer and þe to him gesibbsuma and þonne þu eft cymst to þam weofode geoffra þonne þine lac 
21.28.98: gyf ðu þinum cristenum breðer deredest þonne hæfð he sum ðing ongean þe and þu scealt be godes tæcunge hine gegladian ær þu þine lac geoffrige 
21.28.99: gyf þonne se cristena mann þe þin broðor is þe ahwar geyfelode þæt þu scealt miltsigende forgyfan 
21.28.100: ure gastlican lac synd ure gebeda and lofsang and huselhalgung and gehwylce oðre lac þe we gode offriað 
21.28.101: þa we sceolon mid gesibbsumere heortan and broþorlicre lufe gode betæcan 
21.28.102: nu cwyð sum mann ongean þa rædinge ne mæg ic minne feond lufian þone þe ic dæghwamlice wælhreowne me togeanes geseo 
21.28.103: eala þu mann ðu sceawast hwæt þin broðor þe dyde and þu ne sceawast hwæt ðu gode gedydest þonne ðu micelan swærran synna wið god gefremedest 
21.28.104: hwi nelt ðu forgyfan þa lytlan gyltas anum menn þæt se ælmihtiga god þe þa micclan synna forgyfe 
21.28.105: nu cwyst þu eft micel gedeorf bið me þæt ic minne feond lufige and for ðone gebidde þe me hearmes cepð 
21.28.106: ne wiðcweðe we þæt hit mycel gedeorf ne sy ac gyf hit is hefigtyme on þyssere worulde hit becymð to mycelre mede on þære toweardan 
21.28.107: witodlice þurh þines feondes lufe þu byst godes freond 
21.28.108: and na þæt an þæt þu his freond sy ac eac swylce ðu bist godes bearn þurh þa rædene þæt þu ðinne feond lufige 
21.28.109: swa swa crist sylf cwæð 
21.28.110: lufiað eowre fynd doð þam tela þe eow hatiað 
21.28.111: þæt ge beon eowres fæder cild se ðe on heofenum is 
21.28.112: þeah se mann habbe fullne geleafan and ælmyssan wyrce and fela to gode gedo eall him byð idel swa hwæt swa he deþ buton he habbe soðe lufe to gode and to eallum cristenum mannum 
21.28.113: seo soðe lufu is þæt gehwa his freond lufige on gode and his feond for gode 
21.28.114: mænigfealde earfoðnyssa and hospas wolde gehwa eaðelice forberan wið ðan þæt he moste sumum rican men to bearne geteald beon and his yrfenuma to gewitendlicum æhtum 
21.28.115: forberað nu geþyldelice for ðam ecan wurðmynte þæt ge godes bearn getealde beon and his yrfenuman on heofenlicum spedum þæt þæt se oðer forðyldigan wolde for ateoriendlicre edwiste 
21.28.116: we seggað eow godes riht healdað gyf ge willan 
21.28.117: gyf we hit forsweogiað ne byð us geborgen 
21.28.118: cristes lufu us neadaþ þæt we symle þa godan tihtan þæt hi on godnysse ðurhwunian and þa yfelan we mynigiað þæt hi fram heora yfelnessum hrædlice gecyrran 
21.28.119: ne beo se rihtwisa gymeleas on his anginne 
21.28.120: ne se yfela ne ortruwige þurh his unrihtwisnysse 
21.28.121: se mildheorta god cwæð be eallum synfullum mannum twa word swyðe fremfulle 
21.28.122: declina a malo et fac bonum 
21.28.123: þæt is buh fram yfele and do god 
21.28.124: nis na genoh þæt þu fram yfele gebuge butan þu symle be þinre mæðe god gefremme 
21.28.125: god cwæð þæt he nolde þæs synfullan deað ac he wyle swyðor þæt he gecyrre fram his synnum and libbe 
21.28.126: we sculon beon þeonde symle on godnysse and ælce dæg geeacnian us þa ecan speda þa hwile ðe we moton 
21.28.127: forðam þe we ne magon æfter ure geendunge aht to gode gedon ac þær we habbað edlean þæs ðe we geearnodon her on life 
21.28.128: ne sceal se wisa mann beon butan godum weorcum 
21.28.129: ne se ealda ne beo butan eawfæstnysse 
21.28.130: ne se iunga ne beo butan gehyrsumnysse 
21.28.131: ne se weliga ne beo butan ælmesdædum 
21.28.132: ac uton symle smeagan hu we magon gode gecweman on eallum þingum þa hwile ðe we gesundfulle beon moton her on life 
21.28.133: drihten cwæð on sumere stowe 
21.28.134: arwurða þinne fæder and eac þine modor and se ðe fæder oþþe moder wyrgð he sceal deaþe swyltan 
21.28.135: swa swa we be sumum cilde rædaþ þe wæs receleaslice afedd 
21.28.136: hit wolde wyrian wælhreowlice drihten and se fæder ne rohte his receleasnysse 
21.28.137: þa æt nyxtan comon cwealmbære deofla swutelice gesewene on sweartum hiwe inn to þam cilde 
21.28.138: and hit sona hrymde fæder min fæder min me nimað þas deofla 
21.28.139: and behydde his heafod on his fæder bosme and wyrigde drihten 
21.28.140: and swa gewat sona mid þam sweartum deoflum forscyldigod to helle 
21.28.141: hit is gerædd þæt þa beoð gesælige þe soðfæstnysse lufiað and buton hiwunge farað 
21.28.142: forðam wite gehwa þæt god gesyhþ on ælcum menn hwæðer he færð þe mid soðfæstnysse þe mid hiwunge 
21.28.143: forþam ðe he ofersceawað and ofersyhþ ealle þing 
21.28.144: and him nan ðing digle nis þe on ælcere stowe andweard is 
21.28.145: and on swa hwylcere stowe swa we to him clypiað æfre he byð andweard þær man inlice clypað to him 
21.28.146: be þam is awriten 
21.28.147: eadig byð se mann þe symle byð forhtigende and to gode clypiende and soðlice se heardmoda befylð on yfele 
21.28.148: uton nu geþencan þis sceorte lif and georne clypian to þam ælmihtigan gode þæt he us geunne þæs ecan lifes þe nænne ende næfð 
21.28.149: forðam þe nan mann nat þyssere worulde geendunge ne furðon his agene geendunge 
21.28.150: mænig mann wolde þone maran dæl his lifes aspendan on his lustum and þone læssan on dædbote gyf he wiste hwænne he geendian sceolde 
21.28.151: us is bediglod ure geendung to þy þæt we sculon symle us ondrædan þone endenyhstan dæg þæne we ne magon næfre foresceawian 
21.28.152: we sculon forðig wacian on ure heortan and on geleafan 
21.28.153: we sculon wacian on hihte and on soðre lufe 
21.28.154: we sculon wacian on godum weorcum and don buton idelum gylpe gyf we hwæt lyttles to gode gedoð 
21.28.155: þæt we moton faran into heofenan rice mid þam mildheortan gode se þe on ecnysse rixað on ealra worulda woruld a butan ende amen
