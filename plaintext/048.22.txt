48.22.1: men ða leofestan us is on ælcne sæl geornlice to smeagenne and to hycgenne hu we þurh haligra boca tæcunge ures drihtnes willan mid godum dædum and mid halgum mægenum gefyllan magon 
48.22.2: and huru on ðisse halgan tide þe eallum mannum to hreowsunge and to dædbote geset is 
48.22.3: us is ðonne swiðe gedafenlic þæt we gelomlice ure circan secan 
48.22.4: and ðær mid micelre eadmodnysse and stilnysse us to urum drihtne gebiddan and godes word gehyran 
48.22.5: and se ðe on oðrum ðingum abisgad sy oððe to ðam ungehænde þæt he dæghwomlice his circan gesecan ne mæge he huruðinga on ðam sunnandagum and on oðrum freolsdagum þider cume to his uhtsange and to mæssan and to æfensange 
48.22.6: and na to nanum geflite ne to nanum woruldlicum spræcum 
48.22.7: ac to ðam anum þæt he his synna gode andette and hire forgifnysse bidde 
48.22.8: and ðære halgan þenunge mid micclum godes ege gehlyste 
48.22.9: and siððan mid ælmæsdædum gange him to his gereorde 
48.22.10: and mid micelre syfernysse and gemetfæstnysse his goda bruce 
48.22.11: and na mid nanre oferfylle ne mid oferdrince 
48.22.12: forði ðe cristenum men nis nan ðing wyrse ðonne druncenscipe 
48.22.13: men ða leofestan we willað eow warnian and us sylfe alysan mid gode 
48.22.14: and ælcne ðæra læran ðe us hyrcnyan wile þæt hi fram ðam unþeawe gecyrran and hi sylfe mid syfernysse healden 
48.22.15: þonne is manna gehwilccum to witanne þæt ælc ðæra manna ðe ðæne deofollican unðeaw hæfð þæt he wile on his gebeorscipe þurh his halsunge and ðurh his neadunge gedon þæt oðre men nimað mare ðonne hit gemet sy sceolon agildan gescead gode ægðer ge be him silfum ge be eallum ðam þe hi to ðam geneadiað 
48.22.16: besceawigen nu ða druncengeornan þæt hi synt micele mættran ðonne nytenu ðonne ða nytenu nellað nan ðing mare drincan ðonne him ðearf bið 
48.22.17: and se earma man wile drincan ðreo swa feala ge feower swa feala swa his nead wære 
48.22.18: and he eac on anum dæge mid ungesceade and mid micelre synne forspilð þreora daga oððe feowera andlifene 
48.22.19: and þæt nære to agitenne ðeah seo andlifne forspilled wurde gif ða men for gode ne forwurdon 
48.22.20: us is to witanne men ða leofestan þæt gif ða druncengeornan men heora druncennyssa geswican nellað and micele dædbote don ac þurhwuniað on ðam oferdrincum æt heora ende hi næfre mid gode nane reste ne begytað on ðam toweardan life 
48.22.21: ac hi buton ælcere tweonunge sceolon on ecnysse forwurðan 
48.22.22: forðan hit ne mæg na beon leas þæt paulus se apostol cwæð 
48.22.23: neque ebriosi regnum dei possidebunt 
48.22.24: þæt is on urum geðeode þæt næfre druncengeorne nagon godes rice 
48.22.25: ðonne cwyð sanctus agustinus 
48.22.26: toforan eallum oðrum ðingum ic eow bidde and ðurh þæne ondrædendlican domesdæg ic eow halsige þæt swa oft swa ge eow gemænelice gebeorscipas gegearwiað ge ða fatu ðæs micclan gemetes ðe þreo men oððe feower and eac hi hwilum beoð buton ælcum gemete hwilum willes hwilum geneadode gewuniað of to drincanne of eowrum gebeorscipe awurpað eall swa attor 
48.22.27: forðan ðe se ungesælige gewuna belaf of hæðenra manna biggenge 
48.22.28: and swa hwylc man swa ðis geðafað oððe on his agenum gebeorscipe oððe on oðre mannes ne tweonige he na þæt he ne offrað deofle 
48.22.29: forðan ðe þurh ðisne drync beoð ægðær ge ða sawle ofslagene ge ða lichaman gewanhalode 
48.22.30: he bytt eft on oðre stowe þæt manna gehwylc besceawige mid him sylfum hu hefig synn seo druncennys is and hu feala unðeawa heo on ðam men gebringð 
48.22.31: and þæt nan man ne geðafige þæt he ðære druncennysse oferswiðed wurde 
48.22.32: ðonne ða druncengeornan na þæt an þæt hi on ðam toweardan life mid ecum tintregum tocwylmede synt 
48.22.33: ac eac hy synt on ðisum andweardan life mid mænigfealdum untrumnyssum gewæhte 
48.22.34: and ðas ðing he sægð na þæt an læwædum mannum ac eac gehadedum 
48.22.35: forðan ðe hit is ðe wyrse þe manega preostas ðe synt þære micclan endebyrdnysse þæt hi sceolon oðrum mannum ðære syfernysse god bodian nabbað naðer ne ða sceama for mannum ne ðene ege for gode þæt hi hit sylfe ærest ne don and eac oðre swa feala swa hi mæst mægon oferdrenceað 
48.22.36: ac witan ða men þe ðyllice synt gif hi nellað hi sylfe gebetan þæt hi sceolon nede ðolian ece susle ge for him sylfum ge for eallum ðam þe hi ðærto tyhtað 
48.22.37: and he cwyð eft 
48.22.38: eala hu earm and hu bewependlic is þære manne lif þe ofer þæt riht þe eallum mannum gemetlice geðenud bið onginnað swilce hi nicumenne syn and swilce hi ealles dæges ær nahtes ne onbirigdon and þurh mistlice naman mare drincað þonne him neodþearf sy 
48.22.39: na þæt an ðurh libbendra manna naman ac eac þurh engla and haligra manna wenað þæt hi him micelne wurðscipe mid ðam gegearwian gif hi hio sylfe mid swiðlicre druncennysse oferlecgað 
48.22.40: and nyton þæt nane men ne doð halgum englum and halegum mannum swa hefigne teonan swa ða ðe on heora naman hira agene sawle ðurh oferdrince ofsleað 
48.22.41: men ða leofestan us is swiðe micel ðearf þæt we eadmodlice hyrcnian ðysum eadigan lareowum and ðysum halgum bocum and us sylfe forhabban fram þisum unðeawe 
48.22.42: forðan ðe þa ðe hira beboda lustlice gehyran willað and getreowlice gefyllað hi habbað ece mede mid gode and mid his halgum 
48.22.43: and ða ðe hit forhicgað and hit don nellað hi habbað ece susle mid deofle and mid his englum 
48.22.44: men ða leofestan we mynegiað eac ælcne getreowfulne man þæt he gelomlice lufige cumliðnysse and nanum cuman ne forbeode þæt he ne mote on his huse gerestan 
48.22.45: forðan ðe manega gode gelicodon þurh þæt þæt hi cuman onfengon and him arwurðlice þenodon 
48.22.46: and ure drihten is to cweðenne þonne he to ðam dome cymð 
48.22.47: hospes eram et suscepistis me 
48.22.48: þæt is on urum geðeode 
48.22.49: ic wæs cuma and ge onfengon me 
48.22.50: witon ðonne ða ðe cumliðnysse geornlice for godes lufan begað þæt hi crist sylfne on ðam cuman onfoð 
48.22.51: ælcum geleaffullum men is eac swiðe to warnigenne wið manaða 
48.22.52: forðan ðe hi synt swiðe forbodene ægðer ge on ðære ealdan æ ge on ðam godspelle 
48.22.53: manega men tellað to lytlum gylte þæt hi oðre men mid manaðum beswicen 
48.22.54: ac witun hi þæt hi beoð ealswa miceles wites scyldige swa ða manslagan and ða unrihthæmeras and ða oferdrinceras 
48.22.55: eac is manna gehwilcum þearf þæt he hine forhæbbe fram leasum cyðnyssum 
48.22.56: forðam hit is swiðe hefig gylt and fram urum drihtne sylfum forboden ða he wið moysen spræc on ðære dune þe man hæt sinai and ðus cwæð 
48.22.57: non falsum testimonium dices 
48.22.58: þæt is ne sæge ðu na lease cyðnysse 
48.22.59: and salomon cwæð þæt lease gewitan ne sceolde beon ungewitnod and swa hwilc man swa sægð leasunga on his nehstan his leohtfet bið acwænced on ðam ytemestan dæge 
48.22.60: and se ðe þæt soð wat and hit for æniges weoruldlices mannes ege forsugað he gecigð godes yrre ofer hine 
48.22.61: ægðer is ðonne scyldig wið god ge se ðe þæt soð bediglað ge se ðe ða leasunga sægð 
48.22.62: forðan ðe se ðe ða leasunga sægð wile derian and se ðe þæt soð wat nele fremian 
48.22.63: on feower wisan bið rihtwisnysse on dome forhwyrfed for ege and for gytsunge and for hatunga and for lufu 
48.22.64: ðonne bið seo rihtwisnys ðurh ege forhwyrfed þonne se dema forhtað for æniges anwealdes þingum þæt he na dear soð asecgan and riht gedeman 
48.22.65: hio bið forhwyrfed for gytsunge þonne þæs deman heorte bið mid sumere mede ablend 
48.22.66: and eac forðan þe he wile ðæne unsyldigan a woh behreafian 
48.22.67: heo bið eac forhwyrfed for hatunge ðonne se deme gewilnað þæt he wile ðam unscyldigan for sumum oðrum þinge derigan 
48.22.68: eac bið seo rihtwisnys þurh lufe forhwyrfed þonne se dema bewereð his freond and his magas ongean riht and ða fremdan mid unrihte bereafiað 
48.22.69: mid þisum feower ðingum bið rihtwisnys oftust on dome forhwyrfed and unscyldige men gederede 
48.22.70: and miccle swiður synt to bemænene þa ðe þa earman ofðricceað mid unrihte þonne þa ðe þæne teonan þoliað 
48.22.71: forðan ðe þa ðe þæne teonan þoliað geendiað hredlice þa hwilwendlican yrmðe 
48.22.72: ac ða þe hi mid unrihte bereafiað beoð betæhte ecum ligeum witun ðonne þa ðe ðyllicne gylt þurhteoð þæt hi synt mid þære ilcan dædbote to clænsigenne þe ða manslagan and þa unrihthæmeras and ða oferdrinceras and þa manswaran 
48.22.73: þæt is þæt hi sceolon seofon gear mid micelre angsumnysse hreowsian oððe hi man sceal ut of godes circan anydan and fram ealra getrywfulre manna gemanan awurpan 
48.22.74: be ðisum þinge cwæð se hælend on ðam godspelle 
48.22.75: quid prodest homini si uniuersum mundum lucretur anime uero sue detrimentum patiatur 
48.22.76: þæt is on urum geðeode 
48.22.77: hwæt fremað þam men þeah ðe he ealne middaneard gestryne and his sawul þolige ece forwyrd 
48.22.78: witodlice þa men þe ðyllice gyltas doð þeah hi fram oðrum mannum syn geðuhte arfæste hi synt him sylfum swiðe wælhreowe þonne hi nellað heora earman sawle gehelpan þe sceal on ðam ungeændudan witum ealle þas unrihtan dæda þolian 
48.22.79: men þa leofestan us is swiðe þearle to efstanne þæt we ure lif and ure þeawas gebeterian 
48.22.80: and þæt we bewepan þæt we ær to yfele gedydon 
48.22.81: and ofer þis ðære syngunge geswican 
48.22.82: forðan ðe god ne besceawað na hwilce we ær wæron ac he besceawað hwilce we beon þonne we dælan sceolon sawle and lichaman 
48.22.83: þæt is to witanne þæt god ne secð na þæs godan weorces angin ac he secð þæne ænde 
48.22.84: forðan ðe ælc man sceal beon demed be ðam geearnungum þe he hæfð þonne he of ðisum life hwyrfan sceal 
48.22.85: sume men onginnað god to donne ac hi hit endiað yfele swa iudas dyde 
48.22.86: he wæs ærest apostol and syððan he sealde urne drihten to cwale and on ænde hine sylfne on grine aheng 
48.22.87: and sume men onginneð yfel to donne ac hi hit geendiað wel swa paulus dyde 
48.22.88: he wæs ærest godes circena ehtere and syððan he wæs bodigend and acoren lareow 
48.22.89: þæt is ðæs godan weorces mægen þæt man þæron ðurhwunige æt his ende 
48.22.90: swa ure drihten on ðam godspelle cwyð 
48.22.91: qui perseuerauerit usque in finem hic saluus erit 
48.22.92: he cwæð þæt se beo hal on ðam toweardan life þe her ðurhwunað on godum dædum 
48.22.93: gif we god beginnað and we on ðam þurhwuniað æt urum ende þonne licað gode ure drohtnung 
48.22.94: efste þonne ælc man þæt he anrædlice gefulfremige þa god þe he beginne þæt he þurh þæt geearnige þæt he onfo þære ecan mede on heofonan rice
