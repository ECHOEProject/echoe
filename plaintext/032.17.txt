32.17.1: men ða leofestan us is to worðianne and to mærsianne seo gemind þæs halgan heahengles sancte michaeles 
32.17.2: se wæs wundorlic ærendraca ðæs ælmihtigan dryhtenes 
32.17.3: eac swilce nu todæge þam getriwum folce he wæs inlihted and gebirhted 
32.17.4: forðon ðonne men ða leofestan blissian we and gefeon in þisne simbelnisse dæg þæs halgan heahengles sancte michaeles 
32.17.5: se is on hefenum gecweden swa swa god sylfa age 
32.17.6: gehyron we forþon sinderlice 
32.17.7: drihtnes he is efenrixiende 
32.17.8: he is swiðe mihtig þam heahenglum þa standað dæges and nihtes beforan þrymsetle dryhtnes 
32.17.9: se is eallra haligra fultum 
32.17.10: and he is reccend eallra haligra saula 
32.17.11: and he is nergende godes folces 
32.17.12: and he is strang on gefeohte wið ðane miclan dracan 
32.17.13: swa hit sagað her on pocalipsis þære bec 
32.17.14: blission we on heofonas and on ða þe on heofnum sint 
32.17.15: forðon ðe sanctus michael he is strang feohtend wið þone miclan dracan 
32.17.16: þæt is ðonne wið ðam awyrgedum gæstum 
32.17.17: on þisne heahengel we sculon gelyfan and biddan us on fultum on ægehwilcere frecennesse þam cristenum folce 
32.17.18: ðis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael se wæs andfenga abeles saule þæs ærestan martires ðone his broðor cain for æfstum ofsloh 
32.17.19: ðis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael se is hæleda healdend 
32.17.20: and dryhtne fultummendum hira feorh he generede 
32.17.21: þæt wæs þonne noe and his suna þry and hira feower wif in þam micelan flode 
32.17.22: ðis is se halga heahengel sancte michael þæs gemynd we nu todæge weorðiað 
32.17.23: se wæs abrahames anlysend þæs heahfæderes ofer caldea þeode cumende fultumendum 
32.17.24: and he wæs latteow þam ðrym heahfæderum abrahame and isace and iacobe þurh ða ælðydigan land and ða uncuðan wegas 
32.17.25: he wæs him simle onweard fultum on æghwilcere frecydnesse 
32.17.26: þis is se halga heahengel sancte michael se wæs ferende on eastron þurh israela hus 
32.17.27: and egypta frumbearn he ofsloh and israela bearn he gefryðode 
32.17.28: þis is se halga heahengel sancte michael se ðe drihtne fultummendum þæt cristene folc mid his gescyldnisse in þam westene feowerti wintra he hit ferede and fedde 
32.17.29: ðis is se halga heahengel sancte michael se gesigefæsted stod beforan cananea cinne 
32.17.30: and þurh iosues handa þæt israelica folc he gelædde to þam gehatlande þæt is flowende hunie and meolce 
32.17.31: þis is se halga heahengel sancte michael and se æþela forestihtend in þæra cræftena handa þe salamones templ timbredon 
32.17.32: þis is se halga heahengel sancte michael se wæs strang scyldend þam þrym cnihtum þa wæron sende in ofen birnendes fires 
32.17.33: and he þa him bistod se engel 
32.17.34: and snitera gast he dihtode in hira muð 
32.17.35: þæt wæs þonne se halga benedicete 
32.17.36: þis is se halga heahengel sancte michael and se æþela scyldend wið deofles swipornesse 
32.17.37: swa se witega sægde þæt þæt deofol þohte þæt he sceolde gelæran þæt folc þæt hi worðodon moyses lichaman for god for his fægernesse 
32.17.38: ða cwæð him to se halga engel 
32.17.39: ic ðe beode mid mines drihtnes worde þæt ðu þæge þristnesse ne gedo þæt ðu his folc ne gescildige 
32.17.40: þis is se halga heahengel sancte michael se ðe a onweard fultum þurhwunode and drihtnes witigan mid him in æghwilcere stowe 
32.17.41: þis is se halga heahengel sancte michael þam dryhten befæste sancta marian saule æfter hire forðfore and he hi him bebead 
32.17.42: þis is se halga heahengel sancte michael se ðe anra gehwilces soðfæstes mannes saule gelædeð þurh þa gatu þæs ecan lifes to hefena rice 
32.17.43: þis is se halga heahengel sancte michael se ðe anra gehwilces haliges mannes bene gelæteð in dryhtnes gesyðe 
32.17.44: and he his hiredes gewyrht mid frofre he him eft toforlæteð 
32.17.45: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael and se snotora dihtend ðære cynelecra husa 
32.17.46: and he is se getreowa hierde ðære halgan heofonlican ceastre 
32.17.47: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael and se gleawa londbigena ðæs cynelican wingerdes se ðe ðisne getreowne gedeð and þa berian he gesamnað and ða winestran he ut awirpð and ðane wæstm þæs godan wingerdes he agifeð his hlaforde 
32.17.48: hwæt sindon þa beorgas ðe he þær samnað 
32.17.49: þæt sindon haligra manna and soðfæstra saula 
32.17.50: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael se goda hirde ðæs dryhtenlican eowdes 
32.17.51: se ðe ne læteð wulf ne ðeof nanewuht gewirðan on his hlafordes heorde 
32.17.52: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael and se gesundfulla sawend cristes æcera 
32.17.53: and se wæstmberenda riftere ðæra hwitra ðeodlanda se his hlafordes bernas gefelleð mid þy clænestan hwæte and ða egelan and ða fulnesse ut aworpeð nymðe ðæt sindon ða soðfæstan ðe he ascadæt fram ðam sinfullum saulum 
32.17.54: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael and se getreowa þeow þane dryhten gesette ofer ealne hiwscipe þæt he him mete sealde on ða rihtan tid 
32.17.55: hwæt is se mete nymðe ðæt he sceal on domesdæge anra gehwelcum men his dæda edlean forgildan 
32.17.56: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael and þæt beorhte tungel þæt bið ascinende dæges and nihtes on hefonum betwexh ðam gæstelicum tunglum beforan ðam godcundancyninge 
32.17.57: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael and se æðela nowend and se gleawa frumlida 
32.17.58: and se þancwirðesta stigend se ðe his scip gefelleð and mid heofonlicum wælum hit gefylleð 
32.17.59: þæt is ðonne mid þam halgum saulum and mid ðy wrygelse ðære godcundan gefillnesse 
32.17.60: ofer þæs sæs yðe he hit gelædeð 
32.17.61: þæt is ðanne ofer ðisses middangeardes frecennesse 
32.17.62: and þa halegan saula gelædeð to þære yðe ðæs heofoncundan lifes 
32.17.63: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael se ðe com on fultum þam crystenan 
32.17.64: swa hit sægð in actum apostolorum þæt on sumere ceastere ðære nama wæs træleg and æghwelce geare hæðen here ayddon ða ceasterware 
32.17.65: ða gecwædon ða ceasterware him betweonum ðreora daga fæsten 
32.17.66: and þa þæt fæsten geendod wæs ða com him to sanctus michael and he wæs to gefeohte gearu 
32.17.67: ða stod he ofer ðæs ceasteres burugate and hæfde him ligen sweord on handa 
32.17.68: and he aflimde ða elðeodigan sona þæt hi flugon on oðer ðeodland and hi næfre ma ðær oðeowdon 
32.17.69: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael and se mycila mundbora se nu todæg his stowe ætywde on eorðan þæt men sceolden hi ðære dæghwamlice dryhten weorðian 
32.17.70: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus michael se ðe ær ðisse worulde ende ofslihð þone ealdan feond þæt is se micla draca se ðe æt frymðe middangardes gesceapen wæs to ðam beorhtestan engle 
32.17.71: ac he selfa hit forworhte mid ði he cwæð 
32.17.72: ic hebbe min heahsetl to norðdæle 
32.17.73: and ic beo gelic þam heahstan cyninge 
32.17.74: and þa gefeol he and gehreas mid his werode on niwulnesse grund 
32.17.75: efene se illca antacrist se ær ðisse worlde ende cymeð on ðisne middangeard to ðam þæt he sceal gesamnian ða ðe his sindon 
32.17.76: þanne cymeð sanctus michael and hine ofslihð 
32.17.77: forðon ðe he hit æfre geðohte þæt he scolde gelic beon ðan heahstan cyninge 
32.17.78: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus mihael se ðe on þam neahstan dæge worulde ende and æt þam egesfullan dome he ðonne ða deadan aweceð mid dryhtenes hæse 
32.17.79: beoruhtere stefene he clipað and þus cwið 
32.17.80: surgite surgite arisað arisað 
32.17.81: and þonne arisað ealle ða deadan ðe eorðe forswealg oððe sæ bescencte 
32.17.82: oððe fir forbærnde oððe wildeor abiton 
32.17.83: oððe fuglas on lande tobæren oððe wirmas on eorðan fræten 
32.17.84: þis is se halga heahengel sanctus mihael se ðe ða godan to life gelaðað and gelædeð and þa yfelan on deað bescenceð 
32.17.85: and þonne ða halgan saula to heofona rice he gelædeð 
32.17.86: and þa geomriendan he blisað and þa wanhalan he gelacnað 
32.17.87: and þa elðeodegan he afrefreð and þam winnendum he ræste forgifð 
32.17.88: lærnerum gefean he ontyneð and þam lærrendum ongit he gerum læteð 
32.17.89: uton þone nu men ða leofestan biddan we þone halgan heahengel sanctus mihael þæt ura saula sie anfenge 
32.17.90: and hi gelæde on heofoncund rice to þam dryhtene ðe lifað and rixað mid fæder and mid suna and mid þam halgan gaste in ealra worlda world a butan ende amen
