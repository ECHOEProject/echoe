feria tertia de letania maiore 

331.55.1: men ða leofestan þys syndon halige dagas and halwendlice us to begangenne and to healdenne þurh rihtne geleafan mid fæstenum and mid ælmessylenum swa urum sawlum nydþearflic is 
331.55.2: utan geþencan hu ælmihtig god for us þrowode and geþafode ærest þa he wæs on heofona rices hyhðu up ahafen and on his wuldre unonwendedlic 
331.55.3: þa geeadmette he hine to þan þæt he syðþan on þisne middaneard wæs acenned and to menn geworden 
331.55.4: and he ða her on worulde wæs þrowigendlic monn 
331.55.5: and gyt he hine sylfne geeadmette to þon þæt he wolde for manncynnes þearfe geþrowian 
331.55.6: and hine sylfne lætan for ealles middaneardes are and alysnesse ahon and acwellan 
331.55.7: ærest he þæt geþafode his agenum willan þæt hine mann for ure þearfe genam and geband 
331.55.8: and he wæs mid swipum beswungen and hine man on þæt neb hræhte and hine man mid bradum handum on þæt neb sloh 
331.55.9: and him þyrnene helm for oðerne cynehelm on þæt heafod sette 
331.55.10: and eall he þæt eadmodlice for ure þearfe geþrowode 
331.55.11: and micle maran and mænigfealdre þing þonne ic nu on þisse hwile areccan mæge oððe asecgan þa þe he for mancynnes hælo and are on him sylfum aræfnode and forbær 
331.55.12: and þæt her ealra mæst is swa ic ær sæde þæt hine man for ure ealra neodþearfe and for ealles middaneardes alysednysse on rode gealgang aheng and gefæstnode 
331.55.13: and hine man eac þær mid spere gewundode 
331.55.14: and he þa his þæt halige heafod onhylde and his gast on his fæder hand ageaf and on his geweald bebead 
331.55.15: and eac to helwarum nyþer astah and ðone ealdan wiðerweardan feond þær geband and getigde 
331.55.16: and mænige halige sawla þæra manna þe him ær her on worulde gehyrdon and gecwemdon he þonon up ateah to heofona rices hyhðo and to his wuldre gelædde 

331.55.17: forðon men ða leofestan us is swyðe mycel þearf þæt we ealle þa þing geþencan and asmeagan geornlice and eadmodlice 
331.55.18: and him þæs symle inweardlice þancas secgan ealra þæra þinga þe he for mancynnes þearfe mildelice on him sylfum forbær and geþrowode 
331.55.19: and we symle geornlice his þa halgan and þa godcundan bebodu agan to gefyllanne and to gehealdenne on us sylfum 
331.55.20: ne syndon nænie on þissere worulde ne on þissum life þæs fæstlice and ðæs mislice þing þæt hi symle full hrædlice aweg ne gewitan 
331.55.21: þonne bið þæt swyðe scandlic and swyðe bysmorlic þæt man þæt lufige and on þam hangie þe he gesyhð þæt him daga gehwylce fram higeð and efsteð 
331.55.22: ac uton we micle swyðor habban godcunde lufe betwyx us 
331.55.23: forðam þe ic sæde ær for urum lufon ealles middaneardes fruma and ende to þissere eorðan astah 
331.55.24: and þa eac on þissere worulde for urum lufon deað þrowode and on him sylfum aræfnode 
331.55.25: uton we þonne forðon men þa leofestan geornlice tiligean þæt we for his lufe and for his willan us sylfe and ures lichaman lustas and willan on us sylfum cwylmen 
331.55.26: and þæt we eac for his lufon and for his willan ealle þa lustfulnessa þissere andweardan tide and ðissere gewitendan forseon and forhycgan 
331.55.27: þæt we of ðam wacran and of þam wyrsan to þam beteran and to þam selran becuman moton 
331.55.28: of ðissum lichamlicum þingum to þam godcundan and of þisses middaneardes life to his gesælignysse and to þam hihte þæs toweardan lifes and þæs ecan æristes becuman magan and moton 
331.55.29: ac utan hyhtan us and þeodan us to ælmihtigum gode and to his are and to his forgyfnysse 
331.55.30: forðon se sealmscop þæt lærde and swa cwæð 
331.55.31: cumað ge and uton us gebiddan and forð gangan beforan ælmihtigum gode and hreowsian for urum synnum and mandædum 
331.55.32: and don we godcunde gyfe to ðon þæt we godes are and his miltse begytan magon and moton 
331.55.33: and uton aweorpan fram us þone yfelan and ðone grimman gewunan 
331.55.34: and us mid godcunde mægne gearwian þæt we þæt heofoncunde lif geearnian magon 
331.55.35: and þæt we ne þurfon beon gode on þa wynstran hand gesette ne þone cwyde gehyran þe he to þam cwyð 

331.55.36: leofan men ac efston we mid fæstenum and mid ælmesdædum þæt us seo hell ne forswelge 
331.55.37: god ælmihtig is swyðe mildheort and manþwære 

be domesdæge 

331.55.38: ac hwæðere he on domesdæg byð swyðe reþe and swyðe rihtwis 
331.55.39: and þy he bið domesdæg gehaten forþan ðe drihten sylf on þæm dæge demeð eallum eorðwarum þa þe he ær sylfa hider gescop and geworhte swa hwæðer swa hi on þissere worulde ær geworhton swa heofona rice swa hellewite 
331.55.40: send þonne his englas to feower dælum þisses middaneardes and ðær his ða heofoncundan byman blawað 
331.55.41: and þonne ealle eorðware up arisað of ðæm ealdum eorðscræfum and of ðæm ealdum eorðdenum þe we on gehydde wæron 
331.55.42: þonne bið eal þes middaneard swyðe onhrered and onstyred 
331.55.43: duna myltað swa weax deð on fyre 
331.55.44: sæs beoð adrugode 
331.55.45: and steorran hreosað of heofonum swa snaw deþ on eorðan þonne hit swyðost sniwoð 
331.55.46: and stanas formyltað 
331.55.47: and seo sunne bið on þeostru gecyrred and se mona ne sylð nænne sciman of him 
331.55.48: and seo heofon bið toborsten fram eastdæle middaneardes oð þone westdæl 
331.55.49: and ðonne ealle heofonwaru and eorðwaru beoð swyðe forhte gewordene 
331.55.50: and þonne oðre siðe ure drihten hider on þisne middaneard becymeð to demanne mid mycelre menio engla and heahengla apostola and martyra 
331.55.51: and he þonne his þæt heofonlice werod him on þa swyðran healfe gesetteð and þa manfullan beoð on þa wynstran healfe gecyrred 
331.55.52: and he þonne on his fotum and on his handum þa dolhswaþu forð æteoweð and ðus sprecende bið and ðus cwyð 
331.55.53: eala þu man forhwon noldest þu þas wunda gelacnian þas ic for ðe þafiende wæs to þon þæt ic ealle þine wunda gelacnode 
331.55.54: oððe forhwan noldest ðu þysne þyrnenan helm me of þæm heafde alysan to þan þæt ic ðe of hellehæfte nearwan alysde and of deofles anwealde 
331.55.55: and he þonne eft oðre siðe swyðe mycclum and swyðe reðum wordum specende bið to þæm þe him on þa wynstran healfe gesette beoð and ðus cwyð 
331.55.56: gewitað ge awyrgedan gastas fram me in þæt forwyrde lif and in þa nyðemestan helle of þissum godcundan gemanan on deofla gemanan and of þissum heofonlican heape in þone hellican þreat 
331.55.57: ne cweð he þonne na þæt eow wæs gegearwod fram fruman þisses middaneardes 
331.55.58: ac he cwyð þæt wæs gegearwod fram fruman þisses middaneardes deoflum and his gescolum 
331.55.59: ðær is þonne heof and wop and cyrm and granung and cwanung and toða gristbitung 
331.55.60: and ðær is seo singale niht 
331.55.61: and ðær syndon þa ecan þystru 
331.55.62: and ðær ne bið næfre nænig leoht geseald 
331.55.63: ac þær bið se hearda hungor and se bitera þurst 
331.55.64: and ðær is wanunga and wita ma þonne æniges mannes earan ahlystan magon 
331.55.65: ne nanes mannes tunga nis to þam swyft þeah þe heo hæbbe xii heafdu and ðæra heafdu æghwylc hæbbe xii tungan and ðæra tungena æghwylc hæbbe xii stefna and ðæra stefna gehwylc hæbbe snyttro salomones ne magon hi þeahhwæðere ealle þa wean and ða witu ariman ne areccan þe þa earman and ða werigan sawla geþafian and þrowian sculon 
331.55.66: and nan þæra wita mare ne byð þonne þæt ure drihten him næfre eft on gemyndum ne byð ne ne becymð 
331.55.67: þæt is yrres dæg and æftes dæg 
331.55.68: and þæt is susle dæg 
331.55.69: and þæt is se bitera bifigenda dæg and se cwacienda dæg and se forhtigenda domesdæg 

331.55.70: tylian we nu forðon men þa leofestan mid fæstenum and mid ælmesdædum and mid cyriclicum wæccum and socnum and mid eorðlicum leohte þæt we magon and moton æt þam ælmihtigan gode geearnian þæt we moton on domesdæge beon on his swyðran healfe gesette and þone cwyde gehyran þe he to þam cwæð þe þonne on his swyðran gesette beoð 
331.55.71: he cweð cumað ge gebletsode þe mine bebodu heoldon and onfoð freolice mines fæder rice þæt eow wæs fram fruman þisses middaneardes togeanes gegearwod 
331.55.72: geunne us þonne se ælmihtiga god þæt we to ðam heofonlican eþle becuman magon and moton þær he leofað and ricsað mid fæder and suna and mid þam halgan gaste aa on ecnysse in ealra worulda woruld a butan ende amen
