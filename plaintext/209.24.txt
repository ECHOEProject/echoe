209.24.1: augustinus sæde on his cwidbocan þæt nan mann ne mihte asmeagan godes æ and his bebodan fullice healden bute se þe smylte mod hæfde and frig ælcere gedrefednysse 
209.24.2: he sæde þeh þæt þære wæren swyðe feawe oððe nan þe swa frig wære 
209.24.3: cwæð þeh þæt nan ne scolde beon swa idel þæt he betwux þan bisegan þysser wurlde sum þing ne ongunne leornigen beo his frigdomes mæðe 
209.24.4: and ælc mann eall alæte swa swa he mæst muge unnytte embhogen and geæmtige hine to godes þeowdome 
209.24.5: forþan þe god forgelt ælcen mænn beo his dæden swa swa he sylf cwæð on his godspelle
