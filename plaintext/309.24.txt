de cena domini 

de huius diei ueneratione et de domini misericordia in memorria æternæ iohannes euangelista uenera 

309.24.1: sægeð hit on ðysum bocum be ðære arwurðnysse þyses halgan dæges 
309.24.2: and be ðære unasecgendlican mildheortnysse ures drihtnes hælendes cristes hu arfæstlice he on ðysum dæge manncynne eadmodnysse bisne onstealde

309.24.52: et quia a deo exiuit et ad deum uadit

309.24.172: forðan heora æghwylc be heom sylfum synderlice þus cwæð numquid ego sum domine ac ic hit drihten eom 
309.24.173: butan iudas ana he swigade lengest 
309.24.174: forðan þe he wæs on his wordum þam oðrum swa ungelic swa he wæs on his geearnungum 
309.24.175: þeah ðe he ealra latost and eac bealdlicost cwæde 
309.24.176: numquid ego sum rabbi 
309.24.177: sægest þu lareow þæt ic hit sy 
309.24.178: þa cwæð he crist him to andsware tu dixisti þæt þu cwæde 
309.24.179: þa hlionode sanctus iohannes ofer þæs hælendes breost swa ic ær sæde 
309.24.180: acsode hine þa and him to cwæð min drihten hwylc ure is þæt þæt þa dæde gedon mynteð þæt he ðe belæwe 
309.24.181: þa andswarode him se hælend and him to cwæð se hit is se ðe ic him þysne bedeptan hlaf ræcan wille 
309.24.182: bestang þa þone hlaf þe he him on handa hæfde on þæt sealtfæt þe him beforan stod and þa sealde þam scariothiscan iudan and he hine þa sona ætt 
309.24.183: and æfter þa breades sticce eode him on þæt wiðerwearde deofol 
309.24.184: and he þa sona þanon ut gewat swa he syððan fram eallum his ræde gewiten wæs 
309.24.185: þa ongan he drihten heom endleofenum openlice his þa halgan þrowunge secgan and eall þa þing þe he wolde and geteohhad hæfde þæt be him geweorðan sceolde 
309.24.186: sægeð man þæt eft tomerigenne hu þæt geworden wære 
309.24.187: ac uton nu geþæncan men þa leofestan be ðære bysene þe se hælend þam scariothiscan iudan þone hlaf sealde 
309.24.188: þæt us is on ðam micel wærlicnys getacnad and æteowed on ðære onfangennysse ures drihtnes lichaman 
309.24.189: þæt is þonne þæs halgan husles 
309.24.190: uton forðan tilian þæt we syn clæne and unwæmme þam to onfonne swa swa þa oðre apostoles wæron næs na swa swa iudas wæs 
309.24.191: to þan þæt we magon and moton beon dælneomende godes rices 
309.24.192: mid urum drihtne hælende criste se ðe eall þas þing for mancynnes lufan and hælu mildheortlice abær and gemetfæst aræfnede 
309.24.193: god us gefultumige þæt we to him becuman moten se ðe leofað and rixað a on ecnysse god amhnamen
