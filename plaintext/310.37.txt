de confessione 

310.37.1: leofa man ic axie þe on drihtnes namen hwylces geleafan ðu beo to gode 
310.37.2: nu munegie ic ðe leofa cild þisse worde 
310.37.3: ilyfst ðu on god almitigne þe is fæder and on þone soðan sunæ and on þone halgan gast 
310.37.4: ic ilyfe 
310.37.5: gelyfst ðu þæt alle men sceolon on domesdæga of dæðe arisan 
310.37.6: gea leof ic ilyfe 
310.37.7: ofðincð þe alles þæs þe ðu to yfele hafst iðoht and icwæden and iworht 
310.37.8: gea leof al hit me ofðincð 
310.37.9: god hit ðe forgife and ic mote 
310.37.10: fæst nu ælc dæge to nones and to anes meles buton sunnandæg 
310.37.11: and forga hwit and forga ælc hæmedþing þas halgan tid alce niht 
310.37.12: and heald þe swiðe clænlice þæt ðu mage underfon ðet halige husel on þunnresdæg ær estron and eft on æsteræfen and on æsterdæg 
310.37.13: and sec þine cyrcan ilome 

310.37.14: nu sæcge ic þe her hwæt ðe is to donne hwæt to forletenne 
310.37.15: lufa ðinne drihten ærest þinga mid alle mode and mid alle mægene and mid clænre heortan and soðre lufa and þinne nextan swa swa þe sylfne 
310.37.16: and beo ælmesgeorne be ðinre mæðe and beo manþwæra and circgeorn 
310.37.17: and þe ðær georne to gode bide and to allum his halgum wið þam ðe þine synna þæs ðe forgifenron beon and þæt ðu þæt ece lif habban mote æfter þisses lifes ende 
310.37.18: and ne do þu nanum oðrum þæt ðu nelle þæt man ðe do 

310.37.19: leofa cild þonne ðu to cyrcan cume þonne sing þu ðær singallice þine bedu 
310.37.20: ne nan idel þær ne do ne ne spec 
310.37.21: and gebide for þine fæder and for ðinre moder and for ðinnum scrift and for al cristen folc and for alle cristene sawlæ 
310.37.22: þone bist ðu on ure bedrædene 

310.37.23: and þu ahst to fyllene þine seofen tidsangas under dæg and niht 
310.37.24: þæt is to ælcan tidsange seofan paternoster 
310.37.25: þet is þæt drihtenlice gebed þe ure drihten sylf tæhte his leofan leorningcnihtum þa ða heo hine axodon hu heo sceoldon heom biddan 

310.37.26: and þe ðe his dægsang cunne singe þone swa he oftest mage and his credan ilome 
310.37.27: þæt is ðe rihta leafa þe ælc mon cunnan sceal 
310.37.28: and bebeorh þe wið þa æhta hæfedsynna þæt ðu þa ne fremme 
310.37.29: þæt beoð morðor and stala and manaðas and unrihtgitsung and unrihthæmed and gifernessæ and tælnessa and leasa witnessa 

310.37.30: and heald þu wæl þa twelf ymbrigdagas þe on twelf monðum beoð 
310.37.31: and burh þe wið liblacas and attorcreftas and dyrnegeligera and twyspecnessæ and oformodignessa and geornessa oðre manne æhta and wið ærætas and alræ galnessæ and wið alle unsibbe 
310.37.32: þe ðe wyle miltce habbæn æt gode wið alle þæs misdæde he hine mot and sceal georne gewarnian 

310.37.33: ic ðe bidde and beode eac þæt ðu gode ælmihtigum georne hyre 
310.37.34: forþon þe me is mucel neod þæt ic ðe riht lære and þe is neodþearf þæt ðu riht do 
310.37.35: and þæt ðe nefre deofol to ðam beswican ne mote þæt ðu nefræ ne læte unandet ænige synnæ þære þe ðu iwroht habbe 
310.37.36: ne beo heo nefræ to ðam micel ne to ðam lytel ne þe nefre ne þince to ðam hæfig ne to ðam uneaðlic ne to ðam fracodlic þæt ðu his effre læte ænig þing unandet 

310.37.37: and iþenc þæt ðu ænne nacodne licamen on ðas weorld mid þe brohtes and þu hi scealt eft ana alætan buton þu hwæt for godes lufon to gode gedo þa hwile ðe ðe god þisses witendan lifes onlænan wylle 

310.37.38: and iþænc þæt ðe is ðeo tid swiðe uncuð and þe dæg þe ðu scealt ðæs lænan weorld forlæten and to godes dome iled beon 
310.37.39: and ðonne mid þinre anre sawle riht agildon alra ðinga gehwylces þæs ðu ær mid þinum licaman fremedest godes oððe yfeles 
310.37.40: þær ne bið nan ðing godes almihtiges eagum bidiglod 

310.37.41: do nu swa ic lære 
310.37.42: ic bidde þe forlet ælc yfel þæs ðe ðu mage and do to gode þone dæl þe ðu mæge 
310.37.43: þonne most ðu wununge habbon mid gode sylfum on heofenum þær ðær he sylf leofað and rixað mid fæder ant sunæ and halig gast a on ecnesse amen
