sermo ad populum dominicis diebus 

283.4.1: leofan men us bisceopum and eallum mæssepreostum is swiðe deope beboden þæt we æfre sculon mynegian and tyhtan eow læwede menn georne to eowre sawla þearfe and hu ge agan her on life rihtlice to libbanne 
283.4.2: and gif we swa ne doð þonne beo we swyðe scyldige wið god ælmihtigne 
283.4.3: nu wylle we eow secgan be þæs haligan sunnandæges halignysse hu se ælmihtiga god hine gehalgode 
283.4.4: and hu he wyle þæt he freols beo fram eallum unrihtdædum and þeowetlicum weorcum 
283.4.5: for ic wat þæt hit is eow uncuðre þonne ge þearfe ahton 
283.4.6: hit wæs þæt siððan crist þæs ælmihtigan godes sunu wæs astigen up to heofena rice 
283.4.7: syððan he hæfde her gewunad onmang mannum þreo and xxx wintra and healf gear 
283.4.8: þæt men forgymdon godes laga swyðor þonne heora þearfa wæron 
283.4.9: and þæs halgan sunnandæges freols wyrs heoldon þonne hit gode licwyrðe wære 
283.4.10: ða asende se ælmihtiga god an ærendgewrit ufan of heofenan be anum halgan engle to anum bisceope se hatte petrus 
283.4.11: se wæs biscop on antiochia þære burh þær þær sanctus petrus se apostol ærest gesæt his biscopsetl 
283.4.12: on þam gewrite stod eall be þæs dæges halignesse 
283.4.13: and þæt gewrit ne awrat nan eorðlic man 
283.4.14: ac godes agen ængel swa swa seo halige þrynnys hit sylf gedihte 
283.4.15: and eac se ængel bebead þam biscope þæt he hit sceolde cyðan eallum cristenum mannum þæt men geswican heora unrihtes þe hi on þam halgan dæge drugan and gyt to swiðe dreogað 
283.4.16: nu is þis þæt angin þæs engles spræce þa þa he þæt gewrit þam bisceope on hand sealde 
283.4.17: he cwæð ic godes engel middaneardes hælendes boda beode and hate þæt ægðer ge cyningas ge eorlas and gerefan þe mæst unriht dreogað and eac ealle men ægðer ge gehadode ge læwede þæt hi lufian rihtwisnysse on wordum and on worcum and on eallum þingum 
283.4.18: and þæt hi hyran drihtne hælende criste eorne on eallum ege 
283.4.19: forþan þæt is ealles wisdomes angin þæt man habbe ege to gode 
283.4.20: and ic beode þæt men healdan þone drihtenlican dæg fram eallum þeowetlicum weorcum 
283.4.21: þæt is fram sæternesdæges none oð monandæges lihtinge 
283.4.22: forþan þe se dæg wæs se forma dæg þe se soða scyppend þæt is god ælmihtig gesceop þa þa he ealle þing gesceop 
283.4.23: and eac syððan fela wundra on þam dæge geworhte ægðer ge ær þan þe he menniscnysse underfenge ge eac syððan 
283.4.24: ðæt is þonne þæt he gesceop heofenan and eorðan and englas and heahenglas and ealle heofenlice miht and endebyrdnysse on þam dæge 
283.4.25: and eac on þam dæge he gesceop manna sawla 
283.4.26: and þa þa moyses se heretoga lædde godes folc of egipta lande þa on þam dæge he hit lædde ofer þa readan sæ 
283.4.27: swa þæt he sloh mid anre gyrde on þa sæ and heo toeode on twa 
283.4.28: and þæt folc for betwux þam twam wæterum on þam grunde ealle drygsceode oð hi coman to þam lande up 
283.4.29: and on þam dæge com ærest se heofenlica mete ufan of heofnan þam ylcan folce to bilyfan 
283.4.30: and god hi mid þam afedde xl wintra on þam westene þe hi to foron 
283.4.31: and se mete hatte manna 
283.4.32: and on þam dæge wæs crist þæs lifigendan godes sunu geboren of sancta marian innoðe 
283.4.33: soð man ealswa he is soð god 
283.4.34: middaneard to alisanne of deofles anwealde þe his ær geweald ahte for adames gylte 
283.4.35: and syððan he acenned wæs he awende on þam dæge wæter to wine 
283.4.36: and on þam dæge he wæs gefullod 
283.4.37: and on þam dæge he gereordade æt anum mæle v þusend manna of fif berenum hlafum and twam fixum syððan he hæfde þone bilyfan mid heofoncundlicre bletsunge þam ylcan dæge gebletsod 
283.4.38: and þa þa hi ealle fulle wæron þa bær man þær up of þan þe hi læfdon twelf leapas fulle 
283.4.39: and on þam dæge aras crist of deaðe syððan he þrowade on rode for mancynnes hæle 
283.4.40: and on þam dæge wæs þæra iudeiscra manna geleafleasnys gewiten fram mannum and riht geleafa asprang onmang godes gelaðunge 
283.4.41: syððan crist æteowode hine sylfne sancte petre æfter his æriste of deaðe 
283.4.42: and on þam dæge sende god þone halgan gast ofer þa apostolas ealle þær þær hi wæron on anum huse inne belocene 
283.4.43: and tæhte heom þæt hi cuðan ealle þa spræca þe syndon swa wide swa middaneard is 
283.4.44: and on þam dæge wyrð middaneard eall geendad 
283.4.45: and on þam dæge cymð god to demanne eallum mancynne ælcum be his agenum gewyrhtum 
283.4.46: and farað þonne þa godan and þa rihtwisan into ecere myrhðe mid gode and his englum 
283.4.47: and þa arleasan farað to helle mid deofle and þær beoð gecwylmede on cwicsusle 
283.4.48: and eardiað þa a butan ende mid him 

283.4.49: nu forþam bebead drihten god eow þæt ge healdan þone sunnandæg fram ælcum geswinclicum worce and swyðost fram þam þe ic eow nu secgan wylle 
283.4.50: ðæt is þæt man ah to forganne ealle fulnyssa þe gode laðe syndon 
283.4.51: þæt is forliger and druncennys mannsliht and leasung 
283.4.52: reaflac and stalu unrihthæmed 
283.4.53: and geflit æfest and ælc þæra mandæda þe mannum forboden is 
283.4.54: and ælc gemot ænig mann to fremmanne 
283.4.55: ac man ah cyrican and haligdom to secanne and þær hine georne inne to gebiddanne 
283.4.56: and mid eadmodnysse hlystan loca hwæt þa lareowas heom þær to godes lage tæcan 
283.4.57: and þa godan weorc þe god us beboden hæfð to adreoganne on þam drihtenlican dæge 
283.4.58: ic eow wylle eac eallswa cyðan þæt man ah seoce men to geneosianne 
283.4.59: and deade bebyrian 
283.4.60: earmingas þam þe onhagað fedan and scrydan 
283.4.61: and loca hwa þære mihte age he mot gehæftne man alysan wreccan 
283.4.62: and ælþeodige underfon 
283.4.63: and ælc be his mihte welwyllendlice heom god don 
283.4.64: wudewum and steopcildum and utancumenum froferlice fylstan to rihte 
283.4.65: and loca hwylc cristenman sy ungesibsum man ah on þam dæge hine to gesibsumianne 
283.4.66: þis syndon þa weorc þe gode syndon anfenge on ælcere tide and swyðost hig syndon to healdenne on sunnandagum 
283.4.67: forþan se sunnandæg is se forma dæg ealra dagena 
283.4.68: and he bið se endenyhsta æt þyssere worulde ende 
283.4.69: and gif ge þis nellað healdan cwæð god ic wylle swingan eow mid þam smeartestum swipum 
283.4.70: þæt is þæt ic witnige eow mid þam wyrstan wite swa þæt ic sende ofer eow min yrre on feower wisan þæt is hunger and sweordes ecge cwyld and hæftnunge 
283.4.71: þæt is þa þe ahte syndon hi sculon fleonde on gefeohte beon ofslagene 
283.4.72: and þa ealdan sculan earmlice licgan heapmælum æt ham hungre acwolene 
283.4.73: and man sceal þa geoguðe geomorlice lædan gehæft heanlice mid heardum bendum 
283.4.74: and swa bysmorlice bringan of heora eðle 
283.4.75: and betæcan eow teonlice syððan on hæðenra hand heries lafe 
283.4.76: and syððan æfter þære earmlycan eowre geendunge ic besence eowre sawla on susle on helle 
283.4.77: swa swa ic hwilon dyde þa twa burh sodomam and gomorram þe mid heofonlicum fyre her wurdan forbærnde 
283.4.78: and ealle þa þe him on eardodon æfre byrnað on hellegrunde on hatan fyre 
283.4.79: forþan hi þone mildan god manfullice gremedon 
283.4.80: and ealswa hit gelamp on moyses dagum mines gecorenan þæt wære twegen men þa wæron genemnode dathan and abiron 
283.4.81: hi ic besencte mid sawle and mid lichaman on hellegrund for heora oferhigde 
283.4.82: and forþan hig spræcon bysmorlice be me and be minum sacerdum 
283.4.83: and ic sende ofer eow þa þeode eow to hergianne and eower land to awestenne þe ge heora spræca ne cunnan 
283.4.84: forþan þe ge ne healdað sunnandæges freols and forþan þe ge me forseoð and mine beboda noldon healdan 

283.4.85: ac wite ge gewislice and gemune ge wel georne þæt ic æfre fram frymðe bebead þone drihtenlican dæg to healdenne 
283.4.86: swa hwa swa ænige cypinge on þam dæge begæð oððe oðre þing 
283.4.87: þæt man claðas waxe oððe ænig cræftig man him on his cræfte tylige 
283.4.88: oððe man efesige oðerne man oððe bread bace 
283.4.89: oððe ænig ungelyfed þing bega on þam dæge 
283.4.90: he scel beon utlaga wið me and ealle þa þe him to þam unrihte fylstað and him þæt geþafiað 
283.4.91: forþan þa men þe swylc þing begað ne begytað hi na mine bletsunge ne mine myltse 
283.4.92: ac heom becymð færlice min grama ofer for þæs dæges forsewennysse 
283.4.93: and ic asende ofer eow mancwealm and orfcwealm

283.4.96: and hu ic geþafode þæt twegen cyningas foran of rome mid here to hierusalem þære burh þe me wæs burga leofost ær þan þe hig mine beboda tobræcon þe þære burge þa geweald ahton
