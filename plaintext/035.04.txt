35.4.1: men þa leofestan her onginð þæt halie gewrit þe com fram heofenan into hierusalem 
35.4.2: soðlice hit gefeol beforan þam gaton effrem 
35.4.3: and þær hit wæs funden þurh anes preostes handa þæs nama wæs achor 
35.4.4: and he hit sende to anre oðre byrig to oþrum preoste þe genemned is ioram 
35.4.5: þe hit asende fram bethania byrig to oðrum preoste þe genemned is machabeus 
35.4.6: and he hit asende to monte garganum þær sancte michaeles circe is þæs heahengles 
35.4.7: soðlice þæt ylce gewrit þurh cristes willa ure hlafordes com to rome to sancte petres byrgene 
35.4.8: and ealle þa men þe wæron on þam burgum þær þæt gewrit to com dydon þreora daga fæsten and halie gebedu and ælmessan 
35.4.9: þæt ure milda hlaford heom fultum sealde and geopenode gewitt on manna heortan to oncnawenne ure drihtnes hælendes cristes mildheortnesse 
35.4.10: eac hit segð for hwilcon þingum þis gewrit com to hierusalem and to oðrum burgum þus gesutelod 
35.4.11: and hit com on þone halgan sunnandæg 
35.4.12: and þæt folc soðlice budon þone ylcan dæg to mærsianne mid micelre gecyrrednesse and geleafan 
35.4.13: on sunnandæg nan man ne healde gemot ne ne demon domas 
35.4.14: ne aðas ne swerion ne grindan ne hlafas bacan 
35.4.15: ne bicgan ne syllon on þone halgan sunnandæg 
35.4.16: ne wyrtan on wyrtune ne gaderion 
35.4.17: ne nanne ne tælon ne nane huntunge nabbon 
35.4.18: forþam soðlice þurh þas þing bið þes middangeard forworden and cymð godes yrre ofer eow 
35.4.19: eala yrmingas nyte ge þæt god geworhte heafenas and eorðan sæ and eal þæt þæron is on syx dagum 
35.4.20: and syððan geworhte þone forman man adam 
35.4.21: and for his agægednesse fif þusend and twa hund and viii and twentig geara rihtwise and synfulle on helle forlet 
35.4.22: crist com for us on þisne middaneard on þeowan hiwe 
35.4.23: and micel for us þolode 
35.4.24: and æfter his æreste to heofena rice rihtwise clypode 
35.4.25: ic eow bidde and mynegie abysegiað eow þa hwile ge þæs fyrstes habbon on eowrum gebedum and on fæstenum and on wæccan and on ælmessan and on mycelre forhæfdnesse eoweres lichaman 
35.4.26: gað to cirican gelomlice 
35.4.27: and settað rodetacn geond eower hus 
35.4.28: and ic eow beode fyliað criste on axan and on hæran mid þreora daga fæstene ealswa niniuete dydon þæt ge ne forwyrðon 
35.4.29: eala yrmingas þreagað eow and begitað cristes rice þe eow is ge gearwod fram frymðe middaneardes 
35.4.30: forþam þe dæghwamlice deað gegearwað beforan eowrum eagon 
35.4.31: and ic kyðe eow þurh þis gewrit 
35.4.32: gif ge nellað eow gerihtlæcan and sunnandæg ne healdað 
35.4.33: and eower teoðunge getrywlice syllað of eallon þam þe eow drihten alæned hafað 
35.4.34: na þæt an of eowrum geswince ac eac ge agon eowerne cræft ealswa teoðian 
35.4.35: and of ealre eowre cypinge and of eallum þam þingon þe ge æfre agan 
35.4.36: and on eower flæsce þurh forhæfdnesse eoweres lichaman 
35.4.37: and gif ge nellað gelyfan ge sculon þolian micel wite on helle 
35.4.38: drihten segð and eow soðlice cyð be þisum bufancwedenum þingum þe ge me ne aguldon 
35.4.39: doð dædbote on þissere worulde 
35.4.40: and gif ge nellað geswican and betan ic asendæ ceaferas on eowre wudas 
35.4.41: and gærshoppan on eowerne hwæte þæt fornimað eowerne bileofan 
35.4.42: and ic asende hearde stanas and oðre fela frecednessa 
35.4.43: and ic asende ofer eow hæðen folc þæt fornimað eow and eowre bearn 
35.4.44: gif ge ne healdað þone sunnandæg mid rihte ne ne gelyfað on me and on min bebod 
35.4.45: and ic asende ofer eow hunger and hreohnesse 
35.4.46: and byrnende ren and sweflenne lig and fela ungelimpa 
35.4.47: and ic asende on eowrum muþum and on eowrum nosum and on eowrum eagum and on earan þæge wyrrestan gnættas and þa geættrode eow to amyrrenne 
35.4.48: ic eom soð god and soð ic eow secge 
35.4.49: and þus swerige þurh me sylfne and þurh mine halgan englas cherubin and seraphin 
35.4.50: þæt min yrre cymð ofer eow ær on lytlan fyrste 
35.4.51: and fela frecednessa synd gegearwod togeanes eow 
35.4.52: and gefyrn wære gif minre leofan moder þingung 
35.4.53: nære sancta marian and sancte michaeles and sancte petres and þæra xii apostola 
35.4.54: and soðlice ic eow secge 
35.4.55: gif ge wyllað geswican eowre yfelra dæda 
35.4.56: ic geopenige eow heofena renscuras 
35.4.57: and ic sylle eowre eorðan wæstmbærnesse and genihtsumnesse 
35.4.58: and ic gelenge eower lif 
35.4.59: and ge beoð libbende on ealra worulda world 
35.4.60: eala ge mine getrywestan ic secge eow 
35.4.61: þurhwunige mid eow waciende and gebiddende and ælmessan syllende 
35.4.62: and ealle yfele dædan forlætende and ælc unrihthæmed forfleon and ælcne mansliht forbugan 
35.4.63: healdað mine beboda þæt ge geearnian eow þæt ge habban ealra halgena geferræddene 
35.4.64: men þa leofestan fyliað me and gelyfað on þone dæg and þæt þis gewrit is gesend of minre agenre mihte and of þam þrymsetle 
35.4.65: geclænsiað eow fram eowrum horwum synnum 
35.4.66: and geglengað eowre sawle 
35.4.67: þæt ge magon eow geahnian þæt ece lif 
35.4.68: witað la yrmingas þæt for eowre teoðunge þe ge me ætbrudon 
35.4.69: and for eower untimlican geswince þe ge dydon on sunnandagum and on þam halgum freolsdagum 
35.4.70: and for eower untrywleaste þe ge ne heoldon ongean eowre frynd 
35.4.71: þæt synd eower neheboras and godsibbas and ealle geleafulle 
35.4.72: ic adylgie eower naman of þære liflican bec gif ge þurh dædbote nellað gebetan 
35.4.73: and buton ge gelomlicor eowere cirican secean 
35.4.74: and þær underfon andetnesse and gecyrrednesse 
35.4.75: soðlice eower wif ne wurðiað þone halgan sunnandæg and þa freolsdagas on geare 
35.4.76: ic asende næddran to slitenne heora flæsc 
35.4.77: and hangiende to heora breostan 
35.4.78: and sucende hi ealswa heora bearn 
35.4.79: hi to witniende eal for þære unclænnesse þe ge ne heoldon þone halgan sunnandæg 
35.4.80: and þa cildra þe beoð begiten on sunnanniht and on þam halgan freolsnihtum hi sceolan beon geborene butan eagon and butan fotum and butan handon and eac swilce dumbe 
35.4.81: forþam þe ge ne heoldon mid clænnesse þa halgan niht 
35.4.82: and ne wiðtuga mid eowre tungan to cursiende 
35.4.83: and beoð gemodegode on eowrum scrude 
35.4.84: ic eow bidde þæt ge healdon þone restedæg fæstlice 
35.4.85: and ic eow beode þurh þis gewrit þæt ge ne wahson on þam sunnandæge ne on þam freolsdagum eower heafod 
35.4.86: ne ne sceran ne efsian eower fex 
35.4.87: soðlice þa þe gehyrað þas word and nellað gelyfan hi beoð amansumod 
35.4.88: soðlice þis gewrit feol of heofenum into hierusalem 
35.4.89: and swa com to rome to sancte petres byrgene 
35.4.90: and æror comon twa and þis is þæt ðridde 
35.4.91: and æfter þison ne cymð næfre ma 
35.4.92: ge bisceopas and mæssepreostas kyðaþ þis gelomlice eallum folce 
35.4.93: and sendað þis gewrit geond ealle scira 
35.4.94: þæt eal folc hit geornlice underfoo 
35.4.95: and his gelyfan þæt hit nis þurh nanne man geworht ac þurh þæs hehstan hælendes handa gewriten 
35.4.96: and þa men þe þis gewrit habbað and eow kyþað healdað hi and hi underfoð mid ealre geornfulnesse and lufe and blisse for cristes lufon 
35.4.97: forþi se þe underfehð rihtwisne on þæs rihtwisan naman he underfehð þæs rihtwisan mede 
35.4.98: and se þe underfehð witigan on þæs witigan naman he underfehð þæs witigan mede amen
