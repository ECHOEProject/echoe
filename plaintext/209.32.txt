209.32.1: on tiberies dagen þæs mycele caseres hit gelamp binnen lyttle fæce æfter þan þe crist ahangen wæs þæt sum æðele mann wæs þæs name wæs tirus 
209.32.2: and he wæs on equitania rice king under tiberie þan casere 
209.32.3: and he wæs gelomest wunigende on þære ceastre libie 
209.32.4: and he wæs se ilca tirus swa unhal on his andwliten þæt þæt adle þe cancre hatte him wæs on þan nebbe fram þan swyðre nosþyrlle oððet hit com to þan ege 
209.32.5: and wæs þæt sum man wæs farende of iudea lande þæs name wæs natan 
209.32.6: he wæs anes burhmannes sune on israele lande þæs name wæs nau 
209.32.7: and se ilce nathan wæs forliðen þæt he wæs gefaren fram ælcen lande to oðren and fram sæ to sæ swa þæt he hæfde ealle eorðe gemæren þurhfaren 
209.32.8: and se ilca nathan wæs gesænd fram tiberie þan casere to romes byrig to þan þæt he scolde his ærende læden claudium 
209.32.9: and he ferde forð on þære sæ and se suðerne wind hine bedraf to þære burh libia 
209.32.10: and tirus þær burge hlaford þæt gefran and gecneow beo heora færelde þæt heo of iudea lande wæron and cwæð 
209.32.11: næfre ic ær swylcne wunder ne geseh þæt of iudea lande scipen scolden hider cumen 
209.32.12: and tirus þider sænde and het þæt þider scolde to him cumen se þe eldest wære on þan scipe 
209.32.13: nathan him sone com to 
209.32.14: and tirus him sone axede hwæt eart þu and hwanen come þu hider 
209.32.15: he him andswerede ic eam nathan naos sune 
209.32.16: and ic eam of grecane rice 
209.32.17: and ic of iudean wæs þan pontisscen pilate underðeodd 
209.32.18: and ic eam gesænd fram þan casere tiberie to claudium 
209.32.19: and me wæs se suðerne wind beæften 
209.32.20: and hider adraf and awræc þæt ic nat hwar ic eam 
209.32.21: tirus þa cwæð 
209.32.22: la gyf þu mihtest ænige bote me finden of minen yfele ic þe wolde gyfen gold and seolfer swa mycel swa þin scip heonne ferigen wolde 
209.32.23: and he him his yfel æteowde 
209.32.24: þa cwæð nathan 
209.32.25: læt þe fulhtigen on name þæs fæder and þæs sunen and þæs halgen gastes 
209.32.26: þonne byst þu sone hal 
209.32.27: tirus his worden gelefde and fulht underfeng 
209.32.28: and he wæs sone hal þurh rihten geleafe and swa fæger and swa strang swa he wæs þa þa he wæs þrittig wintre 
209.32.29: and he þa ealle his þeode to cristendome awænde 
209.32.30: and his godes on fyre forbærnde 
209.32.31: and halige mynstres arærde 
209.32.32: and halige rode þærinn gesette 
209.32.33: and he þa nathan and his ærendraca æfter his broðre titus and vaspasius sænde 
209.32.34: and wiðinnen feower wucan comen to him mid mycelen ferde 
209.32.35: and heo þa befrinen þone casere hwæt heo scolden 
209.32.36: and he heom atealde of þan nazarenisscen kinge hwu he gepined wæs and ealle þa wundra þe he on ierusalemes lande worhte 
209.32.37: and he æteowde his unhæle and hwu he fulluht underfeng and ciricen hrærde and þæron belefde 
209.32.38: and begen his broðren þa to cristendome awænde 
209.32.39: and heo to ierusalem asænde and mid mycele ferde þa burhware uten belagen 
209.32.40: titus and vaspasius and tirus ferden geond eall þæt land and ealle þa iudees ofslogen þe criste nolden heren and fulluht underfon 
209.32.41: and heo lagen abuten þa ceastre þæt heo næfden nænne bigleofe 
209.32.42: and heo inne þære ceastre namen to hræde þæt heora ælc oðerne ofsloh 
209.32.43: and heo þa eall þæt land to cristendome awænden 
209.32.44: þa com godes bebod ofer heom eallswa heo ær abæden 
209.32.45: þæt wæs his wraca þæt heo syððen æfre unwurðe wæron on heora lifdagen and get synden underlinges 
209.32.46: forþan þe heo heora hlaford belæwden 
209.32.47: ne wurð of heom næfre cyng ne ealdor ofer land ne ofer burh 
209.32.48: þiss synd þa gedone þing þe beo uren ælmihtige hlaforde godes sune gedone wæron 
209.32.49: þan seo lof and wille and wyrðmynt fæder and sune and þan halgen gaste aa buten ænde amen
