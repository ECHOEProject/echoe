331.54.1: men ða leofestan we magon gehyran secgan be ure sawle þearfe 
331.54.2: forþon þe us is neodþearf þæt we on þisse tide þa hwile þe we moton and magan þæt we geornlice us clænsian and frætewien us sylfe mid godum dædum to þam ecum leohte 
331.54.3: eac us is þearf þæt we on ælce tid ure synna andetten and beten 
331.54.4: and hwæðre we sceolon onsundrum on þas halgan dagas to urum drihtne clypian 
331.54.5: and biddan þæs ecan lifes urum sawlum and þæs ecan heofonan rices 
331.54.6: þæt we on soðre hreowe ure synna andettan and beten 
331.54.7: and we for þæm gerime þæs hweorfendan geares ymbe xii monað þas halgan dagas weorþiað mid godcunde lofe and mid gastlicum sange 
331.54.8: and ða halgan reliquias beran gode to lofe and his halgum 
331.54.9: and we sculon eadmodlice biddon ealle þa halgan on heofonum þæt hi us syn þingeras wið þone ælmihtigan drihten þære halgan þrynnesse 
331.54.10: and we sculon biddan eac ealle halgan þæt hi us æteowen þa heofonlican mildsunge heora fultumes 
331.54.11: þæt we magan and moton þurh heora bene geearnigen þæt we deofles niþas oferswiðen and us ece lif geearnigen 
331.54.12: forðan is gedafenlic cristenum folce þonne hi gesamnigen hi to heora sawle þearfe þæt hi þonne georne gehyren godes worda and ðæra halgena gewrita 
331.54.13: ðonne magon we on þisse worulde geearnian ure synna forgyfenesse and ures lichoman hælo and gesibsum lif and eorðan wæstmas 
331.54.14: and þæt us ealra mæst is ece eadignes æfter þisse worulde 
331.54.15: swa ure drihten on his godspelle cwæð 
331.54.16: doð hreowe eowra synna þonne genealæcað ge heofona rice 
331.54.17: and eft he cwæþ 
331.54.18: mara gefea bið godes englum on heofonum þæt an synfull mann to bote gecyrre þonne be nigon and hundnigontig þe næfre ne agylton 

331.54.19: hwæt us þonne nu is myccle betere þæt we to þære bote gecyrran þa hwile þe se læcedom on ure mihte seo 
331.54.20: forðam drihten is swyðe mildheort and arfæst 
331.54.21: and we him syndon swyðe leofe gyf we tela wyllað 
331.54.22: and we hine magon swyðe hraðe us to mildheortnesse gecigan mid godum dædum 
331.54.23: þeah ðe we hine ær mid synnum abulgan 
331.54.24: he wile sona onfon þa soðan hreowe and bote 
331.54.25: ac hwæþere ne sceolan we beon to swyðe balde on unrihtwisnesse 
331.54.26: and ure synna mid synnum ne ecan 
331.54.27: and þæt þencan and cweþan þæt we magon ures lichoman lust fremman þa hwile þe we on geogoðe syn and þonne on elde we doð soðe hreowe 
331.54.28: hwæt mæg beon mare dysignes þonne æni mann þis on his mode geþence 
331.54.29: hwylc mann is þe wite þone ytemestan dæg his lifes 
331.54.30: forðam ealle menn no on elde ne gewitað 
331.54.31: ac on mistlicum hadum hi of worulde gewitað 
331.54.32: and þonne on domesdæge hi beoð gemette on þæm ylcan geearnungum þe hi ær on eorðan geearnedon and ðær for eallum riht agyldað 

331.54.33: men ða leofestan us is mycel neodþearf ymbe þæt to þencene þeah ðe we butan synnum beon ne magon þæt we næfre ne syn bescyrede þæs ecan rices 
331.54.34: forðam on domesdæge þis mennisce cynn byð on twa tosceaden beforan drihtnes þrymsetle 
331.54.35: oþer dæl on þam swyðran healfe oþer on þa wynstran 
331.54.36: beoð þonne cristes þa gecorenan him on þa swyðran healfe 
331.54.37: ðæt beoð heahfæderas and witegan and þa apostolas and þa martiras and eall se halga heap se ðe god ariht geheold 
331.54.38: and hi þonne swyðe bliðe beoð 
331.54.39: witon þæt hi habbað þone awyrgedan gast oferswiþed 
331.54.40: and heom beoð heora synna forgyfene 
331.54.41: and hi geseoð þone wynsumlican scyppend mid englum and mid heahenglum 
331.54.42: swylce we eac agan myccle þearfe þæt we moton on heora gemanan beon 
331.54.43: swylce eac beoð þa synfullan asceadene on þa wynstran healfe þæs scyppendes 
331.54.44: þær beoð þonne gemette þa woruldstrangan kynegas and caseras 
331.54.45: and woruldwelige gitseras and unrihthæmede 
331.54.46: and struderas and werigcwedende 
331.54.47: and oferdrinceras and ða æfestegan 
331.54.48: and ða leasan hi sylfe þær ongytað 
331.54.49: hwæt willað hi þonne cweþan beforan þam reþan deman þonne hi nabbað naht to syllanne buton heora lichoman and sawle 

331.54.50: ac uton we nu forðan menn þa leofestan urne drihten lufian and his bebodu healdan eallum mægne 
331.54.51: drihten us mid swa micelre lufan lufað 
331.54.52: he wilnað þæt we ealle hale syn and gesunde and to þære soþan hreowe gecyrran and to þam soðan andgyte his godcundnesse 
331.54.53: drihten wile þæt ure lif sy gestaðelod on clænnesse and on soðfæstnesse 
331.54.54: nele he þæt se synfulla mann on his synnum þurhwunige and æfter his deaðe on ecum witum swelte 
331.54.55: ac he wyle þæt we in þisse lænan tide geearnien þæt we on ecnesse ne forwurðan 
331.54.56: se arfæsta drihten and se mildheorta ne bideð he æt us gold ne seolfor ne nænig woruldgestreon 
331.54.57: ac he wilnað þæt we clænsien ure sawla and urne lichaman þæt we magon heo him swa clæne agyfan swa he hi us ær clæne befæste 

331.54.58: menn þa leofestan we sceolon mid monigfealdum godum ure sawla clænsian 
331.54.59: mid fæstenum and mid ælmesdædum and mid clænum gebedum 
331.54.60: forðon se monn se þe gelomlice to drihtne clypað þonne breceð he deofles mægn and his costunge him fram aflemeð 
331.54.61: se monn þe cleopað him to mid inweardre heortan ne sceal he no wenan þæt god his bene nelle gehyran 
331.54.62: swa we be ðon magon ongytan þæt ionas se witega wæs under sæs yðum in hwæles hryfe 
331.54.63: and he ða to drihtne cleopode and he sona his stefne gehyrde 
331.54.64: forðon se monn se ðe hine gelomlice to gode gebiddeð þonne aflemeð he fram him ealles feondes costunga 
331.54.65: swa se mann se ðe his ælmessan singallice for gode syleð þonne adwæsceð he his synna swa mon mid wætere adwæsceð byrnende fyr 

331.54.66: uton we nu forðon men þa leofestan neoman sibbe and lufe us betweonan 
331.54.67: forðon on þam bið eall drihtnes bebod mæst 
331.54.68: uton neoman clænnesse and gesceadwisnesse ealra godra weorca 
331.54.69: forðon butan þæm ne mæg næni man gode lician 
331.54.70: and don we singallice godes willan þæt we þæt geearnigen þæt we motan beon bliðe beforan þam heahsetle þæs ecan cyninges 
331.54.71: þær bið þusend þusenda engla werodes þonne þær ætstondað ealle gesceafta mid ege and mid fyrhtum 
331.54.72: and þær bið þæt gescead gecorenra and synfulra 
331.54.73: ðær bið þæt mæste gemot and ælces monnes dæda þær beoð forð gelædde 
331.54.74: forðon nu arisan we fram þæm slæpe and symle se ytemesta dæg wunige on urum heortum 
331.54.75: forðon us bið ungewiss hwæðer we moton fram ærnemergen þonne we arisað lyfian oð æfen 
331.54.76: and swa bið eac uncuð on þære nihte fyrste hu us gelimpe 
331.54.77: swa ure drihten on his godspelle be ðon cwæð 
331.54.78: waciað on ælce tid and eow gebiddað forðon þe ge nytan þa tid hwænne drihten cymð to demanne þisne middaneard hwæðre hit bið þe on æfen þe on middeniht þe on hancred þe on morgen 
331.54.79: ac beoð gearwe forðon on þa tid þe ge ne wenað se mannes sunu cymð 
331.54.80: ac behealdað eow þy læs þe he eow slæpende gemete 
331.54.81: and ne gehefegiað eowre heortan on oferfylle and on oferdrynce and fram eallum þissum yfelum ahebbað eow 
331.54.82: forðon se monn se ðe nyle þus don he hine æteoweð eft in þæm dome scyldine þonne he byð gesewen ure hælend on his þæm domsetle to wræce þam synfullan mannum and to edleane þam gecorenum 

331.54.83: on þæm dæge byð mycel ege æteowed eallum manncynne þonne hi sceawiað godes sunu cumendne on heofones wolcnum on micclum mægne and on þæm mestan godes þrymme 
331.54.84: ðonne beforan him eall eorðe forbyrneð and eall heofona mægn stranglice beoð onstyred on þæm micclan domesdæge 
331.54.85: and seo rod þe ure drihten on þrowode seo byð æteowed ofer cristes gesyhðe and se þyrnena helm on his heafde 
331.54.86: and þa wunda on his sidan ealle þa beoð opene 
331.54.87: and þonne cweð se eca dema 
331.54.88: eala þu mon geherstu ic þe gescop and geworhte mid minum handum of eorðan lame 
331.54.89: and ic ageat minne gast on þine þa eorðlican limu 
331.54.90: and ic þe gestaðelode betweox neorxnawonges wiste 
331.54.91: and ðeahhwæðre þu wære me herwiende 
331.54.92: and ic eac on fæmnan bosme astah and ðær wæs lichomlice acenned for ðinre hælo to þan þæt ðu me gelic wære geworden 
331.54.93: ic wæs mid claðum bewunden and on binne geseted 
331.54.94: and monig mislic sar for ðe geþrowode to þam þæt ic wolde ða synne þine forlornesse adwæscan 
331.54.95: ic wolde þæt ic ðe fram ecum deaðe alysde 
331.54.96: geseoh nu minra nægla swæð þe ic on rode for ðe genæglad wæs 
331.54.97: her ðu miht geseon þa wunda þe ic gewundod wæs and ða rode on þære ic for ðe deað geþrowode 
331.54.98: ic onfeng eall middaneardlicu sar to þan þæt ðu wære on heofonum wlitig 
331.54.99: forhwon forlure ðu þæt ic for ðe geþrowode 
331.54.100: oððe forhwan onfenge ðu unþancwyrðlice þa gife þinre alysnysse 
331.54.101: ne ofþinceð me nu no min þrowung 
331.54.102: ac agyf me þin lif forðon ic min for ðe gesealde 
331.54.103: and agyf me þine sawle þa ðu mid synnum acwealdest and gewemdest mid fyrenlustum 
331.54.104: oððe forhwan ahenge þu me mid þære hefegan rode þinra hleahtra 
331.54.105: miccle hefigre me is seo rod þinra synna þonne me si seo ðe ic lichamlice mid wyllan gestah and for ðe deað on geþrowode 

331.54.106: æfter þissum wordum he þonne anra gehwylcum demeð swa he þonne wile 
331.54.107: hwær magon hi þonne on þam dæge behydan þa arleasan men and ða synfullan þa ðe nu forhicgað þa godes domas 
331.54.108: hwæt þam monnum þonne bið sarlice gegan þa ðe on þisse andweardan worulde him sylfum geearniað ece witu and ða heardan eardungstowe mid deoflum on helle 
331.54.109: forðon seo hæftnes heora yrmða wunað in worulda woruld 
331.54.110: ne þær on þæm egeslicum witum nænig stefn bið gehyred buton heof and wop 
331.54.111: and næni rest gemeted buton biter attor and ece cwealm 
331.54.112: ac uton we biddan urne drihten þone mildheortan scyppend þæt he ontyne þa earan ure heortan to his þære halgan lare to onfonne and heo syðþan fæstlice on urum heortum wunige 
331.54.113: and we oðre men georne monigen to þam godcundan gode þæt we þonne ætgædere magon mid þæm godum to þæm heofonlican eþle becuman 
331.54.114: þær we urne drihten ecelice geseon moton 
331.54.115: þær us næfre syðþan hunger ne þurst wyrdeð ne adl ne æce ne deofles costung ne leofra gedal ne sorh ne gewinn ne þeostru 
331.54.116: ac þær beoð lof singende engla þreatas and swete symbelnes ealra haligra 
331.54.117: and ðær þonne anra gehwylc swa miccle bliðra bið swa he ær mare gewinn and earfoðu her on worulde aræfnede for godes naman 
331.54.118: ðonne ealra cyninga cyning ealle þa þe he gearwe gemeteð he mid him gelædeð on his þæt ece rice 

331.54.119: men ða leofestan geþence we geornlice and smealice forhwan eall cristen folc þas andweardan tide þus lufiað and weorþiað and ða halgan reliquias folgiað 
331.54.120: butan tweon swutolice we magon þurh þa halgan gesamnunga ongytan þa ecan reste 
331.54.121: and æghwylcum þara bið heofona rices duru ongean untyned þe þa halgan reliquias mid geleafan folgiað 
331.54.122: we þonne men ða leofestan habbað nedþearfe þæt we mid soðre lufan and mid rihtan geleafan þa gesamnunge secan and ða godcundan lare gehyran 
331.54.123: þonne beo we æt urum ytemestan dæge gewlitegode mid godes þæm gecorenum 
331.54.124: be þæm is awriten and ðus cweden 
331.54.125: ðær þa soðfæstan men scinað and leohtað swa beorhte swa sunne on þæs uplican fæder rice 
331.54.126: ac uton nu tilian men þa leofestan geornlice þa hwile þe we magon þæt we motan blissian mid urum drihtne and mid eallum his halgum þær is ece bliss and ece rest a in ealra worulda woruld a butan ende amen
