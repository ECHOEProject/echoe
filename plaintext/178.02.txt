178.2.1: her onginneð þæt gewrit þe com of heofonum to hierusalem 
178.2.2: and biforan þam geate þe is gecweden effrem on eorðan afeoll 
178.2.3: ðis gewrit soðlice in ðam halgan burh hierusalem of heofenum dun afeal and to sancte petres sepulchrum becom 
178.2.4: ðis is þæt ðridde gewrit þæt wæs asend of heofonum 
178.2.5: and æfter ðisson ne cymeð nefre nan oðer 
178.2.6: ge bisceopes and mæssepreostas cyðað gelomlice ðis gewrit eallum folce 
178.2.7: and sendað fram anre burhscipe to oðre 
178.2.8: and hi biddon and geornlice læron þæt hi gelyfon þæt hit nis afunden fram ænigan eorðlice men ac gewriten fram handum ðes haligan hælendes
