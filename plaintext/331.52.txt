de letania maiore 

331.52.1: leofan men ðas dagas synd gehaten letanie 
331.52.2: þæt synd gebeddagas 
331.52.3: on þissum dagum we sceolon biddan ure eorðlicra wæstma genihtsumnysse and us sylfum gesundfulnysse 
331.52.4: and þæt gyt mare is ure synna forgyfennesse 
331.52.5: and we rædað on bocum þæt se gewuna þissa gangdaga wurde aræred on þone timan þe gelamp on anre burhscire þe uigenna is genamod 
331.52.6: þæt wearð mycel eorðstyrung 
331.52.7: and feollon gehalgode godes cyricean and manega hus hruran 
331.52.8: and comon wilde deor and tosliton and abiton ealles to fela þurh godes yrre 
331.52.9: and ðæs cyninges botl wearð mid heofonlicum fyre forbærned 
331.52.10: and fela ungelimpa gewearð for folces synnan 
331.52.11: and þa bead se biscop mamertus þreora daga fæsten 
331.52.12: and þæt man halidom sceolde wyrðlice styrien and mid ælmeslacum georne god gladian 
331.52.13: and þæt manna gehwylc unsceodum fotum þam halidome sceolde eadmodlice fylian 
331.52.14: and ealle to criste geornlice clypian 
331.52.15: and sona swa þæt gedon wæs þa wæs swutele gesyne þæt heom god syðþan uðe lisse and miltse 
331.52.16: ne wearð næfre syððan þanonforð eft swylc gedrecednys innan þam lande 
331.52.17: ac wearð þæt to bysne wide and side þæt man þanonforð þa gangdagas on gewunan hæfde 

331.52.18: leofan men nu bidde ic and lære ælcne cristene man þæt he þissa þreora daga fæsten rihtlice gehealde ælce dæge to nones and to anes mæles butan hwa hit for ylde oððe for iuguðe oððe for unhæle gelæstan ne mæge 
331.52.19: gyf þonne þissa þreora þinga ænig hwylcne man lette þæt hine to þam fæstene ne onhagie þonne is hit alefed þæt he mot mete þicgan on þa gerad þe he his ælmessan sylle and nænne oðerne man him to ne spanne to ðam gereordum butan þær hwylc oðer mettrum man sy þe þæt fæsten aberan ne mæge 
331.52.20: gyf þonne se hala mid þam unhalan þone mete þigeð þonne wurþað hig begen þurh þæs mettruman unmihta beswicene 
331.52.21: and nis nanum men alefed for nanre neode þis fæsten to abrecane ne mid ælmessan to alysenne butan hine þæra þreora þinga hwylc forwyrne yld oððe iuguð oððe unhæl 

331.52.22: leofan men se halga apostol iacobus mynegode us ealle freondlice on þam pistole þe man ær rædde 
331.52.23: and us eac luflice lærde þæt we georne began ure agene þearfe 
331.52.24: he cwæð confitemini alter utrum peccata uestra 
331.52.25: ðæt is on englisc anddettað georne he cwæð eowre synna eow betweonan 
331.52.26: and ælc for oðerne he cwæð gebidde georne þæt eow god ælmihtig gesundfulnysse geunne 
331.52.27: forðam ic secge eow þæt rihtwises mannes gebedu fremiað swyðe mycclum 
331.52.28: elias on ealddagum se halga wæs man eall swa we synd 
331.52.29: and he abæd þeah æt gode þæt he forwyrnde þam wiðerweardan folce þe he mid wunode feorðe healf gear ælces renes 
331.52.30: and he abæd eft syðþan æt þam soðan gode þæt he renas forgeaf and eorðlice wæstmas 
331.52.31: ðær is swutol þæt haliges mannes gebedu magan mycles wealdan 
331.52.32: se halga apostol sæde eac on þissum pistole þæt gyf hwylc man gehwyrfeð oþerne fram gedwylde þæt is fram deofles larum he alyst witodlice his sawle fram deaðe and fela synna fordiligað 
331.52.33: ac leofan men understandað eow sylfe swyðe georne þæt ge rihtlice and wærlice þæt gehealdon þæt eow mæst þearf is to gehealdenne 
331.52.34: þæt is rihtne cristendom and rihtne geleafan on god ælmihtine þe ealle þing gescop and geworhte 

331.52.35: leofan men se halga godspellere lucas rehte on þissum godspelle þe man nu ær rædde hu ure drihten hwilum to his leornicnihtum spræc 
331.52.36: he cwæð gyf besorh freond cymð to oðres huse on midre nihte and he næfð him to donne swa swa he wolde 
331.52.37: þonne gæð he and biddeð his neahgebur þæt he him alæne hlaf for ðære neode his cuman þe him to com 
331.52.38: ðonne andwyrdeð se oðer þær wiðinnan and cweð þæt he ne mæge arisan 
331.52.39: segð þæt his dyre synd fæste and his cild on heora reste 
331.52.40: ac bideð þæt he hine na ne swence 
331.52.41: gyf he þonne se oþer forðon georne clypað and cnucað þonne æt nehstan arist he forðam onhrope and getiþað þæs þe him neod bið 
331.52.42: be þissere bysne ure drihten lærð us hwæt we don sceolon 
331.52.43: ðæt is þonne þæt we sceolon clypian and biddan georne gelome god ælmihtig þæt he ure neoda gecnawe and ure læsta gebete 
331.52.44: and gyf we ægðer ge mid wordum ge mid weorcum rihtlice and geornlice clypiað þonne wile he ure bena sona gehyran 
331.52.45: ac butan seo heorte rihtlice hycge and weorc þam wordum fylstan ne behealdeð þa word naht swyðe mycel 
331.52.46: swa he sylf on his godspelle swutollice sæde and ðus cwæð 
331.52.47: non omnis qui dicit domine domine 
331.52.48: ðæt is on englisc ne færð na ælc þæra into heofonum þe gelome clypað to criste 
331.52.49: ac se ðe wyrceð godes willan and on þam þurhwunað forð oð ende se witodlice færð on an to heofonum 
331.52.50: leofan men utan clypian georne oft and gelome and earnian georne þæt god ælmihtig ure bena gehyran wylle 
331.52.51: uton gelomlice geþencan þone egeslican dom þe we ealle to sceolon 
331.52.52: and utan understandan þæt hit þærto nealæcð georne 
331.52.53: ðeos woruld is eall wyrse þonne heo wæs and dæghwamlice heo yfelað swyðe 
331.52.54: forðam þe hit sceal gewyrðan þæt gefyrn awriten wæs 
331.52.55: ueniet tempus quale non fuit 
331.52.56: ðæt is on englisc swa egeslic tima cymð to mannum swa næfre ær on worulde ne gewearð 
331.52.57: þæt is on antecristes timan 
331.52.58: and se tima wyrð on worulde sorhful and egesfull 
331.52.59: forðam þe antecrist bið sylf deoful and ðeah mennisc man geboren 

331.52.60: leofan men her segð eac on þyssum bocum be ðære mycclan arwyrðnysse ures scyppendes hu eadmodlice he ymb mancynn hogode 
331.52.61: forðam þe he hit gescop and geworhte 
331.52.62: and he wolde þæt seo gesceaft ongeate heora scyppend 
331.52.63: se ðe he to life gescop on neorxnawanges gefean þær he hi undeadlice gestaðelode 
331.52.64: and þeahhwæþre þurh deofles beswicenysse gelamp þæt seo mennisce gesceaft of þam undeadlican life hider nyðer gefeoll 
331.52.65: and her on sare and on yrmðum syðþan cendon and leofedon 
331.52.66: and syðþan æfter heora life to helle and to ecum deaþe bedrifene wurdon 
331.52.67: oðþæt se wuldorlica cyning se ðe is ealles middaneardes alesend onbærnde mænig gastlic leohtfæt mancynn to onlihtenne 
331.52.68: to þon þæt nænig deofla dimnes ne bysgode þam ðe þæt leoht þære soðfæstnysse ongytan woldan 

331.52.69: gehyrað nu broðor mine hwylc þa leohtfatu wæron þe he ure drihten onbærnde on þissum middanearde mancynne to onlihtanne 
331.52.70: þæt wæron ærest heahfæderas þa wæron gefylde to godum weorcum and ðeawum þa þe godes folc lærdon geond þysne middaneard 
331.52.71: þanon onwocon witegan þa wæron gelærde þurh þone halgan gast 
331.52.72: and nalæs þæt an þæt hy ðæs folces synna þreadan ac eac swylce þone cristes tocyme ures hælendes bodedon 
331.52.73: and æfter þon wæron halige apostolas þa ðe he sylfa geceas to þam þæt he ure drihten hi of his sylfes muðe lærde 
331.52.74: and geond þysne middaneard sende to hælenne and to lærenne eall mancynn and him ðus to cwæð 
331.52.75: euntes docete omnes gentes 
331.52.76: farað nu and lærað geond ealle þeoda hwæt sy to lufianne oððe to forganne 
331.52.77: hu men magon þa ecan witu befleon and ða ecan reste geearnian heora sawlum on heofonan rices gefean 
331.52.78: and ða æfter þissum wæron soðlice gesette halige biscopas and mæssepreostas and diaconas and mænige leorneras geond godes cyrcan 
331.52.79: þæt we nu magon ongitan and witan hwæt us is to donne oððe to forganne 
331.52.80: gehyrað nu broðor mine and ongytað forhwan us is gesett þeos tid to weorðianne fram þam halgan easterdæge oþ ðas tid þe we nu weorþiað 
331.52.81: se ælmihtiga godes sunu se ðe undeadlice æt þære tide aras and oft and gelome mid his gingrum wæs and hine sylfne undeadlice æteowde 
331.52.82: and he eadmodlice and geornlice lærde ealle þeoda to heofonan rices gefean 
331.52.83: ðonne syndon us þas þry dagas to þon gesett þæt we sceolon þa halgan cristes þegnas onhyrgan 
331.52.84: þæt is þæt we sceolon ure woruldweorc forgan fram underne oð non 
331.52.85: and to godes cyrcan cuman and for urum synnum þingian 
331.52.86: and forð mid þam halgum reliquium gan swa us þa halgan apostolas mynegodon to weorþianne urne hælend and his þa halgan 
331.52.87: forðon þe nu ealle eorðan wæstmas growende syndon and blowende geopenade 
331.52.88: and þæt ure bileofan syn growende and gebletsode to hælo ures lichoman and sawle 
331.52.89: forðon us is þeos tid geset ure sawla hælo on to biddanne 
331.52.90: and ures lichoman bletsunge and ura woruldgoda genihtsumnesse mid godcunda lofsange 
331.52.91: and mid þam halgum reliquium we sculon beon gode lof secgende and cristes rodetacen forð beran and his þa halige godspell and oðre halignessa 
331.52.92: mid þam we sceolon bletsian ure þa eorðlican speda 
331.52.93: þæt synd æceras and wudu and ure ceap and eall þa þing þe us god forgyfen hafað to brucanne þe we bi leofian sceolon 
331.52.94: utan we nu symle þang secgan urum scyppende his miltsa 
331.52.95: forðon þe he us swyðe lufode ofer ealle oðre gesceafta þa ðe nu under heofonas hwolfe syndon 
331.52.96: he sylfa us gescop and us geworhte þa we næron 
331.52.97: and he us gesohte þa we forwordene wæron 
331.52.98: and he us abohte of hellewitum and of deofles anwealde mid his ðy deorwyrðan blode 
331.52.99: and us opene gedyde þone weg to heofonan rice 
331.52.100: forðon we sculon ongytan hu mycel nydþearf us wæs his tocymes to ure alysednysse 
331.52.101: la hwylc mihte beon hefigre nydþearf þonne se eca deað and hwylc mihta beon hefigre wite 
331.52.102: þonne ure synna and se hearda þeowdom oððe hwylc mihte beon mare wælgrimnes 
331.52.103: þonne ure hæftnydnes on helleþeostrum 
331.52.104: cuðlice we wæron on deofles þeowdome 
331.52.105: and we wæron on helle fæðmum 
331.52.106: of þæm he us alysde 
331.52.107: and us lif sealde on heofonan rice 

331.52.108: leofan men se witega cwæð be heofona rice and be hellewite þæt næfre nære gemet on heofonan rices wuldre swa mycel unwynsumnes on ænigum laðe 
331.52.109: oððe on hungre oððe on þurste 
331.52.110: oððe on cele oððe on hæte 
331.52.111: oððe on æce oððe on alde 
331.52.112: oððe on ænigum laðum gewinne 
331.52.113: þæt wære swa mycel swa anes lytles fugeles sweg 
331.52.114: swylce he cwæð se ylca witega þæt næfre nære on hellewitum swa mycel wynsumnes swa anes lytles fugeles sweg 
331.52.115: wa þam þe geearnoð hellewite 
331.52.116: ðær is ece bryne grimme gemencged 
331.52.117: and þær is ece gryre 
331.52.118: þær is granung and wanung and a singol heaf 
331.52.119: þær is ealra yrmða gehwylc and ealra deofla geþring 
331.52.120: wa ðam þe þær sceal wunian on wite 
331.52.121: betere him wære þæt he man nære æfre geworden þonne he gewurde 
331.52.122: forðam nis se man on life þe areccan mæge ealle þa yrmða þe se gebidan sceal se ðe on þa wite ealles bereoseð 
331.52.123: and hit is ealles þe wyrse þe his ænig ende ne cymð æfre to worulde 
331.52.124: eala leofan menn uton don swa us mycell þearf is 
331.52.125: beorgan us georne wið þone egsan 
331.52.126: and helpan ure sylfra þa hwile þe we magon and motan þe læs we forweorðan þonne we læst wenan 
331.52.127: ac utan lufian god ofer ealle oðre þing 
331.52.128: and his willan a wyrcan swa we geornost magon 
331.52.129: þonne geleanað he hit us swa us leofast bið þonne we æfre betst beþurfon 

331.52.130: him symle sy lof and wuldor in ealra worulda woruld a butan ende amen
