feria ii in letania maiore 

56.31.1: men ða leofestan us is swiðe mycel neodþearf þæt we gehyron sumne dæl þyses gastlican gewrites þe her us forð læreð and manað to ure sauwle þearfe 
56.31.2: and eac swylce be þissere tide halignysse and be hyre arwurðnysse 
56.31.3: þe gecweden is on gastlicum gewritum þurh gastlice leorneras to þære gastlican gesetnysse þæt þas þry dagas wæron letanias genemned 
56.31.4: us is þonne swyðe mycel neodþearf on æghwilce tide þæt we habban mid us soðe sibbe and soðe eadmodnysse 
56.31.5: and þæt we geearnian mid urum hlutrum gebedum and mid ure heortan onbryrdnysse and mid urum clænum ælmæssum þurh þa cyrican gesetnysse þæt we gemunon and geþencan swa us mynegiað ure lareowas þæt we biddan þone ælmihtigan drihten his mildheortnyse and gebletfæstnysse lifes ge gastlices ge woruldlices 
56.31.6: uton we þonne geþencan þæt we mihton drihtene mid þære mæstan eadmodnysse on þas þry dagas geornfullice gehyran for ðissa daga wurðunge þæt he ure bene gehyre 
56.31.7: uton we þonne on þysum dagum and on æghwylce tide gehyran and gehealdan his beboda 
56.31.8: uton gehyron æt ærestan and geþencan þone cwyde þe he sylf on his godspelle cwæð and us bebead ofer eallne middaneard 
56.31.9: þam þe his beboda healdan wyllað 
56.31.10: þæt ure æghwilc gecume æt urum reliquium 
56.31.11: and nænig mann hine sylfne þanon ne sceade fram þam halgan gemote drihtenes sylfes 
56.31.12: and þam cyriclicum þe on ymbhwyrfte bið cristes reliquium halgum and þam apostolicum and þær halgan cristes rode 
56.31.13: swa we leornodon and getreowlice we magon ongytan þæt se ælmihtiga drihten swyðe mænigfealdlice gyfe gemetfæstum mannum dyde hyder on þisne middaneard 
56.31.14: and forðan he swa dyde forþan he wolde þæt þeos clæne gesceaft ongeate hyre þone ecean scyppend 
56.31.15: forðan ðe he manncynne onlyhte mænige gastlice leohtfatu and sende hider on þisne middaneard 
56.31.16: forþan þe he wold gedwæscean þa deorcnysse and synne þysternysse fram urum heortum acyrran þurh his þa halgan and þa gastlican leohtfata 
56.31.17: and swylce he eac wolde þæt we þises leohtes andweardnysse geearnodon 
56.31.18: þæt we moston on þære toweardnysse mid him sylfum and mid his þam halgum þæs ecan leohtes brucan 
56.31.19: mænig mann on þysum gemote wunað and hwæðere næfð þæt andgyt hwæt þa gastlican leoht synd 
56.31.20: buton hit hym seo gastlicenysse gerece swa hit gecweden ys on gastlicum gewritum þurh gastlice leorneras þæt us wæron to leohtfatum gesette 
56.31.21: ure heahfæderas abel enoch melchisedech noe abraham isaac iacob hi us onlihton and onstealdon eallra goddra weorca bysne 
56.31.22: eac þa wæron godes þa halgan witegan þa þurh þone halgan gast us lærdon 
56.31.23: and hi us foresædon ealles þises middaneardes fægernyssa and his wynsumnyssa 
56.31.24: and eac swylce hy us foresædon ealles þises middaneardes þysternyssa and his synna and his gedwolan 
56.31.25: and þonne nalæs þæt an þæt hi us tacnodon ymbe þyses andweardan middaneardes gesetnysse 
56.31.26: ac hi us eac sædon ymbe þa toweardnysse þæs forhtigendan domes and þone soðfæstan deman crist sylf 
56.31.27: and eac þyssere tacnunge hy eac getacnodon hwylc wæs heofona rice to geearnienne and hwylc wæs hellewite to befleonne 
56.31.28: on þa gelicnysse þe se witega cwæð be heofona rice and be helle þæt næfre nære on heofona rices wuldre swa micel unwynsumes on ænigum laðe 
56.31.29: oððe on hungre oððe on þurste 
56.31.30: oððe on cyle oððe on hætan 
56.31.31: oððe on adle oððe on ænigum laðe gewinne 
56.31.32: þæt wære swa mycel swa anes lytles fugeles sweg 
56.31.33: swylce he cwæð se ylca witega þæt nære næfre on hellewyte swa mycel wynsumes swa anes fugeles sweg 
56.31.34: swylce eac wæron us to leohtfatum gesette cristes apostolas 
56.31.35: þa wæron onsende on þysne middaneard geond myssendlice þeoda þurh crist sylfne 
56.31.36: forði þonne þæt hi sceolon menn trymman and læran and fullian and deofolscype nyðerian 
56.31.37: and þeo deofolican lustas adwescan and drihtenes rice bredan 
56.31.38: swa swa crist sylf to his apostolum sprecende wæs and þus cwæð 
56.31.39: ic eow sende on þeode and on oðre on þa gelicnysse æfter eorðlice wisan þe eorðlican ic onsende an sceap on wulfa heap 
56.31.40: ne wæron hy swa fræcne onsended 
56.31.41: forðon ðe hi hæfdon þæt gastlice mægen and þone heofonlican fultum cristes sylfes 
56.31.42: þone fultum hi geearnedon þurh þæt þe hy wæron cristes willan wyrcende 
56.31.43: forþan hi sittað on heofonan rices wuldre on þa swyðran healfe godfæder 
56.31.44: þar hy him nænig lað ne ondrædað ne hunger ne þurst ne adle ne deofles costunga 
56.31.45: ne him þar ne byð næfre nanes godes wana 
56.31.46: ne him næfre þæs lifes and þære wynsum nysse nænig ende ne geyrnð 
56.31.47: broþor mine hwæt we leornodon þæt cristes apostolas wæron mennisce menn on þa gelicnysse þe we syndon 
56.31.48: we þonne leornodon þæt hi geearnodon þæt rice and þæt setl þæt hi on wuniað on heofonanrices wuldre mid þam ylcum dædum þe us alyfede syndon gyf we us sylfe ne forgymleasiað godra dæda 
56.31.49: ne synd we næfre to ofermode on urum yfelum dædum 
56.31.50: ac we hraðe to þære anddetnysse and to þære bote gecyrron 
56.31.51: micel gegearnung þæt is menniscum menn þæt he geearnige mid godum dædum to þam ælmihtigan drihtene þurh his anes mægen mid godes fultume 
56.31.52: þæt he mæge mid þi apostolican hade ealle þa heorde þe he gode healdan sceal and fram hellewite generian 
56.31.53: swa cristes apostolas dydon 
56.31.54: and habbað geearnod þæt heora æghwylc an mæg eallum manncynne geþingian þæt him se hælend forgyfeð ealle heora synna 
56.31.55: uton we þonne broðor mine on þas halgan tide geornfullice and eadmodlice biddan þone ælmihtigan drihten his mildheortnysse 
56.31.56: and we eac biddan þa halgan apostolas þa wæron on gebyrdon æfter lichamlicre wisan us gelice 
56.31.57: and hi us wæron to leohtfatum gesette 
56.31.58: þæt hy syndon ure þingeras to þam lyfgendan gode 
56.31.59: þæt we beon gehælede of urum synnum for drihtenes mildheortnysse 
56.31.60: and for bene þæra haligra apostola 
56.31.61: and for weorðunga þissere halgan tide 
56.31.62: swylce eac us syndon to lareowum gesette ure þa gehalgodon bisceopas þa us trymmað and lærað to þam ecean rice gyf we him mid eadmodnysse þeowian willað 
56.31.63: and eac swylce us syndon to lareowum gesette ure mæssepreostas þa beoð dæghwamlice anwearde toforan urum eagum 
56.31.64: þa us cænnað þurh þone halgan gast and trymmað to þam ecean life 
56.31.65: menig mann nafað þæt andgyt hwæt seo acennednysse sy 
56.31.66: we beoð acenned lichamlice þurh fæder and þurh modor hider on þisne middangeard 
56.31.67: æfter þam we beoð acennede gastlice be lichamlice and be eorðlice 
56.31.68: þreo acennednyssa beoð gastlice 
56.31.69: swa drihten sylf on hys godspelle sprecende wæs and þus cwæð 
56.31.70: ne mæg se eorðlica mann on ecnysse mid me wunian on minum rice butan he sy acenned þurh wæter and þurh haligne gast 
56.31.71: æghwylc þæra þe on me gelyfað and gefullod bið þurh wæter and fæder namann and on sunu and on þone halgan gast se bið on ecnysse mid me gehæled 
56.31.72: þæt is seo forme accennednyss gastlicu þonne we onfoð fullwihte æt ure mæssepreosta þenunge 
56.31.73: þonne is seo æftre accennednyss gastlicu syððan se mann gewintrod bið and þæt geseald hafað þæt he andette his scrifte ealle his synna 
56.31.74: syððan he bið godes þeowa geciged 
56.31.75: forþan ðe se soða anddetnyss is æt frymðe eallra gastlicra læcedoma 
56.31.76: þonne is seo þridde accennednyss æfter þam soðan andetnyssa syððan se mann gewintrad bið and he hine sylfne gemedmað þurh þa soðan anddetnysse 
56.31.77: and þurh his scriftes fultum þæt he bið andfenge drihtenes sylfes lichaman and his blode 
56.31.78: þonne he hafað fullice ealle þa hadas þe hine to þan ece life gelædað 
56.31.79: he geanlicode his lichaman heofonlican hlafe 
56.31.80: and he onlihte his blod wine on lifes calice 
56.31.81: and us þa onsende hider on þisne middaneard to earnan and to wedde eces lifes 
56.31.82: and swylce us wæron to leohtfatum gesette and to lareowum þa halgan godspelleras 
56.31.83: þurh þæara lare we magon witan hwæt þe lufian sceolon and hwæt we gedon sceolon 
56.31.84: and hwæt we forgyfan sceolon and mid hwylcum dædum we magon hellewite befleon 
56.31.85: swa he drihten sylfa on his godspelle sprecende wæs be þyssere halgan tide and be his godspellerum and þus cwæð 
56.31.86: forþan ic þas gangdagas sette forþan þe ic wille þæt mine leorneras and mine godspelleras secgon and bryman þæt ece lif 
56.31.87: and ic wille æghwylcum gemiltsian þe ðas halgan tide weorðiað and on me gelyfað and mid godspellerum gelyfan willað 
56.31.88: forþon broðor mine on þas andweardan tide beoð reliquias haligra manna upp ahafene 
56.31.89: and forð aborene mid leofsange and mid gastlicum sange on sunderlice stowa gehwilce swa hwær swa hi gesette beoð 
56.31.90: us is þonne swyðe mycel nydþearf þæt we mid swiðe mycelre eadmodnysse urum reliquium folgion and þam þeowian 
56.31.91: swa we magon ongytan be sumre eorðlicre bysne gyf menn him beoð betweonan ungesibsume him þone secað sona æfter menniscre wisan gemotstowe and dom gedemed bið and gesibbað hi 
56.31.92: syððan sceal ælc mann gelæstan swa se dema him bebeodað and þæt gemot bið þonne eall gesybod 
56.31.93: be þære bysne we magon ongytan þas halgan tide forþan ðe þas dagas syndon ure gemotdagas gastlicra gemota 
56.31.94: þonne bið ure gastlice gemotstow on ymbhwyrfte ure reliquia 
56.31.95: swa on cyrican swa butan swa on hwylcre stowe hi on gesette beoð 
56.31.96: wite þonne þæt æghwylc mann þæra þe on þysum gemote wunige and þis godspell gehyreð þæt he eac þam georne mid eadmedum ontyne þære heortan earan to manunge þyses godspelles and to þæs gastlican deman cristes sylfes 
56.31.97: his domas he on gastlicum gewritum us gesette and þurh gastlice leorneras us ontynde 
56.31.98: gyf we þas gastlican domas gelæstað willað þe crist sylf on his godspelle beodað on þisse gemotstowe þonne beoð we beforan cristes heahsetle on þam myclan dome þam soðfæstan deman gecorene 
56.31.99: and we þonne motan gehyran þone cwyde þe he sylf cwæð to þam þe he sette on þa swyðran healfe 
56.31.100: cumað ge gebletsode to mines fæder rice þæt eow wæs gegearwod fram middaneardes fruman 
56.31.101: broðor mine us is swyðe mycel nydþearf þæt us þys godspell gerecce mid hwylcum dædum we sceolon þone soðfæstan deman weorðian and him þeowian 
56.31.102: ærest on þysse halgan tide þe nu onweard his and swa hyt gecweden hys on þysum godspelle þæt we sceolon upp ahebban and forð beran ure halgan reliquias ða menn þe synd on endebyrdnysse to gastlicum lofe gelæred 
56.31.103: and þonne on ymbwyrfte þæra gastlicra lofsanga eall folc gewunie 
56.31.104: and nan mann hine sylfne ne sceade fram þam gastlican gemote butan hit seo nyd si þe mare 
56.31.105: and manna gewitnyss þæt he him fylian ne mæge 
56.31.106: forþan ðe ælcum þæra bið heofonan rices duru ontyned þe mid soðan geleafan þam halgum reliquium fyliað 
56.31.107: and ægwylc mann gehealde clænlice his fæsten þas þry dagas oð ða nontide 
56.31.108: and þonne þicge þa mettas þe he hwilon þæt lengtenfæsten þe se hælend on þrowode feowertig daga and feowertig nihta tosomne for eall manncynn 
56.31.109: uton we nu þonne geþencann þæt we swyðe medemlice gedydon þæge tide ge ure fæsten ge ure ælmessan ge ure beda ge ure gelastas for namann þæs ælmihtigan drihtenes 
56.31.110: swa we magon ongytan be þære eorðlican bysene ærest be urum fæstene 
56.31.111: se mann se ðe þenceð þæt he leohte candele gewyrce þæt he mæge lyhtan mid nihtlicum þystrum on his huse þonne gewyrceð he him godne tapor and hine þonne smyreð 
56.31.112: þonne bið se tapor þurh þa smyrunge onbyrhteð and seo þystru his huses bið onlyhted 
56.31.113: nafast ðu æt þam tapore nænig leoht ne nænig recc gyf he þære smyrennysse ne underfehð 
56.31.114: swa þonne bið þam menn þe cwyð þæt he wile fæstan for naman þæs ælmihtigan drihtenes 
56.31.115: and þonne þone ðæg þe he þæt fæsten on fæsteð þonne bið he þone dæg geunrotsod on his mode and on his þeawum wiðerweard 
56.31.116: and þeah þe he þone cristes þearfan geseo þænne þencð he þæt he nelle ægðer ge fæstan ge eac þone þearfan gefrefrian and aretan 
56.31.117: þonne gelimpæð his þeowum oððe freomenn þæt he hafað ymbehydig ærende to þam fæstendan menn 
56.31.118: þonne þencð he ne wille ic mine ærende abeodan ær his hrif geweordod sy 
56.31.119: forþan þe hi oft onleton ongyton þæt hi wæron on heora fæstene geunblissod 
56.31.120: broðor mine ne bið swylc fæsten gecoren þam soðfæstan deman 
56.31.121: ac gyf þu wille medomlice fæstan for naman þæs ælmihtigan drihtenes þonne gefylst þone cwyde þe on þam sealme gecweden wæs 
56.31.122: drihten forlæt me mine scylda on þa gelicnysse þe ic forlet ælcum þeara þe wið me agyltað 
56.31.123: swa sceal se mann don se ðe wyle rihtwislice fæstan for naman þæs ælmihtigan drihtenes 
56.31.124: ac se mann se þe þencð þæt he clænlice fæste and þonne eac þenceð to wrace worda and dæda þam þe ær wið hine agylton 
56.31.125: and ne wile forgyfennysse syllan and mildheortnysse and gefean syllan ealra þeara yfela worda and dæda þe he æfre wið cristes willan geworhte 
56.31.126: þæt fæsten bið gelic þam unsmyredan tapore se þære beorhtnysse ne onfehð 
56.31.127: swa bið þam gaste his weg to þam ecean life gebyrhteð gyf se man bið on his fæstene geunrotsod 
56.31.128: forðon us is swiðe mycel nydþearf þæt we swyðe eadmodlice gedon ure fæsten ge ure ælmyssan ge ure hlutran gebeda ge ure unlustas for naman þæs ælmihtigan drihtenes 
56.31.129: gyf we swa wyllað don and þas lare gelæstan þonne magon we geearnian mid þam lastum forgyfennysse eallra þæra unnytta lasta þe we ær on twelf monðum on unnytt geeodon 
56.31.130: and eac swylce we magon geearnian þurh ure clæne fæsten forgyfnysse ealra þæra unnytt æta þe we on twelf monðum geðigdon 
56.31.131: eac swa we magon geearnian ednywunge and geblettfæstnysse eallra ura eorðlicra gestreona 
56.31.132: and eac þæt mare is eallra ura synna forgyfnysse gyf we ne forgymeleasiað on þas andweardan tide godes þeowdomas 
56.31.133: ealne godes dom we magon þurh þreo þing gehealdan 
56.31.134: þæt syndon þa þreo þing þæt se mann hine gehealde on godum geþohtum and on godum weorcum and on godum dædum 
56.31.135: se goda geþoht onbryrdeð þæs mannes heortan to þam godum dædum 
56.31.136: broðor mine þysum we magon geearnian þæt we moton lyfian and on blisse beon mid drihte sylfum and mid his þam halgum on heofonan rice 
56.31.137: þam þe leofað and rixað on eallra worulda woruld a butan ende amen
