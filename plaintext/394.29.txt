394.29.1: wæs þær in þam sprecenan iglande sum mycel hlæw of eorþan geworht 
394.29.2: þone ylcan hlæw iu geara men bræcon and dulfon for feos þingum 
394.29.3: ða wæs þær on oðre sidan ðæs hlæwes gedolfen swylce mycel seaþ 
394.29.4: on þam seaþe ufan se eadiga wer guðlac him hus and eardungstowe getimbrode 
394.29.5: sona on fruman þæs ðe he þæt ancersetl gesæt þa þohte he þæt he nawðer þara ne wyllenes hrægles ne linenes brucan wolde ac on fellenum gegyrlan þæt he wolde ealle dagas his lifes alifigean and he hit swa forð gelæste 
394.29.6: ælce dæge wæs his ondleofenes swylc gemetegung of þære tide þe he þæt westen ærest eardigean began 

394.29.7: þa gelamp hit sume dæge mid þy þe he þy gewunelican þeowdome his sealmas sang and his gebedum ætfealh þa se ealda feond mancynnes gengde geond þæt græswang swa grymetende leo þæt he his costunga attor wide geond stregde 
394.29.8: mid þy he þa yfelnes mægen and his grimnesse attor teldað þæt he mid þy atre þa menniscean heortan wundað þa semninga swa he of gebendum bogan his costunge ða earhwinnendan stræle on þam mode gefæstnode þæs cristes cempan 
394.29.9: þa he ða se eadiga wer mid þære geætredan stræle gewundod wæs þæs werigan gastes þa wæs his mod þæs eadigan weres swiðe gedrefed on him be þam onginne þe he ongan þæt westen swa ana eardigan 
394.29.10: he ða hine hider and þyder gelomlice on his mode cyrde and he gemunde þa ærran fyrena and leahteras þe he gefremede and geworht hæfde 
394.29.11: he wende þæt he hie næfre gebetan ne meahte 

394.29.12: þa wæs se eadiga wer guðlac mid þære ormodnesse swa gedrefed and gewundod þæt he sylfa nyste hwider he mid his mode cyrran wolde 
394.29.13: þa wæs ðy ðriddan dæge þære æfterfyllendan nihte þæt he ðam wolberendan geþohtum fæste wiðstod and eft swa he witedomlice muðe sang and þus cleopode to gode and cwæð 
394.29.14: min drihten mid minre geswencendnesse ic clypige and cige ac gehyr ðu me and me gefultuma in minum earfeðum 

394.29.15: ða wæs sona æfter ðam þæt his se getrywa fultum him to com sanctus bartholomeus and nalas þæt he him on slæpe ætywde ac he wæccende þone apostol on gelicre fægernesse geseah and sceawode 
394.29.16: and he wæs þa sona se eadiga wer swiðe feonde þæs heofonlican cuman frefrede hine þa sanctus bartholomeus and hine mid wordum trymede and strangode 
394.29.17: and hine het þæt him ne tweode no ac þæt he wære anræde and he him on fultume beon wolde in eallum his earfeðum 
394.29.18: ða he se haliga guðlac þas word gehyrde his þæs getrywan freondes ða wæs he on gæstlicre blisse and heofoncundre gife swiðe gefeode and his geleafan fæste in god sylfne getrymede and fæstnode 
394.29.19: syððan seo tid wæs þæt næfre þæt deoful eft wið hine þære ormodnesse wæpnum on hine sceotode 

394.29.20: swylce eac gelamp on sumne sæl þa he ymb ða drohtunga smeade his lifes hu he mihte gecwemlicost libban ða coman þær semninga tu deoflu of þære lyfte slidan and þa mid cuðlicum wordum ðus cwædon 
394.29.21: we syndon gewisse þines lifes and þines geleafan trumnesse we witon and þin geþyld eac we cunnon unoferswiðed þær we þin cunedon and costedon þæt we mid manigfealdum cræfte ussa wæpna stræla wið þec sendan 
394.29.22: wene ic þæt we ðe furðor ne wyllan leng swencan ne ðe mid brogan bysmrian ac nales þæt an þæt we ðe ðær nu nellað lætan þær ðu geðoht hæfdest 
394.29.23: ac we ðe eac wyllað secgan be ðam eallum þe iu geara westen geardodan hu hie heora lif lifdon 

394.29.24: moyses ærest and elias hie fæston and swylce eac se hælend ealles middangeardes in westene he fæste 
394.29.25: and swylce eac ða mæran munecas þe mid egiptum wæron and þa ær in westenum eardodan þa ðe þurh heora forhæfednesse in him eallum uncysta ofslogon and acwealdon 
394.29.26: þonne gif ðu þæs wilnast þæt ðu of ðe ða ærran fremednesse yfelra leahtra of aðwea þonne scealt þu þinne lichaman þurh forhæfednesse weccean 
394.29.27: forþan swiððor swa ðu þe her on worulde wecst and weccest to forgifenesse þinra gylta swa ðu þonne eft bist in ecnessum getrymed fæstlicor 
394.29.28: and swa micle swiðor swa ðu on þyssan andweardan life ma earfeða dreogest swa micle þu eft in towyrdnesse forgifest 
394.29.29: and þanne þu bist on fæsten her on worulde astreaht þonne byst ðu ahafen for godes eagan 
394.29.30: forðan þin fæsten ne sceal beon þæt a twega daga fyrst oððe þreora oððe ælce dæge þæt ðu ðe swa on teala micelre forhæfdnesse ahebbe ac on seofon nihta fyrste fæsten bið to clænsigeanne se man 
394.29.31: swa on syx dagum ærest god ealles middangeardes fægernesse gehiwode and on þam seofoðan hine reste swa þonne gedafenað þane man gelice syx daga fæsten þone gast frætewigean and þonne þy seofeðan dæge mete þycgan and his lichoman restan 

394.29.32: ða he ða se eadiga wer þas word gehyrde þa aras he sona and to gode cleopode and hine gebæd and þus cwæð 
394.29.33: min drihten god syn mine fynd a onhinder gecyrred forðan ic þe ongite and geþence forðan þu eart min scyppend 

394.29.34: þa sona se awyrgeda feond efne swa rec beforan his onsyne aidlode 
394.29.35: he þa sona forseah þa deofollican lare forþan hie þa ealle idle and unnytte ongeat 
394.29.36: ac þa feng to þære teala myclan andleofone þæt wæs to þam berenan hlafe and þone geþygde and his feorh big fercede 
394.29.37: ða þa werigan gastas þis ongeaton þæt he forhogode hie and heora lare hie þæt mid wependre stefne bemurnon and wide geond þæt land wæðdon 
394.29.38: and he se geadiga wer swa gesigefæsted þa bysmornesse ealle forhogode þara werigra gasta and him for naht dyde 

394.29.39: swylce eft gelamp on sumum sæle ymb unmanega dagas þæs þe he guðlac þy gewunelican þeowdom wæccende þa niht in halegum gebedum wunode þa on þære nihte stilnesse gelamp semninga þæt þær com micel mænego þara werigra gasta and hie eal þæt hus mid heora cyrme gefyldon and him on ælce healfe inguton ufan and neoþan and æghwænon 
394.29.40: wæron hie on syne egeslice and hæfdon heafdu and lange sweoran and mænigre onsyne wæron fulice and orfyrme on heora bearde and hæfdon ruge earan and wohneb egeslice eagan and ondrysenlice muðas 
394.29.41: and heora teð wæron horses tuxum gelice and him wæron þa hracan lige afylled and hie wæron ondrysenlice on stefne 
394.29.42: and hie hæfdon wosceancan and micle cneowo and hindan greate and misscrence tan 
394.29.43: and hashrymedon on heora cleopunge and hie swa ungemetlice hrymdon and foran mid forhtlicum egesum and ungeþwærnessum þæt hit þuhte þæt hit eall betweoh heofone and eorðan hleoðrode þam egeslicum stefnum 
394.29.44: næs þa nænig ylding to þam syððan hie in þæt hus comon 
394.29.45: hie ða sona þone halgan wer eallum limum gebundenum hine tugon and læddon ut of ðære cytan 
394.29.46: ond hie hine on þæt swearte fenn læddon and hine on þa horwihtan wæter wurpon and besencton 
394.29.47: æfter þam hie hine bæron and læddon æfter reðum stowum þæs westenes betuh þa þiccan gewridu þara brymela þæt eal se lichoma wæs gewundod 

394.29.48: mid þy hie ða lange on þære þystran nihte hine swa læddon and swencton þa leton hie hine bidan ana and gestandan heton hine þa þæt he of ðam westene gewite oððe gif he þæt nolde þæt hie woldon mid maran brogan bysmrigan and wæcan 
394.29.49: he ða se eadiga wer heora worda ne gymde ac he mid witigende muðe þus cwæð 
394.29.50: dryhten me is on ða swiðran healfe forðam ic ne beo oncyrred 

394.29.51: þa æfter ðam þa werigan gastas hine genamon and hine swungon mid isenum swipum 
394.29.52: and þa æfter þam hine læddon in þam andrysenlicum fiðerum betuh ða caldan facu þære lyfte 
394.29.53: þa he ða wæs on þære heannesse þære lyfte up gelæded þa geseah he ealne norðdæl heofones swylce he wære þam sweartestum wolcnum afylled swiðra genipa 
394.29.54: þa geseah he semninga þær ða ondrysenlican fiðeru ongen cuman þara werigra gasta and unmæte weorod hyra þær coman togenes and hie sona þær tosomne geþyddon 
394.29.55: hie ða sona þone halgan wer gelæddon to ðam sweartum tintreges gomum helledures 
394.29.56: ða he ðær geseah þa smicendan þismas þara byrnendra liga and þone ege þære sweartan nywylnesse he ða sona wæs ofergeotol ealra þæra tintrega þe he fram þam werigum gastum ær dreah and drefde 
394.29.57: and nalas þæt an þæt he þær þa leglican hyðe ðæs fyres upþyddan geseah and eac þa fulan receastunge swefles þær geseah up geotan 
394.29.58: hie þa sona þa werigan gastas betwuh ða grimlican ligeas in hruron and feollon and þær þa sawla arleasra manna manigfealdum tintregum tintregudon 

394.29.59: þa he se eadiga wer guðlac geseah þa micelnesse þara wita and hine forþy ege swiðlice onþræc ða clypedon hie sona þa werigan gastas micelre clypunge and þus cwædon 
394.29.60: us is miht seald þe to scufanne on ðas witu þysse neowolnesse and her þæt fyr þæt ðu sylfa in þe bærndest and for þinum synnum helleduru ongen þe openað 
394.29.61: mid þy þe ða werigea gastas þyssum wordum spræcon and him swa to beotodon þa andswarude he him and him to cwæð 
394.29.62: wa eow þystra bearnum and forwyrde tuddor ge syndon dustes acsan 
394.29.63: hwa geaf eow yrmingum þæt ðæt ge min geweald ahton in þas witu to sendanne 
394.29.64: hwæt ic eom her andweard and earo and bide mines dryhtnes willan 
394.29.65: forhwan mid eowrum leasum beotingum me bregean 
394.29.66: hie þa werigan gastas hine to ðam gegearwodon swylce hine man þærin scufan wolde 

394.29.67: þa semninga com se heofones biggenga se haliga apostol sanctus bartholomeus mid heofonlicre beorhtnesse and wuldre scinende betuh þa dimman þystro þære sweartan helle 
394.29.68: hie ða þa werigan gastas ne meahton wære fægernesse þæs haligan cuman þær gewunigean ac hie sylfe in heolstre hyddon 
394.29.69: þa he ða se eadiga wer his þone getrywan freond geseah ða wæs he mid gastlicre gefeannesse and on heofoncundre blisse swiðe gefeonde 
394.29.70: and þa aefter þam fleah se haliga guðlac mid þam apostole sancte bartholomei to heofona rices wuldre and hine se hælend þær onfeng 
394.29.71: and he þær leofað and rixaþ in heofona rices wuldre a butan ende on ecnesse amen fiat
