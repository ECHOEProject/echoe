209.51.1: sanctus iohannes geseh ofer garseg swylc hit an land wære 
209.51.2: þa genam hine se ængel and gebrohte hine to neorxenewange 
209.51.3: neorxenewange nis naðer ne on heofene ne on eorðe 
209.51.4: seo boc sægð þæt noes flod wæs feowrtig fedmen heh ofer þa hegesta dunen þe on middenearde synden 
209.51.5: and neorxenewang is feowrtig fedme herre þone noes flod wæs 
209.51.6: and hit hangeð betwonen heofone and eorðen wunderlice swa hit se eallwealdend gescop 
209.51.7: and hit is eall efenlang and efenbrad 
209.51.8: nis þære naðer ne dene ne dune 
209.51.9: ne þære ne byð ne forst ne snaw ne hagel ne reign 
209.51.10: ac þær is fons uite 
209.51.11: þæt is lifes welle 
209.51.12: þonne kalendas januarii inn gað þonne flowð seo welle swa fægere and swa smoltlice and na deoppere þone mann mæig gewæten his finger on forewarde ofer eall þæt land 
209.51.13: and swa gelice ælce monðe ane siðe þonne se monð inn cumð seo welle onginð flowen 
209.51.14: and þær is se fægere wudeholt þe is genemmed radionsaltus 
209.51.15: þær is ælc treow swa riht swa bolt and swa heh þæt nan eorðlic mann ne geseh swa heh ne seggen 
209.51.16: ne cann hwilces cynnes heo synden 
209.51.17: ne fealleð þær næfre leaf of 
209.51.18: ac heo byð singrene wlitig and wynsum welena unrim 
209.51.19: neorxenewange is upprihte on eastewearde þisse wurlde 
209.51.20: nis þær ne hete ne hunger 
209.51.21: ne þær niht nefre ne byð ac simble dæig 
209.51.22: sunne þær scineð seofen siðe brihtlycor þone on þissen earde 
209.51.23: þær wuneð on godes ængles unrim mid þan halgen sawlen oðð domesdæig 
209.51.24: þær wuneð on an fugel fæger fenix gehaten 
209.51.25: he is mycel and mære swa se mihtige hine gescop 
209.51.26: he is hlaford ofer eall fugelcynn 
209.51.27: ælcere wuca ane siðe se fægere fugel hine baðeð on þære lifes welle 
209.51.28: and þonne flihð se fugel and gesett uppe þæt hegeste trow ongean þære haten sunne 
209.51.29: þonne scinð he swa sunneleome 
209.51.30: and he gliteneð swilc he gyldene seo 
209.51.31: his feðeren synden ængles feðeren gelice 
209.51.32: his breost and bile brihte scineð fægere and fage 
209.51.33: feawe synden swylce 
209.51.34: hwat his eagene twa æðele synden swa clæne swa cristal and swa scire swa suneleome 
209.51.35: his fet synden blodreade begen and se bile hwit 
209.51.36: hwæt se fægre fugel flihð of his earde se þe is fægere fenix gehaten 
209.51.37: þonne wuneð he witodlice on egipte lande fiftene wucan feste togædere 
209.51.38: þonne cumeð him to swa swa to heora kinge fageninde swyðe eall þæt fugelcinn 
209.51.39: and fægere gegreteð ealle fenix 
209.51.40: writigeð and singeð ealle abuten him 
209.51.41: ælc on his wisen ealle hine herigeð 
209.51.42: þonne ferð þæt folc feorrene 
209.51.43: swyðe wafigeð and wundrigeð wylcumigeð fenix 
209.51.44: hal beo þu fenix fugele fægerest feorren þu come 
209.51.45: þu glitenest swa read gold ealra fugela king fenix gehaten 
209.51.46: þonne wyreceð heo of wexe and writeð fenix 
209.51.47: and meteð hine fægere þær se madme stant 
209.51.48: þonne fagenegeð þære fugeles ealle fægere and fage 
209.51.49: feale togædere fealleð to foten fenix greteð 
209.51.50: his stemne is swa briht swa beme 
209.51.51: and his sweora swylce smete gold 
209.51.52: and his forebreost fægere geheowed swylce marmelstan mæres cinnes 
209.51.53: and him on read heow rudeð on þan hrynge 
209.51.54: goldfelle gelic gliteneð fenix 
209.51.55: þonne færð eft se fugel fægere to his earde emb fiftene wucan 
209.51.56: and fugeles manige ealle him abuten efne ferden ufene and nyðene and on ælce healfe oððet heo nehiget neorxenewange 
209.51.57: þær inn gefærð fenix fugele fægerest 
209.51.58: and eall oðer fugelcinn to heora earden gewændeð 

209.51.59: nu sæigð her sanctus iohannes soðen worden swa se wyrhte cann þæt æfre binnen an þusend wintren þynceð fenix þæt he forealdod seo 
209.51.60: gegadered togædere ofer eall paradis þa deorwurðe boges and heapeð tosamne 
209.51.61: and þurh godes mihte and þære sunne leome se heap byð onæled 
209.51.62: and þonne fealleð fenix onmiddan þæt micele fyr and wurð forbærned eall to duste 
209.51.63: þonne on þan þriddan dæge ariseð se fægere fugel fenix of deaðe and byð eft edgung 
209.51.64: and færð to þære lifes welle and baðað hine þærinne 
209.51.65: and him wexeð on feðeren swa fægere swa heo æfre fægerest wæren 
209.51.66: þuss he deð æfre binne þusend wintren he hine forbærnð and eft edgung upp ariseð 
209.51.67: and næfð he nænne gemaca 
209.51.68: and nan mann ne wat hweðer hit is þe karlfugel þe cwenefugel bute god ane 
209.51.69: þes halge fugel is fenix gehaten 
209.51.70: wlitig and wynsum swa hine god gescop 
209.51.71: and þuss he sceal drigen drihtenes wille 
209.51.72: se þe is on heofone heh and halig ealra kinge king 
209.51.73: crist us generige þæt we on wynne wunigen mote mid þam þe leofeð and rixeð a bute ænde amen
