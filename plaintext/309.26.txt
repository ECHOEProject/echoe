309.26.1: men ða leofestan magon we hwylcumhwega wordum secgan be ðære arwurðnysse þisse halgan tide and þeoses halgan dæges þe nu þyses morgenes andweard bið 
309.26.2: and þeah ðe us syn ealle þa halgan drihtenlican tida and eac his þæra haligra þa ðe ymb twelf monað beoð mid hea lofe to weorðienne and to wuldrienne

309.26.4: weorðedan we ær to wintre æt geolum þæne drihtenlican gebyrddæg þa he us luflice and eadmodlice hider on middaneard gesohte on menniscne had and on menniscum lichaman

309.26.6: and þeah ðe he hit ær beforan þurh his þa halgan bec toweard sæde and gehete he þæt þonne nu on ðeosse wundorlican symbelnysse openlice gecydde þurh his sylfes lichaman ærest þe he ær onfeng to his godcundnysse and eac þurh manegra oðera haligra manna þa ðe eac mid him of deaðe arison and efne soðlice manna wæron

309.26.33: hwæt we gehyrdon þæt se godspellere cwæð þæt sancta maria cristes lichamlice modor and þa oðre wifmen þa ðe eac mid hire wæron þæt heo to ðære byrgenne comon þy æfenne þæs restedæges se ðe lihteð on ðone ærestan restedæg

309.26.38: ne wæs þæt æniges liges tacen þeah ðe þa godspelleras þæt sumum wordum todældon and swa ungelice cwædon 
309.26.39: ac heora æghwylc þæs soð sæde forðan ðe heo sancta maria and þa oðre wifmenn þa ðe eac mid hire wæron ealle þa niht wacedon and gelomlice to ðære byrgenne eodan and eft fram 
309.26.40: for ðære micclan lufan þe heo to him drihtne hæfdon and eac for ðam hihte þære æriste þe he crist him ær toweard sæde þætte æfter his þrowunge geweorðan sceolde

309.26.42: hwæt we witan þonne hit on morgene ærest dagian onginneð þæt þonne gyt beoð þeostru 
309.26.43: hwæt þæt þonne bið eac swylce swiðe ær on morgenne ge efne æt sunnan upgange 
309.26.44: forðan þæt leoht and seo dagung hwanon cymð þæt elles buton fram þære sunnan þeah ðe we þonne gyt þa sunnan sylfe geseon ne magon 
309.26.45: forðan seo sunne hafað þonne mid þy heofone þas eorðan utan ymbswifen and ymbcerred 
309.26.46: þonne astigað heo þanon eastan up swa we geseon magon 
309.26.47: ac he sanctus matheus ana þæt deagollice and gerynelice spræc and cwæð þæt hit wære þy æfenne þæs restedæges 
309.26.48: and wæs hwæðere on ærnemerigen swa þa oðre godspelleras sædon 
309.26.49: ac forðan he þæt cwæð þe hit þa wæs þære nihte æfen swa þæs dæges æfen bið þonne seo niht cymð swa þonne bið eac þære nihte æfen þonne se dæg cymð 
309.26.50: uespere autem sabbati he cwæð þi æfenne þæs restedæges se ðe lihteð on ðone ærestan restedæg 
309.26.51: hwæt we witan symle þonne hit æfnað þonne þeostrað hit næs na leohteð and þæt leoht wanað næs na wexeð 
309.26.52: sanctus matheus þæt ana deagollice and gerynelice spræc be ðære weorðunge þysse halgan nihte efne swa swa heo eall gastlice onlihted beon sceolde þurh þa gefealican wyrde þære drihtenlican æriste þe on ðysse halgan nihte ende geworden wæs þa hit furðum dagian ongan 
309.26.53: hwæt we þæt witan and eac leorniað þætte ær beforan æt frymðe worulde seo niht symble fylgde þone dæg æfter þære endebyrdnysse þe heo æt frymðe on gesceapene wæron 
309.26.54: ac þa seo drihtenlice ærist gewearð on ðysse halgan nihte ufanweardre þa wæs syððan a ðurh þæt seo endebyrdnys oncerred þara tida and syððan a se dæg fylgde þa niht 
309.26.55: swa we nu þa drihtenlican niht næmniað sunnanniht and þonne eft on morgenne þone dæg hatað sunnandæg 
309.26.56: and we swa eall þæt rim þæra dogra æfter þære endebyrdnysse tellað

309.26.58: forðan mancynn wæs æt frymðe worulde efne swa hit onlihte and on dæge gesceapen and geseted wære þa he ure drihten þa menniscan gecynd to his sylfes anlicnysse gesceop on snyttru and on wisdome and on foreþancolnysse

309.26.62: he us þa æghwæðer ge mid wordum ge mid bisenum lærde hu we of synnum and of deofles anwealde acerran sceoldan and eac helle tintregu befleon and þæt heofoncunde wuldor begytan

309.26.137: ne ondrædað eow cwæð he ac gað and secgað minum gebroðrum þæt heo faren on galileam on þæt þeodland þæt heo me geseon sceolan 

309.26.138: hwæt we nu magon on ðysum wordum ongytan þa micclan eadmodnysse ures drihtnes þe he wið mancynn gecydde 
309.26.139: we men þe þæs wyrðe ne syndon þæt we his þeowas sien gecwedene and he hwæðere us him to broðrum and to bearnum nemde 
309.26.140: is þæt swiðe micel ar and wurðmynt þæt we men magon beon gode sylfum to broðrum and to bearnum genemde 
309.26.141: ac uton gemunan þæt we sceolon mid halgum dædum and mid soðfæstum us þa wunrðmyndo geearnian 
309.26.142: ealle þa ðe nu willað beon bilewite and mildheorte him betwynan and arfæste wið oðre men heo habbað godes lufan and manna on rihtne had 
309.26.143: ealle þa beoð þurh þæt gode sylfum to broðrum and to bearnum gewordene and hi moton to widan feore beforan cristes sylfes ansyne gefean and blissian 
309.26.144: swylce eac ealle þa ðe nu synne and mandæde fremmeð and heo oferhydige and inwitfulle beoð and unarfulle wið oðre men and þeofet and unriht geligeru begangað and earme men forneomað and fordemað ealle þa beoð þurh þæt deofle sylfum to broðrum and to bearnum genemde and gewordene 
309.26.145: uton gemunan hu sarlice and hu geomorlice se ealda feond manncynnes ure þa ærestan ealdras adam and efan neorxnawonges fægernysse bereafode 
309.26.146: and heo mid his facne and inwite beswac and forlærde þæt heo on ðære byrignysse þæra beweredra blæda heora scyppendes bebod ælmihtiges godes awegan and oferferdon 
309.26.147: and þa þurh þæt of ðam eadiglican life and of engla gesiðscipe on ðas sarlican woruld and on ðas niðerlican wræce aworpene and asende wurdon þe we nu her on libbað and drohtniað 
309.26.148: ge efne se gylt gewearð to ðan grim manncynne þætte ne wæs syððan æfre ænig mennisc lichama þætte ne sceolde deað þrowian 
309.26.149: ne þæt gyta sarlicra wæs þætte ne wæs æfre ænig to ðæs halig mann on his life þæt he hwæðere to heofona rice moste ær ðan þeos halige drihtnes æristtid geworden wæs 
309.26.150: ac þa halgan men and þa gedefan þa ðe ær beforan þan gode and fullfremede wæron þonne heo of lichaman eodan þonne wæron hi on ræste gelædde 
309.26.151: næs na hwæðere on heofonan rice ac heo þære frofre bidon and to ðære hihtan hwænne se on middaneard come se ðe æghwæðer ge him þe ær wæron heofona rices duru ontynde 
309.26.152: ge þæt he þonne eac þam þe nu gyta lifigenda on lichaman wæron þone weg cuðne and openne gedyde to heofona rice 
309.26.153: ure drihten þæt geare on ðære ealdan æ his þam halgum gehet and him þone gefean and þa frofre toweard sæde þæt he us hider on middaneard secan wolde on menniscum lichaman 
309.26.154: he þæt þa æt nextan swiðe arfæstlice and swiðe mildheortlice gefylde geefne þy furþor þe he sylf on ðas halgan tid for mancynnes hælu deað geðrowade on ðam frigedæge þe nu wæs 
309.26.155: and he eft þæs þy þriddan dæge of deaðe aras on ðam arwurðan dæge þe nu þysum morgenne bið 
309.26.156: and he þurh þæt ealle þa of ðam grimman þeowdome hellewita to ecum life and to ungeendadlicum friðe alysde and generede þa ðe on his lufan rihtlice awuniað and his bebodu medumlice and fulfremedlice healdan willað 
309.26.157: forðan þæt is swiðe rihtlic and swiðe gedafenlic þæt æghwylc mennisc tunge þæs ælmihtigan drihtnes þrym and his mycelnys mid lofum and mid wurðmyndum her geandette 
309.26.158: ac uton nu cerran to ura sylfra gewitnysse gif we hwylce synne hæbben ungeandette and ungebette þæt we þonne þa clænlice geandettan and medumlice gebetan 
309.26.159: uton beon arfulle and mildheorte us betweonum lufian us sylfe rihtlice and urne drihten ofer ealle oðre þing and manna gehwylc þurh hine sylfne god do 
309.26.160: æghwylc oðerne to gode tyhte and lære forðan þæt is swiðe rihtlic þætte þa men þe þæt mynteð þæt heo god on ecere sibbe geseon and gefeon þæt þa him sien betweonum sibsume 
309.26.161: arleasra manna þæt is þæt heo betweonum figen and hatigen forðan ðe heo beoð deofles gewyrhtan and heo heora lif libbað him sylfum to sceande 
309.26.162: and efne swa heo heora feondum þurh heora synne georner hyrnað swa he heom æt nextan grimra geweorðeð 
309.26.163: uton nu tilian mid godes fultume þæt we mid unwæmre heortan and mid clænre ingewitnysse to drihtnes wigbede gangan moton 
309.26.164: and þær þam halgan husle onfon þæt us is her on middanearde geseald eces lifes to wedde and buton þæs weddes onfangennysse næfre ænig man godes rice gestigeð 
309.26.165: forðan æghwylc þæra manna se ðe þam rihtlice onfehð ælc þæra onfehð eces lifes wed and æghwylc þæra þe him unmedum to gedyrstigað þonne onfehð se strængre godes fæhðu and ecere forwyrde wræce 
309.26.166: forðan we sceolon geðæncan þa hwile þe we moten and hit on urum gewealde sy þæt we þa þing don þe us to ecere hælu gelimpan mote ær ðan us færinga deaðes þeostro fornime and forgripe 
309.26.167: uton gemunan þætte ure nænigum his goda æfter þeosse worulde to fela ne ðynceð þonne he þæra on ecnysse brucan sceal þe he ær on ðeosse medmicclan tide for gode gedyde 
309.26.168: ne magon we æt domesdæge þonne ure drihten geldeð æghwylcum anum men æfter his sylfes gewyrhtum nahwæðer ne gold ne seolfor for us syllan ne nænige woruldspeda 
309.26.169: ac we þonne þæt deoreste wed ageofan sceolon manna gehwylc his sylfes lichaman and sawle 
309.26.170: uton la tilian þæt we þonne sy to life næs na to deaðe gedemde 
309.26.171: uton þancian þam ælmihtigum drihtne ure alysnysse and tilian þæt we næfre mid synnum forwyrde þa gife ure alysnysse we ðe wæron mid þy deorwurðestan cristes blode alysde 
309.26.172: swa sanctus paulus se apostol cwæð þæt he drihten ne sparode his agene bearne 
309.26.173: ac he hine for us eallum to deaðe gesealde for ure ecere hælu þæs him sy symle lof and wuldor and seo mæste þanc wurðesta herenys a buton ende in ealra worulda woruld amhnamen
