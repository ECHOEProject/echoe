331.43.1: men þa leofestan us is deope beboden þæt we geornlice mynegian and læran sculon þæt manna gehwylc to gode buge and fram synnum gecyrre 
331.43.2: se cwyde is swyðe egeslic þe god þurh þone witegan be þam cwæð þe godes folce bodian sculon

331.43.31: men þa leofestan eow eallum is cuð þæt þes gearlica ymbrene us gebringð efne nu þas clænan tid lenctenlices fæstenes 
331.43.32: on þam we sceolan ure gymeleaste and forgægednyssa urum gastlicum scryfte geandettan 
331.43.33: and us mid fæstene and wæccum and gebedum and ælmesdædum fram synnum aþwean 
331.43.34: þæt we baldlice mid gastlicere blisse þa easterlican mærsunge cristes æristes wurðian moton 
331.43.35: and ðæs halgan husles þigene mid geleafan underfon us to synna forgyfenysse and to gescyldnysse deofollicra costnunga

331.43.37: gyf þonne þissa þreora þinga ænig hwylcne man lette þæt hine to ðam fæstene ne onhagie þonne is hit alefed þæt he mot mete þycgan on þa gerad þe he his ælmessan sylle and nænne oþerne man him to ne spanne to þam gereordum butan þær hwylc oðer mettrum man sy þe þæt fæsten aberan ne mæge 
331.43.38: gif þonne se hala mid þam unhalan þone mete þicgeð þonne wurþað hig begen þurh þæs mettruman unmihta beswicene 
331.43.39: and nis nanum men alefed for nanre neode þis fæsten to abrecane ne mid ælmessan to alysenne buton hine þæra þreora þinga hwylc forwyrne yld oððe iuguð oððe unhæl 
331.43.40: witodlice þis feowertigfealde fæsten wæs asteald on þære ealdan gecyðnysse þa þa se heretoga moyses fæste feowertig daga and feowertig nihta tosomne to ði þæt he moste godes æ underfon 
331.43.41: eft se mæra witega elias ealswa lang fæsten þurh godes mihte swa swa se oðer gefylde 
331.43.42: and syðþan he wearð geferod lichamlice on heofonlicum cræte to þam uplican lyfte 
331.43.43: and cymð eft he and enoh togeanes antecriste to ði þæt hi þæs deofles leasunge mid godes soðfæstnysse oferstælon 
331.43.44: drihten eac on þære nywan gecyðnysse fæste þurh his godcundan mihte feowertig daga and nihta fram eallum bigleofum 
331.43.45: ðus wæs ure lenctenlice fæsten asteald

331.43.47: nu is us alyfed þurh lareowa ealdordom þæt we dæghwamlice on þissere lenctenlican tide urne lichaman gereordian mid forhæfednysse and syfernysse and clænnysse 
331.43.48: stuntlice fæst se lenctenlic fæsten se ðe on þisum clænum timan hine sylfne mid galnysse befylð 
331.43.49: unrihtlic byð þæt se cristena man flæsclice lustas gefremme on þam timan þe he flæscmettas forgan sceal 
331.43.50: and læsse pleoh byð þam men þæt he flæsces bruce on lenctenfæstene þonne he wifes bruce 
331.43.51: witodlice on eallum tidum gedafenað cristenum mannum þæt hi gode weorc began and ælmesdæda and swaþeah swyðost on þissum gemænelican fæstene 
331.43.52: se ðe on oðrum dagum sleac wære to godnysse he sceal huruþingan on þissum dagum acucian on godum biggengum
