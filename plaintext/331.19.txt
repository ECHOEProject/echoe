to folce 

331.19.1: leofan men hwa mæg æfre oðrum furðor freondscype gecyðan þonne he his agen feorh gesylle and ðurh þæt his freond wið deað ahredde 
331.19.2: ealle we scoldan forweorðan ecan deaðe nære þæt crist for us deað þrowode 
331.19.3: ac he gebohte us þa ealle mid his deorwurðan blode of hellewite and hæfð nu þurh his gyfe manna gehwylc gif he geearnian wylle heofona rice 
331.19.4: ðonne ne þince us næfre to mycel ac us mæg aa to lytel þæt we godes þances to gode gedon 
331.19.5: forðam ne cunne we næfre him geleanian þa ðing ealle þe he us gedon hæfð and dæghwamlice deð and gyt don wile þær we betst beðurfan gyf we sylfe þæs geearnian wyllað
