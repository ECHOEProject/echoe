331.18.1: leofan men for ure ealra þearfe crist com on þis lif and for ure neode deað þrowode 
331.18.2: ær ðam timan næs æni man on worulde swa mære þæt he on an ne sceolde to helle swa he heonan ferde 
331.18.3: swa forð ahte deofol geweald ealles manncynnes eal for ure yldrena gewyrhtan 
331.18.4: ac mycel geþolode ðurh his mildheortnesse crist for ure þearfe þa he let hine sylfne bindan and swingan and on rode ahon and him ægðer þurhdrifan mid isenum næglum ge fet ge handa and swa to deaðe acwellan
