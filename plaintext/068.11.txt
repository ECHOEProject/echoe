larspell 

68.11.1: men ða leofestan sanctus gregorius cwæð 
68.11.2: ic eow halsige on ðone drihten þe gescop heofenas and eorðan and ealle gesceafta 
68.11.3: and on ða halgan þrynnesse and on ða twelf apostolas 
68.11.4: and on ealle godes halgan and on þa cyrcan þe ge to gelyfað 
68.11.5: and on þæt halige fulluht þe ge underfengon 
68.11.6: þæt ge forlætan þa unnyttan spræca gewurðan and þa unnyttan geþancas of eowrum heortum þonne ge cumað into godes cyrican 
68.11.7: and utan mid inweardre heortan þencan ymbe þa godcundan lare þe us godcunde lareowas lærað and sæcgað ymbe þæt heofoncunde rice hu we hit begitan magon and wið deofles costnunge us gescildan 
68.11.8: cyrice is þære sawle scip and sceld on domesdæg 
68.11.9: and heo is us gesceapen to gebedhuse na to nanum gemothuse butan ymbe þæt heofoncunde rice and lare 

68.11.10: ðonne is hit gecweden on halgum bocum þæt ælc man hæfð twegen lareowas on dæg and twegen on niht 
68.11.11: oðer us læreð to heofona rices wuldre þæt is godes engel 
68.11.12: oþer us lærð to hellewites brogan þæs nama is deofol 
68.11.13: ðonne to sunnan upgange þonne bringað hy gode on gewrite se engel eall þæt we on ðære nihte to gode gedoð and se deofol eall þæt we to yfele gedoð and gefremmað and wile oferstælan þone engel gif he mæg mid þam yfelvm weorcum 
68.11.14: and hy cumað to sunnan setlgange and bringað gode eall þæt we to gode gedoð and eac to yfele 
68.11.15: and swa hy us eac lædað into cyrican 
68.11.16: þonne lærð us godes engel stilnesse and gemetlice spræce and smeaunge ymbe godes beboda 
68.11.17: and we gehyrað ures drihtnes lofsangas þærinne and habban gastlice þeawas and bebodu æt urum heortum and herigen urne scyppend ealra gesceafta 
68.11.18: þonne lærð us se deofol unstilnesse and ungemetlice leahtras and unnytte spræce and unnytte geþancas and tælnesse and treowleasnesse godes beboda 

68.11.19: ðonne se ðe oðerne tælan wille þonne gange he ærest on digle stowe and besceawige hine sylfne and beþence hine sylfne and beðence hwæðer hine ne mæge ænig man getælan 
68.11.20: ne bið þeah næfre nan man leahterleas ne synleas ealra þinga 
68.11.21: wa ðæs mannes sawle þe ða unnyttan spræca sprecð and þa ungemetlican hleahtras drifð innan cyrcan 
68.11.22: and eac þam men þe wyrigeð his fæder oððe his moder oððe his hlaford oððe his biscop oððe his scrift 
68.11.23: ða þe þæt doð hi beoð asceadene fram þære heofonlican cyrican and ealra hyra gemanan to deofla gerime 
68.11.24: nytan hy na hu heard wite hy ðæs to leanes habban sceolan æfter domesdæge butan hy hit gebetan ær heora endedæge 
68.11.25: and us beodað ure godcundan lareowas þurh witegena word ægðer ge gehadodum mannum ge læwedum ðæt ærest ða godes þeowas þonne heo into cyrican cuman þæt hy habban gastlice þeawas æt heora heortan and mynsterlice wisan on þeawlicum andgite godes beboda eallum folce to bysne 
68.11.26: ðonne lærað hy us eac swa oft swa we oftost magon þæt we secan ure cyrican mid ælmessum and mid urum clænum gebedum inweardlicre heortan 
68.11.27: and þancien we þam ælmihtigan drihtne ealre þære are þe he us on twelf monðum toforlætt 

68.11.28: ne recð god ælmihtig þære forgolpenan ælmessan ne ðæs gymeleasan gebedes 
68.11.29: ðæt hine lærð se deofol þæt he stande and gorette and locige underbæc ut 
68.11.30: þæt bið gymeleas gebed and he hit ne mæg gebetan forðan þe se deofol hæfð his heortan on his handa and heo bið swa heard swa stan oððe flint ongean godes beboda 
68.11.31: and heo bið swa liðig swa clað and heo mylteð swa wex æt fyre ongean deofles lare and his gedwolan 
68.11.32: and he sceal eallum mode and eallum mægne to gode þencan ymbe þæt heofoncunde rice and bigean his cneowa and his hleor to eorðan and lætan his unnyttan geþancas of his mode 
68.11.33: wa þæs mannes sawle þe nyle geswican unnytre spræce and unnyttes geþances 
68.11.34: he bið wyrse þonne hund oððe æniges cynnes nyten þonne he into cyrcan cymð forðam þe se man bið gefullad ac he þæt abrecð 
68.11.35: forðam he amyrð ægðer ge hine sylfne ge mænige oðre men forðam ælc man hatað þone hund and drifð hine ut of þære cyrican ac man ne deð þæne yfelan man swa 

68.11.36: sanctus paulus apostolus wæs sprecende and sæde þæt him godes engel æteowde sume arlease sawle and swyðe synfulle ut gangende of þam earman lichaman 
68.11.37: and he geseah þæt þær wæron micel meniu engla and deofla and þa fuhton swiðe þearle þa englas and þa deoflu ymbe þa earman sawle 
68.11.38: and þa deoflu hy genaman æt þam englum and oferstældon hy mid hire yfelan weorcum þe heo wyrcende wæs 
68.11.39: and þa wæron þa englas swyðe sarige on heora mode 
68.11.40: and þa deoflu hy potedon and þoddetton þa earman sawle and heton hy ut faran raðe of þam lichaman swyðe heardlice 
68.11.41: and se lichama wæs þa gyt sprecende and he ongan swiðe yfele and bæd his deaðes and sæde þæt him næfre ær nære swa wa swa him þa wæs 
68.11.42: and him andswaredon þa deoflu and hy sædon þæt him wære micele mare and wyrse yfel toweard 

68.11.43: ða axode sanctus paulus þone engel swyðe forhtigendre stæmne and cwæð to him 
68.11.44: min hlaford hwæt is seo ungesælige sawel 
68.11.45: ða sæde he him þæt he wære cyrican slitere 
68.11.46: and þonne he þærin com þonne hyscte he on ða godcundan lareowas 
68.11.47: and he tælde ða cristenan men þe on ðone ælmihtigan drihten gelyfdon 
68.11.48: and he wæs æfre unnytte word sprecende innan godes temple and unnytte geþancas he ðohte þærinne 

68.11.49: and þonne se goda heap ongan singan and god herian and ræddan godspell and sædon ymbe cristes tocyme and ymbe his þrowunge and his haligra þonne sæde he þæt hy lugon and hit worhton heom sylfe 
68.11.50: and him ðuhte þonne seo tid to lang þæt he hit gehyrde and þærinne wære and wende hine þonne aweg 
68.11.51: he ne mihte hit gehyran 
68.11.52: and se deofol hine lærde þonne eall þæt and lædde hine þonne ut of þære cyrican be ðam locce 
68.11.53: swa oft swa he þærinne wæs him þuhte æfre to lang hwonne he moste beon ymbe þæs lichaman oferfylle and ymbe his agene unþeawas 
68.11.54: and he wæs æwbreca and he nolde næfre his synna behreowsian ne andettan his scrifte 
68.11.55: and he sceal standan on þære fyrenan ea oð his weleras oð domesdæg 
68.11.56: ðær ne bið nan gefea ne nan blis ne ðær ne bið nan lufu 

68.11.57: ða cwæð se engel eft to paule 
68.11.58: folga me þonne æteowe ic ðe sumes soðfæstes mannes sawle ut gangende of þam lichaman 
68.11.59: ða eodon hy þa ymbe lytel þa clypode se engel to paule and cwæð him to loca hider 
68.11.60: þa locade he þider 
68.11.61: ða geseah he þa halgan sawle ut of þam godan lichaman gangende 
68.11.62: and he geseah micele menigu engla and deofla 
68.11.63: and hy fuhton stiðlice ymbe þa halgan sawle 
68.11.64: and þa englas hy genamon æt þam deoflum and oferstældon hy mid hire godum dædum 
68.11.65: and þa deoflu wendon sceamigende aweg and hy wæron on miclum unsælum 
68.11.66: and þa englas wæron on swiðe micelre blisse and hy heton byrigean þone godan lichaman and þus cwædon 
68.11.67: benedicat omnia opera eius 
68.11.68: ðæt is on englisc gebletsige eall hire weorc ælmihtig drihten 
68.11.69: and se goda lichama wæs þa get sprecende and þancode gode ealre þære are and þære blisse and þære lisse þe he him lænde 
68.11.70: and he sæde þæt him nære næfre ær swa eðe ne swa myrige on nanum yfele swa him þa wæs and he wæs biddende his lifes 
68.11.71: and him andswaredon þa englas and sædon þæt him wære hundteontigfealdlicre mare myrhð and blis toweard 

68.11.72: ða axode paulus þone engel hwæt seo gode sawel wære 
68.11.73: ða sæde he him þæt heo wære mildheortnesse fyligende and staðolfæst on hire heortan wið deofles costnungum 
68.11.74: and þæt heo wære herigende æfre on hire life urne drihten and urne scyppend ealra gesceafta and ealle his haligra 
68.11.75: and heo lufode þa halgan cyrican and gastlice gebedu and fæsten and ælmessan and þa godcundan heapas 
68.11.76: and eal cristen folc heo lufode swa hire fæder oððe moder and sæde þæt hy wæron hire geswustra 
68.11.77: and þonne se heap ongan singan godes lofsangas and ræddon godspel þonne bletsode heo hy 
68.11.78: and hire þuhte seo tid to scort þæt heo ne moste gehyran ymbe cristes þrowunge secgan and his haligra and ymbe his tocyme and hu heo mihte hellewites brogan wiðstandan and þæt heofoncunde rice geearnian 
68.11.79: and se ðe wæs cyrican ehtere and nolde gehyran ne gelyfan on þa godcundan lare þonne onscunode heo hine swylce heo wære hire gefa 
68.11.80: and heo nolde næfre cuman to godes weofode butan hire ælmessan 
68.11.81: forðam nan man nah to godes cyrican ne to godes weofode idel to cumene þæt he ne bringe be ðam dæle þe hire god læne and hire to onhagude 
68.11.82: forðan hit is geræd and gecweden on halgum bocum ðæt swa swa wæter adwæsceð fyr swa adwæsceð seo ælmesse þa synne 
68.11.83: and sceal beon seo gode sawel on abrahames fæðmum oð domesdæg 
68.11.84: be ðam we magon niman bysne be woruldþingum 
68.11.85: se man þe bringð medsceat þam gerefan se geærendað bet þonne se ðe nænne ne bringð 

68.11.86: we eow sæcgað to soðan þæt se ðe nele her his synna nu andettan his scrifte and betan swa he him tæcð hine sceal on domesdæg gesceamian beforan gode and eallum his halgum and eac eallum deoflum swa þam men dyde þe wurde færinga nacod beforan eallon folce and he nyste þonne mid hwam he þone sceamiendan lichaman bewruge 
68.11.87: swa him bið on domesdæg butan he his synna ær bewreo mid andetnesse and mid dædbote and mid ælmessan and mid geswicennesse and mid halgum gebedum and mid fæstenum and mid manegum godum weorcum 
68.11.88: ða soðfæstan men þonne geseoð heora wuldor and heora wlite and blisse hym toweard 
68.11.89: and þa arleasan þonne geseoð heora wite and heora suselcwale hym toweard 
68.11.90: we magon aðwean mid ælmesdædum ure synna of urum lichaman 
68.11.91: and we eow biddað for eowres drihtnes lufan þæt ge dæghwamlice dælan ælmessan be ðam dæle þe ælcum men to onhagige þeah hit ne sy butan feorðan dæl anes hlafes godes þances ælmesmannum oððe wydewum oððe steopcildum oððe þeowum mannum oððe ælþeodigum mannum 
68.11.92: wa ðæs mannes sawle þe betynð his duru ongean godes þearfan for ðam þingon þæt hine lærð se deofol 
68.11.93: swa him bið betyned heofona rices duru ongean on domesdæge 
68.11.94: ac beon we æfre cumliðe 
68.11.95: ure sawel bið cristes cuma on þam forhtigendan domesdæge 

68.11.96: utan lufian ure cyrican forðam heo bið ure friðiend and werigend wið þæt micele fyr on domesdæg 
68.11.97: and wyrcan we simle brycge and þa betan 
68.11.98: ðeah se man nime ænne stan and lecge on ful sloh þæt se ælmesman mæge mid þam oðrum fet steppan on ða clænan healfe þæt him bið micel ælmesse and micel med for gode 
68.11.99: eac beðearf seo sawel on domesdæg rihtes weges and clænes and staðolfæstre brycge ofer þone glideran weg hellewites brogan 
68.11.100: bærnon we urne lichaman mid clænum gebedum æt urum heortan and mid fæstenum and mid ælmessdædum 
68.11.101: and toforan eallum oðrum þingum wite gehwa þæt he hæbbe clæne heortan to eallum cristenum mannum 
68.11.102: he sceal eac lætan his wyrignesse and lufian his gebedu 
68.11.103: and he sceal forlætan yfele geþohtas æt his heortan 
68.11.104: forðan þæt yfele geþanc hine lærð se deofol 
68.11.105: ðonne ne beo nan man oðres mannes ælmessan to geornfull butan he wite þæt he mæge ægðer gebetan ge his agene synne ge eac oðres mannes 
68.11.106: ælc man hæfð on his agenre byrðene genoh 

68.11.107: ðonne geþænce ælc man him sylf hu swiðe man pinsað þa sawle on domesdæg þonne man sett þa synne and þa sawle on ða wæge and hy man wegeð swa man deð gold wið penegas 
68.11.108: and gif þa penegas teoð swiðor þonne þæt gold þonne miswyrð þam men hraðe 
68.11.109: swa bið þære sawle and þære synne 
68.11.110: gif seo synn tihð swyðor þonne se sawel þonne farað hy butu on forwyrd 
68.11.111: and þa synna yrnað æfter þære earman sawle and hire þus to cweðað 
68.11.112: hwi noldest ðu earme betan ure synna þa þu hy onfenge þurh ure ælmessan 
68.11.113: hwy noldest ðu forwyrhte biddan þe arfulle þingeras wið þone ælmihtigan þrym þære halgan þrynnesse and æt þære soðan annesse 
68.11.114: hwi noldest ðu ungesælige andettan þinum drihtne and biddan þone halgan michael þæt he wære þe fultumigend and ealle his halgan 
68.11.115: and þonne get þus gretað grimmum stæfnum 
68.11.116: hwæt sceoldon þe þeot eorðan fylnes ure ælmessan 
68.11.117: ðu us gehete godcunde gebedo and clæne fæsten and gastlice wæccan and þu hit us aluge 
68.11.118: and þu nystest þe sylfum ane tid dæges witod lif æfter urum deaðe 
68.11.119: and þu ne mihtest him wiðstandan mid þinum golde ne mid þinum seolfre ne þeah þu ahtest eall þysne middaneard to æhte þæt þu ne sceoldest wyrmum to mete and to gebeddan weorðan and wreon þe mid þære cealdan eorðan þær ðu sceoldest to fule and to fracoðe weorðan and to laðlicre ansyne eallum þinum freondum 
68.11.120: and we gelyfdon on ðe swa on urne drihten and þu hæfst us ealle beswicen and belæwed 
68.11.121: we wendon þæt þu wære godfyrht and hæfdest gastlice gebæru beforan us ac ðu hæfdest deofles geþanc æt þinre heortan and þu wære an licetere and þe þuhte æfre to lytel ure ælmesse 
68.11.122: ac heo þe sceal beon nu swyðe grimme forgolden þæt þu wære swa ungeþancfull þinum drihtne 
68.11.123: ne þu ne gelyfdest on ða godcundan lare þe þe laðedon dæghwamlice to ðam uplican rice 
68.11.124: and þu wære swa gifre swa hund and þu næfre nære full þe ma þe hell 
68.11.125: sceamige þe nu þinra unðeawa betwux deaðe and deoflum 
68.11.126: and þu wære þær morð and myrðra ac þu ne miht her swa beon 
68.11.127: ac þu his scealt nu her wite habban on þære grimman helle mid deoflum and mid dracum and mid wælslitendum wyrmum and mid þam grimmestan suselcwale a on ecnysse 

68.11.128: geþence gehwa him sylf hu sceort and hu earmlic þis læne lif is 
68.11.129: ne yldan we na fram dæge to dæge þæt we to gode ne gecyrron 
68.11.130: forðam þe we us nytan witod lif oð æfen 
68.11.131: ne we nyton hwæðer we motan eft dæges gebidan 
68.11.132: ne us ne þearf na tweonian þæt we oðrum ne scylon gebyrian butan tweon oððe heofonwarena cyninge oððe hellewites deofle swa hwæðer swa we her geearniað 
68.11.133: hit is earmlic and sorhlic eallum cristenum mannum to gehyranne and to geseonne eall þæt man us foresægð ymbe cristes hidercyme and his þrowunge and ymbe hellewites brogan 
68.11.134: and se man bið swa blind and swa deaf and he bið swa heard swa stan 
68.11.135: and he ne mæg ongitan þa godcundan lare þe hine lærð and laðað to urum drihtne fram deofles anwealde and his yrmðum 

68.11.136: æghwylcum men is to onscunianne ælc oferfyll on ælcum timan forðam þe heo is drihtne swyðe lað 
68.11.137: oferfyll bið þære sawle feond and þæs lichaman unhæl 
68.11.138: se ðe his to fela nymð he bið wodum men gelic 
68.11.139: and hit bið mægbana and morðslaga 
68.11.140: and hit ne murneð for nanum men 
68.11.141: ne for fæder ne for meder ne for broðer ne for swuster ne for nanum gesibban men 
68.11.142: ealle unsibba hit wyrcð and hit alucð þæs mannes mod þurh deofles costnunge 
68.11.143: ðonne secgað us ure godcundan lareowas þæt hy nyton þa tid hwænne we of þysum earman life gewitan sceolon þe ma þonne se þeof wat þe ðone oðerne lætt and lærð hwonne seo tid cymð þe he hy begen on forwyrd forlæt 
68.11.144: swa bið þam godcundum mannum þe us laðiað to heofona rices wuldre 
68.11.145: þæt hy nyton þa tid þe se ælmihtiga god us wyle habban and to his rice gelaðian 
68.11.146: þær we motan beon gif we hit willað geearnian on wlite and on wynne mid englum and mid heahenglum and mid þam apostolican werode mid fæder and mid suna and mid þam halgan gaste a butan ælcum ende 
68.11.147: god us to ðam gefultumige amen
