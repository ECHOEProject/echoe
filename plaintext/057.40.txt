de inclusis 

57.40.1: sum deofelgast sæde sumen ancre eall helle geryn and hire tintrege 
57.40.2: he sæde þæt þes middaneard nære na mare dryges landes togeanes þan garsegen þonne man precode ænne prece on ane wexbrede 
57.40.3: for his mycelnesse eall þas eorðe abuten lið 
57.40.4: and lytel dæl is under hefene dryges landes þæt hi ne sy eall mid garsecge oferurnen 
57.40.5: eft cwæð se deofel to þam ancre 
57.40.6: gif ænig man beoð ane niht on helle and beoð eft þonen of alæd 
57.40.7: and me betyne þone garseg eall abuten mid isene 
57.40.8: and þone eall afylle mid fyre up oþ hefene hrof 
57.40.9: and ymbesette hine eall abuten mid smiðes belian 
57.40.10: and to æcen belie ænne man 
57.40.11: and se habbe samsones strænchþe 
57.40.12: and wiðinnen þa garsecge mid mannen afylle 
57.40.13: and ælc man hæbbe hamer on hande 
57.40.14: and þa belies blawen and þa hameres beatan and se leig arise 
57.40.15: for ealle þisen broche ne arist seo werige sawle for þære pine þe hi on helle ane niht þolede 
57.40.16: georne cunnie ælc man to befleonne hellewite 
57.40.17: forþon hit is þærinne sarlic to wunigenne 
57.40.18: se awergede gast sæde þan ancre þæt he ne mehte all asecgen þa pine þe þa earme sawle þoliað on helle 
57.40.19: þurh deofles swicdom and adames gemeleaste we wæren æt frumþe ut of paradis ascofene and on þas wrecce world asænd þe we nu on wuniað 
57.40.20: þærfore nabbe we her nane fæstlice stede ne langsume æþele 
57.40.21: ac we gelomlice geherað and dæghwamlice geseoð to hwam se eorðlice dæl and se mænnissce gecynde gewurðan sceal 
57.40.22: beþænce se man hine þe him selfes rechð 
57.40.23: forþon se man þe hefene rice secen wile he hit sceal secan mid swince and naht mid eðnesse ne mid idelnesse
