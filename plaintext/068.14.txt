68.14.1: gehyrað nu men þa leofestan hu þas halgan bec eow myngiað and eow þone weg ætywiað and eac in gelædað to heofena rice 
68.14.2: and hu ge magon þa yfelu þisses middaneardes forbugan and gode gefylstendum to ðam ecan life becuman 

68.14.3: clypað ure drihten þurh his witegan swiðe stiðlice to ðam preostum þe godes folce wisian scylan þæt heo eow myngian and cyðan eow soðfæstnesse weg and þus cweð 
68.14.4: clama ne cesses quasi tuba exalta uocem tuam et adnuntia populo meo peccata eorum 
68.14.5: ðæt is on urum geþeode 
68.14.6: clypa ne ablin ðu ac ahefe up þine stefne swa beme and cyð minum folce heora synna 
68.14.7: gif þu þonne nelt þam unrihtwisan his synna cyðan þu bist his sawle scyldig 
68.14.8: her ge magon gehyran broðru ða leofestan þæt ealle godes mæssepreostas and biscopas and oðre godcunde lareowas synd on mycelre frecednysse gif heo eow godes bebodu ne cyðað 
68.14.9: be ðam cweð drihten þurh þone witegan 
68.14.10: audiens ex ore meo sermonem meum adnuntiabis eis ex me non ex te 
68.14.11: ðæt is on urum geþeode 
68.14.12: of minum muðe gehlystendum þu bodast hym mine spræce of me næs of þe 
68.14.13: nu ge magon ongitan hwylcne ege þa mæssepreostas habbað toweardne 
68.14.14: þonne god wile girnan to hym ealra þara sawla þe hym her on life betæhte synd þonne we witan þæt anra manna gehwylc mæg earfoðlice be his agenre sawle gescead agyldan on domesdæg 
68.14.15: forþam us is micel þearf þæt we eow þone weg cyðen and þæt siðfæt ætywen þe ge þurh magon to ðam ecan life becuman 
68.14.16: and eac eow is to warnienne forðan gif hit us is frecenlic þæt we hit eow ne cyðen hit is eow micele mare frecednes þæt ge nellen gefyllan þæt we eow to eowre sawle þearfe cyðað 
68.14.17: þonne þurh þone witegan is gecweden 
68.14.18: gif þu bytst þam synfvllum þæt he of his unrihtan wegum gecyrre and he gecyrran nelle on his unrihtwisnessum he swelt and þu hæfst alysed þine sawle fram his witum 

68.14.19: ealra ærest sceal ælc cristenman witan and on his heortan trumlice healdan þæt æreste bebod and þæt mæste þæt is þæt he lufige his drihten mid ealre heortan and mid eallum mode and mid eallum mægne 
68.14.20: and oðer bebod is þysum ful gelic þæt is þæt ælc man lufige his nextan swa hine sylfne 
68.14.21: nu wenð manig man þæt þis sy gecweden be his freondum þe him gesibbeste synt and þurh mægrædenne nehste 
68.14.22: ac hit nis be ðam gesibban na swiðor gecweden þonne be ðam fremdan 
68.14.23: ac ure ælc is oðres nehsta þurh þone rihtan geleafan þe we to urum drihtne habbað 
68.14.24: and we synd gebroðru gecigede forðy þe god is ure fæder on heofonum 
68.14.25: þy cweð se hælend on his godspelle 
68.14.26: omnia quecumque uultis ut faciant uobis homines ita et uos facite illis 
68.14.27: ðæt is on urum geðeode 
68.14.28: don ge oðrum mannum ælc ðara þinga þe ge willan þæt hy eow don 
68.14.29: se man hæfð rihte lufe and soðe þe god lufað ma þonne hine sylfne and his nyhstan swa swa hine sylfne and nanum oðrum men ne deð butan swa swa he wile þæt man him do 
68.14.30: gif ge þonne þas ðreo forman bebodu on eower mode asettað and mid healicre arwurðnesse and mid micelum gefean onfon willað þonne magon ge mid godes fultume becuman to oðrum godum dædum 

68.14.31: nu is eac to witanne broðru þa leofestan þæt we scylon þas lenctentide mid swiðe micelre begymene healdan þæt we þurh nan þing nænne dæg þis fæsten ne abrecon ne on æte ne on drence butan sunnandagum on ðam nis nan fæsten geboden 
68.14.32: on oðre tide men gewuniað þæt hy þurh oðra manna lufe hwilum fæsten forlætað 
68.14.33: on þisse tide hit ne mæg nateshwon beon forlæten 
68.14.34: on oðre tide is on æghwylces mannes willan and on his scriftes dome gesett hu he for his synnum fæste 
68.14.35: on ðisse tide gif man ne ful fæst man forgymð godes agen bebod 
68.14.36: on oðerre tide se ðe wel fæst gestrynð him mede æt gode 
68.14.37: on þisse tide se ðe rihtlice ne fæst he gestrynð him hellewite butan hit for untrumnesse oððe for geogoðe sy 
68.14.38: forðam þe ure drihten gehalgode þas ilcan dagas þurh moysen and heliam and þurh hine sylfne þæt heora ælc gefæste xl daga and feowertig nihta 
68.14.39: eornostlice is to warnienne wið þæt þe manega men on gewunan habbað þæt synd þa ðe wenað þæt hy fæstende synd rihtlice gif hy etað sona swa hy þæt belltacen gehyrað þære nigoðan tide 
68.14.40: þæt is seo nontid 
68.14.41: ac hit nis na to gelyfanne þæt hy fæstende synd rihtlice butan hy æfter hyra mæssan þæs æfenes tid wurðunga gebiden 
68.14.42: ac þa þe þis fæsten ariht healdan willað þonne scylon hy ærest gan to heora scriftan and hym unforwandodlice his synna ealle geandettan þe he æfre fram geogoðe oð ðas elde gedon hæfð oððe on geþance oððe on worde oððe on weorce 
68.14.43: ealle be his wisunge gebetan and a geswican þonne beoð hy cristene gif hy þis eall gelæstað 
68.14.44: and hy scylon embe þa nigoðan tidæ heora mæssan gestandan 
68.14.45: and æfter þam heora æfenþenunga syððan don heora ælmessan swa forð swa him fyrmest gehagie 
68.14.46: and þonne mid miclum godes ege gangan to heora mete 
68.14.47: and þær don swa se hælend on þam godspelle beot and þus cweð 
68.14.48: cauete ne grauentur corda uestra in crapula et ebrietate 
68.14.49: ðæt is on englisc 
68.14.50: warniað þæt eowre heortan ne beon gehefegode on oferfylle and on druncennesse 
68.14.51: and se man þe næbbe of hwan he mæge rumlice ælmessan syllan ne onðracige he forðam þeh he lytel hæbbe þonne drihten us mede behet gif we þam þearfan þe ðæs neod bið geræcað cuppan fulle coles wæteres 
68.14.52: and forðy he cwæð be ðam colan wætere þæt nan man ne ðorfte hine beladian þæt he fæt næfde on hwy he hit wyrman mihte 

68.14.53: do gehwa georne on godes est be þam þe hine fyrmest onhagie and þæt is to witanne þeah ðe seo ælmesse on ælcne sæl god beo gedon 
68.14.54: huruþinga man sceal on þissum dagum hy dælan swa forð swa mannes mihta fyrmeste beoð þonne hit be ðam awriten is 
68.14.55: abscondite elemosinam in sinu pauperis et ipsa orat pro uobis ad dominum 
68.14.56: ðæt is on englisc 
68.14.57: behydað þa ælmessan on þæs þearfan greadan and heo gebit for eow to gode 
68.14.58: and ure drihten cweð eft on oðre stowe þurh his witegan 
68.14.59: frange esurigenti panem tuum et egenos uagosque induc in domum tuam 
68.14.60: he cweð brec þinne hlaf þam hingriendan and þam wædlan and þa ælþeodigan gelæd into þinum huse 
68.14.61: mid þam þe he cweð brec þinne hlaf he getacnað þæt þu scealt of þam þone þearfan aretan and þeah þu mare næbbe þonne ænne hlaf 

68.14.62: men ða leofestan hit nis na riht on cristenum folce þæt sume scylon mid oferæte and mid oferdrence beon oferlede and sume hungre cwylmede þonne we ealle synt anes drihtnes ðeowas and mid anum wyrðe gebohte 
68.14.63: þæt is mid his agenum blode þe he for eall mancyn ageat 
68.14.64: and gif we wel doð þonne mote we ealle to anre eadignesse becuman 
68.14.65: forhwi ne mot se ðearfa onfon þines metes þe mid ðe is to onfonne heofona rice 
68.14.66: hwi ne mot se ðearfa onfon þine ealdan gewæda þe mid ðe is to onfonne þæs undeadlican gegyrlan on neorxnawange 
68.14.67: hwi nis se wyrðe þæt he onfo þinra metelafe þe mid þe is to cumenne to engla gebeorscipe 

68.14.68: broðru þa leofostan utan us gegearwian on þyssum dagum þæt we synd on ðam mæran æriste ures drihtnes þurh clænnesse wlitige and fægere and þurh soðe lufan scinende 
68.14.69: and mid ælmesdædum aclænsode and mid eallum godum weorcum gefrætewode 
68.14.70: and ælce yrsunge and andan and hatunge and ealle yfelnesse utan aworpan fram urum heortum ealswa atter 
68.14.71: and gelomlice mid rihtum geleafan and mid godum willan to cyrcan cuman 
68.14.72: and þær mid micelre geornfulnesse æt gode biddan forgifennesse ealra þara gylta þe we dæghwamlice wyrcað 

68.14.73: and wite ælc man swiðe georne þæt he cunne his credan on ðam he sceal geandettan his rihtan geleafan 
68.14.74: and his paternoster on ðam he sceal him æt gode are biddan ge þisses lifes ge þæs towardan 
68.14.75: and ælcum men is swiðe to warnienne wið idele spræce and þæt man idele leoð ne singe on ðysum dagum þe ymbe hæðenscipe and ymbe galscipe geworhte syndon 
68.14.76: and næfre nan man ne geþristlæce ænigne deofles bigencg to donne ne on wiglunge ne on wiccedome ne on ænegum idelum anginne 
68.14.77: and þæt nan man ne sece to nanre wellan ne to nanum stane ne to nanum treowe ne nan man his cild þurh þa eorðan ne teo 
68.14.78: forðam se ðe þæt deð he betæcð þæt cild eallum deoflum and seo moder forð mid 
68.14.79: ne nan man nane neode ne sece butan to gode sylfum and to his halgum 

68.14.80: ac þonne ge to cyrican cumen þonne bringe ge eowre ælmessan to godes weofode swa eow fyrmest gehagie 
68.14.81: forðam drihten cweð 
68.14.82: non apparebis in conspectu meo uacuus 
68.14.83: ðæt is on englisc ne ætyw þu þe on minre gesihðe idelhende 
68.14.84: and doð gelomlice eowre andetnesse eowrum scrifte 
68.14.85: and hine biddað þæt he eow to gode þingie þæt ge motan eowra synna forgyfnesse geearnian 

68.14.86: men ða leofestan to hwy synd we elles on ðisne middaneard acenned butan þæt we scylan mid micelre eadmodnesse urum drihtne hyran 
68.14.87: and swa hwæt swa we þurh ungewis oððe þurh hwylce dysignesse gedon habban þæt eadmodlice mid micelre hreowsunga and mid godum dædan betan þa hwile þe us god þæs fyrstes geunne 
68.14.88: healdan we nu georne godes æ and his bebodu and þam þærto wisien þe us underþeodde synt 
68.14.89: forðam þe ure drihten behet þam þe hine lufiað and his bebodu healdað þæt hy motan æfter þam æriste habban ece lif and neorxnawanges gefean mid fæder and mid suna and mid halgum gaste a worulda woruld a butan ende amen
