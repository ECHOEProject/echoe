69.10.1: we secgeað urum cynehlaforde and eallum folce cyðan wyllað þæt is ærest þæt we ealle to gode ælmihtigum georne gebugan and his beboda healdan 
69.10.2: and unrihtes ealle geswican and to ælcan rihte us sylfe wenian and weman 
69.10.3: and don swa us þearf is andettan ure synna and betan georne 
69.10.4: and þanonforð geswican swa we geornost magon and aa urum hlaforde holdlice and rihtlice hyran 
69.10.5: and þæt godes circan beon beteran mæðe and munde wyrðe þonne hig ær þysan wæron 
69.10.6: þæt is þæt cyricgrið binnan wagum and gehalgodes cyninges handgrið stande efenunwemme 
69.10.7: and cristenum cyninge gebyrað on cristenre þeode þæt he sy eallswa riht is folces frofer and rihtwis hyrde ofer cristene heorde 
69.10.8: and him gebyreð þæt he eallum mægene cristendom rære and godes cyrcean æghwar fyrðrie 
69.10.9: and fryðie and godes are mundie æfre and eall cristen folc sybbie and sehte mid rihtre lage swa he geornost mæge 
69.10.10: and þurh ælc þingc rihtwisnesse lufige for gode and for worulde 
69.10.11: forþam þurh þæt he sceal sylf fyrmest geþeon and his þeodscipe eac swa þe he riht lufie for gode and for worulde 
69.10.12: and him gebyrað þæt he geornlice fylste þam þe riht willan and a hetelice styre þam þe þwyres wyllan 
69.10.13: he sceal mandæde men þreagean þearle mid woroldlicre steore 
69.10.14: and he sceal morðwyrhtan hlafordswican and manswaran mannslagan and mægslagan cyrichatan and sacerdbanan hadbrecan and æwbrecan þeofas and þeodsceaþan ryperas and reaferas leogeras and liceteras wedlogan and wærlogan hatian and hynan and eallum godes feondum styrnlice wiðstandan 
69.10.15: and ægðer he sceal beon mid rihte ge milde ge reðe 
69.10.16: milde þam godum and styrne þam yfelum 
69.10.17: la þurh hwæt sceal godes þeowum and godes þearfum frið and fultum cuman butan þurh crist and þurh cristenne cyningc 
69.10.18: þi he sceal boclarum hlystan swyðe georne and godes beboda geornlice healdan and gelome wið wytan wisdom smeagean gif he gode wyle rihtlice hyran 
69.10.19: ælc riht cynestol stent on þrym stapelum þe fullice ariht stent 
69.10.20: an is oratores and oðer is laboratores and þrydde is bellatores 
69.10.21: oratores syndon gebedmen þe gode sceolon þeowian dæges and nihtes for þæne cyngc and for ealne þeodscipe þingian georne 
69.10.22: laboratores syndon weorcmen þe tilian sceolon þæs þe eall þeodscipe big sceal lybban 
69.10.23: bellatores syndon wigmen þe eard sculon werian wiglice mid wæpnon 
69.10.24: on þysum þrym stapelum sceal ælc cynestol standan mid rihte 
69.10.25: and awacyge heora ænig sona se stol scylfð 
69.10.26: and fulberste heora ænig þonne hryst se stol nyðer and þæt wyrð þære þeode eall to unþearfe 
69.10.27: ac stalige man and strangie and trymme hi georne mid wislicre godes lage and mid rihtlicre woroldlage þæt wyrð þam þeodscipe to langsuman ræde 
69.10.28: and soð is þæt we secgað awacyge se cristendom sona scylfð se cynedom 
69.10.29: and we cyðað eac eorlan and heretogan deman and gerefan þæt hig agan þearfe þæt hi georne unrihtes geswican and rihtwisnysse lufian for gode and for worolde 
69.10.30: and nahwar þurh undom for feo ne for freondscipe forgyman heora wisdom swa þæt hig wendan unriht to rihte oððon undom gedeman earmum to yrmðe 
69.10.31: ac þæt hig gehadedum mannum fylstan bet to godes lagum þonne hig ær þysum dydon 
69.10.32: hit wæs nu lange þæt wæron to wide godes laga laðe and lara forsawne 
69.10.33: and woroldlaga syndan innan þysan earde wraðe forhwyrfde on æghwylcan ende 
69.10.34: and þæt is gesyne wide and side þæt man god gremede mid þam unrihte ealles to lange 
69.10.35: betan þa nu georne þa þe þyssere þeode nu sceolan rædan swa swa god wyle gif hig gode willan rihtlice cweman and on þam myclan dæge heom sylfum gebeorgan bet þonne þa dydon þe beforan wæron þe unriht arærdan to forwyrde heom sylfum 
69.10.36: and we eac þæs habbað fela byrsta and bysmora gebiden 
69.10.37: and gif we ænige bote gebidan sceolan þonne mote we þæs to gode earnian bet þonne we ær þysan dydan 
69.10.38: forþam ær þysan wæs gehwar swicdom swyðra þonne wisdom 
69.10.39: and þuhte hwilum wisast se þe wæs swicolost 
69.10.40: and se þe lytelicost cuðe leaslice hiwian unsoð to soðe and undom deman oðrum to hynðe 
69.10.41: ac wa heom þæs wærscipes and ealles þæs weorðscipes 
69.10.42: ne dohte hit ær þysan inne ne ute ac wæs here and hunger bryne and blodgyte on gewelhwylcan ende oft and gelome 
69.10.43: and us stalu and cwalu hol and hete and rypera reaflac derede swiðe þearle 
69.10.44: and eac us unwederu foroft weoldon unwæstma 
69.10.45: forþam on þison earde wæs and git is swa hit þincan mæg unrihta fela and tealte getrywða æghwar mid mannon 
69.10.46: fela syn forsworene and swyðe forlogone 
69.10.47: and wedd eac abrocene oft and gelome 
69.10.48: and þæt is gesyne on þisse þeode þæt us godes yrre hetelice onsæt 
69.10.49: gecnawe se þe cunne 
69.10.50: ac uton don swa us þearf is beon ymbe þa bote geswican unrihtes and gebugan to rihte 
69.10.51: þonne geearnige we gif we þæt wyllað sona godes mildse 
69.10.52: and riht is eac þæt godes þeowas biscopas and abbodas munecas and mynecena preostas and nunnan regole fylgean and regollice lybban oððon woruldare þolian 
69.10.53: and þæt þa þe rihte heora lif lybban þæt man þa georne fyrðrige and wurðige 
69.10.54: and þæt þa utlagan weorþan þe to godes rihte gebugan nellan buton hig to rihte gecirran 
69.10.55: ac gebuge manna gehwylc georne to rihte 
69.10.56: and muneca gehwylc into his mynstre oððe of earde mid ealle gewite 
69.10.57: and riht is þæt ænige wæpnmen on mynecena beodderne ne etan ne ne drincan ne læwede men on muneca buton hit mid urum hlaforde sy 
69.10.58: oððon elles hwylc þe maran godes ege habbe þæt hit for his neaweste þe betere beo for gode and for worolde and hyra regol huru ne sy a ðe awyrdra 
69.10.59: and preostas and nunnan heora lif rihtan be godes miltse and be heora are 
69.10.60: and ealle godes þeowas we biddað and lærað and huruþinga mæssepreostas þæt hig gode hyran and heora clænnysse healdan and beorgan heom sylfum wið godes yrre and wið þone weallendan bryne þe wealleð on helle 
69.10.61: full georne hig witan þæt hig nagon mid rihte þurh hæmedþing wifes gemanan 
69.10.62: ac hit is þe wyrse þe sume habbað twa oððe ma 
69.10.63: and sume forlætað þa hig ær hæfdon and be lifiendre cwenan eft oðre nimað 
69.10.64: swa ænigum cristenum men ne gedafenað to donne ne huru þam gehadedum þe oðrum mannum riht scylan bysnian and unriht forbeodan 
69.10.65: nu þincð heom þis syllic to gehyranne forþam þe hig habbað heora yrmðe swa on gewunan gebroht swylce hit nan pleoh ne sy þæt mæssepreost lybbe his lif swa swa ceorl 
69.10.66: crist sylf astealde þa clænnysse and his halgan apostolas þe him her on life folgedan þe we godes þeowas healdan scilan 
69.10.67: hit gewearð æfter cristes þrowunge and æfter his æriste of deaðe and his upstige to heofenan and æfter þæra apostola and martyra þrowungæ þæt wearð se soða geleafa swiðe acolad þurh deoflice gedwolmen id est arrianus presbiter et macedonius episcopus et multi alii 
69.10.68: þa gewearð hit þæt constantinus se mæra casere and se æðe la sancte elena sunu þære eadigan cwene feng to rice on romana byrig and he rices weold ofer ealne middaneard 
69.10.69: þa gegaderode he swyðe mycelne synoð on þære ceastre þe is nicea genamod for trymminge rihtes geleafan ymbe þa halgan þrynnesse and ymbe cristes menniscnysse 
69.10.70: se forma sinoð wæs swa swa we sædon on nicea ccc and xviii bisceopa and se oðer wæs syððan on constantinopolim cl bisceopa and se þridda wæs on efesum cc bisceopa and se feorða wæs on calcedonia dc bisceopa and xxx sacerda 
69.10.71: and ealle hig wæron haliges lifes menn 
69.10.72: þas feower sinoðas syndon to healdenne swa swa þa feower cristesbec and hig eac gesettan ealle þa þenunge þe we habbað to godes þeowdome to mæssan and to uhtsange and to eallum tidsangum 
69.10.73: siððan wæron foroft oðre sinoðas wisra bisceopa wide on worolde ac þas wæron þa fyrmestan 
69.10.74: and ealle hig forbudon æfre ælc wiflac weofodþenum þæt is bisceopum and mæssepreostum 
69.10.75: ðencan þa nu þe to þam þriste syn þæt hig god oferseoð and swa mæniges haliges mannes dom swa on þyssum sinoðum gesomnode wæron and gehwar syððan hwylces leanes hig him wenan magon and eac wenan ne þurfon 
69.10.76: ac witod witan þæt hig yfel lean habban scylan buton hig geswican and grimlice godes graman þurh þæt þe hig swa god gremiað þæt hig heora lif eall libbað on unriht 
69.10.77: ac to fela is þæra þe ær þisan misdydan 
69.10.78: and se þe þæs geswican wille and clænnysse lufian habbe he godes miltse and to woruldwurðscipe sy he þegenlage wyrðe 
69.10.79: and riht is gif hwæt færlices on þeode becymð 
69.10.80: beon hit hereræsas beon hit færcwealmas beon hit miswyderu oððon unwæstmas 
69.10.81: beo swa hwæt swa hit beo sece man þa bote aa to gode sylfum 
69.10.82: and þæt ure hlaford læte asmeagean be æghwylcum ende englalandes ealle þa manfullan þe nellað geswican and þances oððe unþances hig to rihte gebigean oððe mid ealle of earde adrifan 
69.10.83: and we lærað and biddað and on godes naman beodað þæt ænig cristenman bynnan syx manna sibfæce æfre ne gewifige on his agenum cynne ne on his mæges lafe þe swa neahsib wære ne on þæs wifes nydmagan þe he sylf ær hæfde 
69.10.84: ne on his gefæderan ne on gehalgodre nunnan ne on ælætan ænig cristenman ne gewifige æfre 
69.10.85: ne na ma wifa þonne an hæbbe 
69.10.86: ac beo be þære anre þa hwile þe heo lybbe 
69.10.87: se þe wylle godes lage giman mid rihte and wið hellebryne beorgan his sawle 
69.10.88: wa þam þe woh drifð ealles to lange buton he geswice 
69.10.89: and sy ælc wydewe þe hig sylfe mid rihte gehealde on godes griðe and on þæs cynges and sytte ælc werleas xii monað ceose syððan þæt heo sylf wylle 
69.10.90: and eac we beodað on godes ælmihtiges naman and on ealra his halgena þæt nan man swa dyrstig ne sy þæt on gehadodre nunnan oððe on mynecenan gewifige 
69.10.91: and gif hit hwa gedon hæbbe beo he utlah wið god and amansumad fram eallum cristendome and wið þone cyningc scyldig ealles þæs þe he age buton he þe raðor geswice and þe deoplicor gebete wið god 
69.10.92: and se þe eard wille rihtlice clænsian and unriht alecgan and rihtwisnysse lufian þonne mot he georne þyllices styran and þyllic ascunian 
69.10.93: and þæt man geornlice beo on æghwylcum ende ymbe fryðes bote and ymbe feos bote 
69.10.94: swa ymbe friðes bote swa þam bondan sy selost and þam þeofan sy laðost 
69.10.95: and swa ymbe feos bote þæt an mynet gange ofer ealle þas þeode butan ælcon false 
69.10.96: and woge gemeta and false gewihta rihte man georne 
69.10.97: and ælces unrihtes heononforð geswice and þæt gehwilc man his teoðunge rihtlice gelæste be godes miltse and be þæs cynges and be ealles cristenes folces and be þære steore þe eadgar gelagede 
69.10.98: and romfeoh gelæste man æghwilce geare be peteres mæssan 
69.10.99: and se þe þæt ne gelæste sylle þærtoeacan xxx peninga to rome and gylde þam cynge on engla lage hundteontig scillinga 
69.10.100: and freolsa and fæstena healde man rihtlice 
69.10.101: sunnandæges freols fram sæterdæges none oð monandæges lihtinge healde man georne 
69.10.102: and cypinge and folcgemota we forbeodað þam halgan dæge 
69.10.103: and ymbrenfæstena healde man rihte swa swa sanctus gregorius angelcynne sylf hit gedihte 
69.10.104: and ealle godes gerihta fyrðrige man georne 
69.10.105: ælc riht arære and unriht afylle 
69.10.106: þonne wyrð þysse þeode sona god milde 
69.10.107: and þæt we ealle gemænelice gehadede and læwede anræde weorðan for gode and for worolde 
69.10.108: and habban us gemæne sibbe and some æfter godes rihte 
69.10.109: and ælce sace sehtan swa we eornost magon and ne lætan us deofol dwelian ne twæman swa swa he oft dyde þonne ure gegadervnga ær þyson wæran 
69.10.110: ac uton ealle anrædlice to gode bugan and hine lufian and wurðian 
69.10.111: and urne cristendom rihtlice healdan and urne cynehlaford mid rihtan getrywðan and ælcne hæðendom mid ealle awyrpan 
69.10.112: and utan geþencean þæt hit is nyr þisse worulde ende þonne hwa gelyfan wylle and þæs deofles timan antecristes 
69.10.113: ac deofol dwelað manna geþohtas þæt hig to lyt þærymbe þenceað 
69.10.114: and gelyfe se þe wylle we witan to soðe þæt hit þærto georne genealæcð 
69.10.115: forþam þeos worold is sorhful and fram dæge to dæge a swa leng swa wyrse 

69.10.116: hwæt we magon geseon hu læne and hu lyðre þis lif is 
69.10.117: hu sarlic and hu sorhful and hu geswincful and hu teonful 
69.10.118: hu tealt and hu wrað hu wiðerweard and hu swicful 
69.10.119: heo is þeos woruld on ofstum and on stormum 
69.10.120: and on adlum and on ungewyderum 
69.10.121: þæt nu ne beoð naht fela manna ætsamne þæt heora sum ne si seoc and samhal 
69.10.122: and þeah þæt gelimpe þæt men sume hwile syn her on worolde swaþeah hig beoð aa on geswince and mid sorge 
69.10.123: forþan þeos woruld nære wyrðe þæt man to hire lufe hæfde ealles to swiðe 
69.10.124: ðy us is mycel þearf þe to godes bydelum gesette syn þæt we godes folc warnian gelome wið þone egsan þe mannum is towyrd þæt is se þeodfeond antichristus þe læs þe hig unwære wurðan aredode and þonne to hrædlice þurh deofol beswicene 
69.10.125: la hwæt is se man on life buton hine god ælmihtig gehealde and he ær þe bet gewarad sy þæt he þonne þurh deofol beswicen ne wurþe 
69.10.126: la hwylc wundor bið þeh se mennisca deofol synfullum mote heardlice derian 
69.10.127: þonne god geþafað þæt he mot on his agenum halgum swylc wundor gewyrcean þæt enoh and elias þurh þone þeodfeond gemartirode weorðað 
69.10.128: þe god silfa fela hund wintra mid sawle and mid lichaman geheold ær 
69.10.129: to þam anan þæt hig þonne sceoldan mid heora lare folce gebeorgan þæt hit eall ne forwurde endemes togædere þurh þæne deofol þe ealle men bregeð and ealle worold gedrefeð 
69.10.130: nis se man on life þe mæge oððe cunne swa yfel hit asecgan swa hit sceal gewurðan on þam deoflican timan 

69.10.131: on godes naman we biddað þæt cristenra manna gehwilc hine sylfne georne beþence and warnige wið þone egsan and geornlice to gode gebuge and fram synnum gecirre swa he geornost mæge 
69.10.132: and geearnige þæt he gemanan habban mote on hefena rice 
69.10.133: þær is ece blis and æfre bið mid þam þe leofað and rixað a butan ende amen
