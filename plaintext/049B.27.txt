de uirtutibus 

49B.27.1: ðonne syndon eahta healice mægnu þurh godes mihte mannum gescifte 
49B.27.2: þæt is i eadmodnes ii sifernes iii clænnes iiii rumheortnes v modþwærnes vi anrædnes vii glædnes viii sibgeornes 
49B.27.3: mid þissan mægnan we us sculon werian and þurh godes fultum deofol oferwinnan and his unðeawan fæste wiðstandan 
49B.27.4: and utan don swa us þearf is 
49B.27.5: warnian us wið deofol 
49B.27.6: and geðencan swiðe georne oft and gelome hwæt we behetan þa we fulluht underfengon oððe þa þe æt fontbaðe ure foresprecan wæron
