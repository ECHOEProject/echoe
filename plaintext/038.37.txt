in quarta feria in letania maiore 

38.37.1: men ða leofestan cwæð se halga lareow 
38.37.2: hwæt we gemunan magon þæt we oft gehyrdon secgan 
38.37.3: þæt wise men þurh haliges gastes gyfe gesetton us þas halgan gangdagas þry to fæstene and on to gangenne ure sawle to þearfe æfter þære halgan drihtnes rode and æfter his halgan reliquium 
38.37.4: þa ðe nu forð borene syndon geond ealne middaneard fram geleaffullum mannum 
38.37.5: togeanes his halgan upstige þe nu on þunresdæg byð 
38.37.6: þæt he his þone halgan lichaman ahof up fram eorðan on heofona hihþo 
38.37.7: and he for ure lufan her on life deað þrowude 
38.37.8: and þurh his mægenþrymm he eft on ærest aras 
38.37.9: and his leorningcnihtum hine sylfne æteowde 
38.37.10: and hi myngode þære gastlican lare þe he him ær his þrowunga sæde 

38.37.11: men þa leofestan þreo and þrittig wintra on þyssum middanearde he wæs 
38.37.12: and æghwilce gode bysene he us onstealde 
38.37.13: and forðan mid menniscum lichaman he hyne gegyrede ofer his godcundnysse þæt he þurh þæt mannum forgæfe þæt hi þe eðelicor ingang hæfdon to heofona rice 
38.37.14: forðan we sceolon biddan hine for his þære halgan rode weorþunga and his upstige to heofonum 
38.37.15: and ealle his halegan we sceolon eac biddan þæt hi mid us bidden ælmihtigne drihten þone ecan cyning þæt he us gescylde wið gedwolan and deofolgyld and wið hæðene þeoda þe us ormætlice swiðe mid heregylde and mid werudgryre ofsett habbað 
38.37.16: and wið arleasum hungre and heregungge 
38.37.17: and þæt he us sibbe forgyfe and smyltnysse lyf 
38.37.18: and uton we hine eac biddan þæt he us gescylde wið grimnysse myssenlicra yfela and wita þara þe he on middangeard sendeð for manna synnum 
38.37.19: to þyssum dagum þurh haligne gast þys fæsten and þes gang geseted wæs 
38.37.20: swa we oft on bocum gehyrdon secgan þæt we mid þære halegan cristes þrynnysse rode and mid his halegra reliquium þe we mid gangað and mid haligum sangum we sceolon bletsian ure land 
38.37.21: and drihten biddan þæt þa wæstmas þe on eorðan syndon geþeon moton mannum to gode and brice 
38.37.22: uton we nu hine georne biddan nu he us forgeaf þæt we þisse tide gebidan moston þæt he his yrre fram us ahwyrfe 
38.37.23: and his mildheortnysse us to læte on eallum urum life ge on þysum andweardan ge on þam toweardan 

38.37.24: men ða leofestan gehyrað lifes bebodu 
38.37.25: and þa ecan lare lustlice onfoð 
38.37.26: and ongytað þone cwyde þæs soðan deman þe we bedydrian ne magon 
38.37.27: forðan drihten þus cwæð 
38.37.28: se ðe forhigð þone lareow ondræde he him þone heofonlican deman 
38.37.29: þæt is drihten god 
38.37.30: hys leorneras wæron lareowas genemnede 
38.37.31: forðan hi us þa halgan lare of godes bocum secgað 
38.37.32: and þæs heofoncundan cyninges tocyme bodiað 
38.37.33: forðan ðe he cymð to demenne eallum middanearde 
38.37.34: and forðan þone soðan deman urne drihten we us ondrædan sceolon 
38.37.35: and for his dome we sceolon forhtian 
38.37.36: and þone micclan and þone langan domesdæg warnien we us ær ðan þe he cume 
38.37.37: and tiligen we georne þæt we þonne gemette syn on godum dædum 
38.37.38: forðan þe þæt is se micela drihtnes domesdæg and ealles mancynnes 
38.37.39: þæt ys yrmðe dæg and gnornunge dæg 
38.37.40: and unrotnysse dæg and granunge dæg 
38.37.41: and wanunge dæg and nearonysse dæg 
38.37.42: and wopes dæg and biternysse dæg and earfoðnysse dæg 
38.37.43: and cirmes dæg and se grimma dæg and gesamnunge dæg heofonwara and eorðwara and hellwara 
38.37.44: þæt is gewryxles dæg lichamana and sawla 
38.37.45: and ne byð nænig mægð þæs micclan mancynnes gegaderod on þam dæge þæt hine þær gescildan mæge 
38.37.46: ne snytero ne gleawnys ne nænig man nah swa micel rice on þysum middanearde þæt he hine þær beladian mæge beforan andweardnysse þære halegan þrynnysse 
38.37.47: ðæt is cyðnysse dæg mancynnes þurh gesceafte fyres and wæteres and windes and þunurada swiðe strange 
38.37.48: þæt ys bymana dæg and hire hleoðrunga 
38.37.49: þisne dæg we us on micelre styrunge toweardne ongytan magon be mænigfealdum þingum þe us nu oft and gelome cnyssað 
38.37.50: be ðam dæge drihten sylf cwæð þæt ariseð þeod wið þeode and rice wið ricum 
38.37.51: þonne bið þær þeoda geþring and mænigfeald hreownys geond mænige stowa 
38.37.52: þas eorðlican tacnu we nu gewordene oft sceawiað 
38.37.53: þonne gewurþað þas tacnu seofon dagum ær þam dome 
38.37.54: on ðam ærestan domesdæges byð micel stefen gehyred of þære heofonan tungle se hatte fyrmamentum 
38.37.55: and blodig wolcn astigþ norðan 
38.37.56: and micele þunerada beoþ gehyrde 
38.37.57: and lygetu bliccetað 
38.37.58: and þæt wolcn bærneð ealne heofon 
38.37.59: and hit þonne onginneð rinan blodegan rene 
38.37.60: on þam æfteran dæge stefen byð gehyred of heofonum 
38.37.61: and eorðe byð onhrered 
38.37.62: and heofoncund leoht oferhæfð ealne middaneard oð ðæs dæges æfen 
38.37.63: on þam þriddan dæge æt þære æfteran tide dæges on heofonum æteoweð fyren tacen 
38.37.64: and of eorðan deopnysse astigð sweflen lyg 
38.37.65: and æt þam feower healfum þyses middaneardes se heofon tobyrsteð 
38.37.66: and micel sweg cymð and gesweorcð 
38.37.67: and of helle astihþ dimnysse and fulnysse stenc and oferhæfð þas eorðan ane tid dæges 
38.37.68: þonne ongytað synfulle men hyra forwyrd and forwyrhtu 
38.37.69: on ðam feorðan dæge fram norðdæle middaneardes micel hream astihþ hellegasta 
38.37.70: þonne fylð eall hæþenra manna deofolgyld on þam dæge 
38.37.71: on þam fiftan dæge æt þære fiftan tide dæges samnunga cumað þunerada swiðe micele 
38.37.72: and stormas fealleð niðer of heofonum 
38.37.73: and þeostre lyft byð ofhrered 
38.37.74: þonne ealle þeoda wiðsacað þisse worulde 
38.37.75: and hi ongytað drihtnes mihte and his tocyme 
38.37.76: on þam sixtan dæge æt þære sixtan tide dæges þes heofon tohlit fram eastdæle oð ðone westdæl 
38.37.77: and eall engla werod cymð ofer eorðan 
38.37.78: and scadað þa soðfæstan men fram þam arleasan 
38.37.79: þonne þa arleasan men fleoð þæt heofoncunde werud hi sylfe to behydenne on dunum and on beorgum 
38.37.80: and cweðað to him untyn ðu þe la eorðe and forswelg us þe læs þe we fundene beon 
38.37.81: on þam seofoðan dæge æt þære seofoðan tide dæges byð domesdæges tacn 
38.37.82: and æt þam feower healfum þyses middaneardes feower englas standað and blawað feower byman 
38.37.83: þonne befealt þes heofon togædere swa man boc betyne 
38.37.84: and seo sunne byð onwended on þeostru and se mona on blod 
38.37.85: and steorran of heofonum feallað 
38.37.86: and eall heofona mægen onhrered bið 
38.37.87: drihten cymð þonne on micclum mægenþrymme 
38.37.88: and fyr on his onsyne scinð 
38.37.89: and on his ymbhwyrfte byð swiðe micel hrernyss 
38.37.90: þonne arisað ealle þa men þa ðe mid gebregednyssum on deaðe swulton fram þam feower healfum þyses middaneardes 
38.37.91: þæt syndon þa ðe on þysum life on fyre forbærnede wæron oððe on wætere adrencte wæron 
38.37.92: oððe on rode ahangene wæron oððe on morðe ofslagene wæron 
38.37.93: oððe wildeor fræton oððe fugelas tobæron 
38.37.94: ealle þa þonne æt þære byman stefne arisað and þurh fyres leoman to godes dome gað 
38.37.95: þær þonne standað þusend þusend engla and micel mægen heahengla and ealle halige 
38.37.96: and soðfæste godes witegan and heahfæderas and apostolas 
38.37.97: þonne ætyweð drihten þa rode þe he on þrowode on þisse ylcan tide for us 
38.37.98: and þær scineð leoht ofer ealne middaneard 
38.37.99: and he ætyweð þa wunda on his sidan 
38.37.100: and þæra nægla swaða on his handum þe he mid wæs on rode gefæstnod for urum lufum 
38.37.101: þonne cwyð se eca cyning to anra gehwylcum þus 
38.37.102: men ða leofestan sege me hwæt geþohtest þu oððe hwæt gecwæde þu oððe hwæt gedydest þu on þinum life 
38.37.103: syle wed be þysum eallum þe ic for ðe dyde and for ðe þrowude 
38.37.104: þonne andswarað se man urum drihtne and cwyð 
38.37.105: næbbe ic ænig wedd to syllenne buton mine sawle 
38.37.106: þonne byð sum boc untyned on ansyne þæs hehstan cyninges 
38.37.107: on ðære beoð awritene æghwilces mannes dæda eal þæt he to gode oððe to yfele gedyde on þysum middanearde 
38.37.108: þær se broðor ne mæg þam oðrum helpan ne se fæder þam suna ne þa neahmagas 
38.37.109: ne ða maðmgestreon ne þysse woruldæhta ænigne man þær gescyldan magon 
38.37.110: ac drihten gyldeð anra gehwylcum menn æfter his sylfes gewyrhtum 
38.37.111: þonne soðfæste and gecorene men forð berað heora wurca hyrsumnysse 
38.37.112: and drihtnes halige martyras heora þrowunga and þæra carcerna nearonyssa 
38.37.113: and mænige earfoðu þe hi adrugon for drihtnes naman 

38.37.114: munecas bærað heora hyrsumnysse and forwyrnednysse þyssa woruldlicra þinga 
38.37.115: and heora þa singalan wæccan and þa drihtenlican bebodu and heora þa gastlican þeowdomas 
38.37.116: læwede men þa ðe her on worulde rihtlice heora lif libbað hi bærað heora ælmysdæda and hluttor lif and clæne on ansyne þæs heahstan scyppendes 

38.37.117: and þonne cwyð se eca cyning to ðam godum 
38.37.118: cume ge gebletsode and onfoð heofona rices wuldre 
38.37.119: þonne þa arleasan and þa synfullan hi bærað nearowne wæstm and sceandfulne on ansyne þæs hehstan scippendes 
38.37.120: þonne cwyð se heofona cyning fare ge fram me awyrgede on þa ecan susle hellegrundes 
38.37.121: and hi þonne ahwyrfað fram haligra manna dreame 
38.37.122: and swiðe heofigende hellewitu secað 
38.37.123: þær is deað buton life and þystru butan leohte 
38.37.124: and hreownys butan wæstmum and sar buton frofre and yrmðu butan ende 
38.37.125: þær ne ongyt se fæder þone sunu ne se sunu þone fæder ne wyrðað 
38.37.126: ne seo dohtor þa modor ne lufað 
38.37.127: ne seo modor þære dehter ne miltsað 
38.37.128: ac anra gehwilc hys sylfes yrmða wepað and heofað 
38.37.129: forðon ðe hellefyr næfre ne byð adwæsced 
38.37.130: ac þa dracan and þa wyrmas þæra arleasra manna sawla slitað 
38.37.131: and hi næfre na beoð sweltende 
38.37.132: þær is eagena wop and toða gristbitung 
38.37.133: and þær is welera þurst and wita stow 
38.37.134: of þysum tintregum men ða leofestan tiligen we us to gescildenne and us to gewarnigenne þa hwile þe we lifes leoht habban 
38.37.135: þe læs þe us færunga þas þeostru forgripun 
38.37.136: and mid geornfullum mode tiligen we ure sylfra swa lange swa we lybbon on þysum life 
38.37.137: mid godum dædum to gewyrcenne þæt we heofona rice gegearnian mid urum drihtne and eallum his halgum 
38.37.138: þær is ece blis and engla sang ungeswyðrod 
38.37.139: and unasecgendlic gefea and godes lof 
38.37.140: and unawendedlic wynsumnyss and seo soðe fægernys 

38.37.141: swa us cydde sanctus iohannes se drihtnes dyrling 
38.37.142: se sceawode heofona rices wuldor and he on heofonum wæs 
38.37.143: and he cwæð ic geseo niwne heofon and niwe eorðan and þa halgan ceastre hierusalem 
38.37.144: seo wæs fram gode gefrætewod and heo wæs befangen mid swiðe micclon wealle 
38.37.145: and twælf gatu wæron on þære ceastre 
38.37.146: þa wæron geworhte of twelf cynna gymmum 
38.37.147: and seo ceaster wæs ymbseald mid godes beorhtnysse 
38.37.148: and hire staðol wæs of eallum deorwurðum stanum gefrætewod 
38.37.149: and hire worðigas wæron þæs hluttestran goldes 
38.37.150: and on ðære ceastre sunne ne lihteð ne mona 
38.37.151: ac godes beorhtnys hi onlihteð 
38.37.152: and seo beorhtnys wæs þæt soðe lamb 
38.37.153: þæt wæs se ælmihtiga drihten 
38.37.154: þær ineodon ealle þeoda and eorðcyningas 
38.37.155: hi comon and gesawon godes wuldor and his mægenþrym 
38.37.156: and hi sealdon gode weorþunga 
38.37.157: and æfter þan cwæð sanctus iohannes 
38.37.158: ic geseah micele men of eallum þeodum 
38.37.159: þa wæron beswapene ealle mid hwitum hræglum 
38.37.160: and clypedon and cwædon si hælo urum drihtne and þanc þe sytteð ofer þysum heahsetle 

38.37.161: forðon us is micel neodþearf men ða leofestan þæt we biddan þa heofoncundan gesamnunga þæt hi us eac þingion to ðam ælmihtigan drihtne and sanctus michael þone heahengel 
38.37.162: utan we us on fultum cygen se ys hyrde neorxnawanges and ealdor ebrea þeode 
38.37.163: and æghwilces godes mannes sawle he geweald hæfð 
38.37.164: and he næfre hi forlæt ær he hi gebringe beforan drihtnes hehsetle 
38.37.165: þær is andweardnys ealra haligra and seo ece blis unasecgendlic on heofona rice 
38.37.166: and þæt is þæt hea rice and þæt herigendlice 
38.37.167: and þæt smylte and þæt gesibsume þæt god his haligum gegyred hæfð and mid hys mægenþrymme gefrætewod 
38.37.168: men ða leofestan herien we nu þone ælmihtigan drihtnn 
38.37.169: and lufien we hine and wurðigen 
38.37.170: he is cyning ealra cyninga 
38.37.171: and he ys scyppend ealra gesceafta gesewenlicra and ungesewenlicra 
38.37.172: and he is fægrost ealra blostma 
38.37.173: and he is snytro þære soðan lufe 
38.37.174: he ys engla symbelnys 
38.37.175: and he is wuldor þæra eadigra apostola 
38.37.176: and he is leoht þæra haligra martyra 
38.37.177: and he is neorxnawanges ece gefea 
38.37.178: and he is hælo ealra untrumra 
38.37.179: and he is alysend þæra gehæftendra 
38.37.180: and he is ealra þinga leoht and ealra tida 
38.37.181: bidden we nu men ða leofestan urne drihten wuldres cyning þæt he us gehwyrfe a to his willan 
38.37.182: and he si ure gemiltsigend and ure frefrigend for his mæran mihte and mildheortnysse 
38.37.183: and þæt he us gelæde þær we on þam ecan gefean moton mid him 
38.37.184: and mid eallum halegum wunian on wuldre and on wurðmynte 
38.37.185: he is drihten sylfa se ðe leofað and rixað mid suna and mid þam halgan gaste a buton ende amen
