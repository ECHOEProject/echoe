be gehadedum mannum 

338.7.1: gehadedum mannum gebyreð ælc clænnes 
338.7.2: forðam þe hi sculon eallum oðrum mannum ælce unclænnesse forbeodan and ælce clænnesse 
338.7.3: gif hi riht doð hi sculon be heom sylfum geornost gebysnian 
338.7.4: þonne is hit swyþe egeslic þæt ða þe sceoldan eallum cristenum mannum riht bodian and eac wel bysnian þæt hi syn sume gewordene bysen to forwyrde swyðor þonne to þearfe 
338.7.5: þæt syndon þa æwbrecan þe þurh healicne had cyricæwe underfengan and syððan þæt abræcan 
338.7.6: nis nanum weofodþene alyfed þæt he wifian mote ac is ælcum forboden 
338.7.7: nu is þeah þæra ealles to fela þe þone æwbryce wyrcað and geworht habbað 
338.7.8: ac ic bidde for godes lufan and eac eorne stlice beode þæt man þæs geswice 
338.7.9: læwedum men is ælc wif forboden butan his rihtæwe 
338.7.10: gehadode syndon sume swa þurh deofol beswicene þæt hi wifiað on unriht and forwyrcað hi sylfe þurh ðone æwbryce þe hi on wuniað 
338.7.11: ac ic bidde georne þæt man þæs morðes heononforð georne geswice 
338.7.12: cyrice is sacerdes æwe 

338.7.13: constantinus se mæra casere gesamnode swyðe mycelne sinoð on þære ceastre nicea for trymmincge rihtes geleafan 
338.7.14: on ðam sinoðe wæron ccc and xviii biscopa of manegum leodscipum gegaderode 
338.7.15: and hi þær geswuteloden rihtne geleafan and gesettan þa þæs to swutelunge þone mæssancredan þe man wide singð 
338.7.16: and cyricþenunga heo fægere gedihtan and mænige oðre þing ægþer ge be godes þeowum ge be gode sylfum 
338.7.17: hy cwædon þær ealle anrædlice þæt hit riht wære gif weofodþen þæt is biscop oððe mæssepreost oððe diacon gewifode þæt he þolode æfre his hades and amansumod wurde butan he geswice and ðe deoppor gebete 
338.7.18: feower synoðas wæron gesamnode for rihtan geleafan ymbe þa halgan þrynnysse and ymbe cristes menniscnesse 
338.7.19: se forma wæs on nicea and se oðer wæs syððan on constantinopolim þær wæron cl biscopa se ðridda wæs on effesum cc biscopa and se feorða wæs on calcedonea fela biscopa ætgædere 
338.7.20: and ealle hi wæron anræde æt eallum þam ðingum þe man on fruman on nicea gesette 
338.7.21: and ealle hi forbudon æfre ælc wiflac weofodþenum 

338.7.22: ðencan ða nu þe to þam ðryste syn þæt hi god oferseoð and swa maniges haliges mannes dom swa on þissum sinoðum gesamnode wæron and gehwær syððon hwylces leanes hy hym wenan magon and eac wenan ne þurfon 
338.7.23: ac gewitod witan þæt hi yfel lean habban sculon and grimlice godes graman þurh þæt þe hy swa god gremiað þæt hi eall heora lif libbað on fylðe 
338.7.24: weofodþenas ic bidde þæt hi beðencan hi sylfe and geswican ælcere fylðe 
338.7.25: and þa þe ær þyssan þone ungewunan hæfdon þæt hi heora wif glengdan swa hi weofoda sceoldan geswican þæs ungewunon 
338.7.26: and glencgan heora cyrican be þam þe hi betst magon þonne wealdað hy heom sylfum ægðer ge godcundes rædes ge woruldcundes weorðscypes 
338.7.27: nis preostes cwene ænig oðer þing butan deofles grin 
338.7.28: and se ðe mid þam gegrinod byð forð oþ his ende 
338.7.29: he byð þurh deofol fæste gefangen and he eac syðþon mot faran on feonda hand and forfaran mid ealle 
338.7.30: ac helpe gehwa georne hys sylfes þa hwile þe he mage and mote and gebuge ælc man fram unrihte to rihte 
338.7.31: þonne gebyrhð man wið ece wite 
338.7.32: and eac se þe þurhwunað on godum dædum forð oð his ende he þæs habban sceal ece edlean 
338.7.33: nu eow is soð asæd understandaþ eow sylfe be ðam þe ge willan 
338.7.34: god eow getrymme to eowre agenre þearfe and us ealle gehealde swa his wylla sy amen
